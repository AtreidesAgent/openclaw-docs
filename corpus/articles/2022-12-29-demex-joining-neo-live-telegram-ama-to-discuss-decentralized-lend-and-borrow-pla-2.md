# Demex joining Neo Live Telegram AMA to discuss decentralized lend and borrow platform

**Published:** Thu, 29 Dec 2022 23:15:20 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/demex-joining-neo-live-telegram-ama-to-discuss-decentralized-lend-and-borrow-platform/
**Tags:** DeFi, Events, Exchanges, bNEO, SWTH

---

Neo Global Development will host CJ, Demex head of business development and strategy, on the next installment of Neo Live. Neo Live participants will be eligible to win from a pool of 20,000 SWTH tokens, which will be distributed to five users who submit questions on Twitter and five on Telegram. The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 7:00 a.m. (UTC) on Friday, Dec. 30.

Demex is a fully non-custodial exchange offering spot, margin, and derivatives trading that launched in Dec. 2020. The Demex team recently integrated bNEO lending and borrowing into Nitron, the non-custodial exchange’s multi-chain permissionless money market. Nitron launched in Nov. 2022 and allows users to lend digital assets and earn interest if the asset is borrowed. At the time of press, approximately 37,000 bNEO has been pooled, which users can borrow at a 2.65% interest rate.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
