# Neo Global Development is hosting a Space to provide updates on recent  Neo X developments

**Published:** Sat, 19 Oct 2024 09:55:17 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-global-development-is-hosting-a-space-to-provide-updates-on-recent-neo-x-developments/
**Tags:** Events, NeoX

---

Neo has announced an upcoming X/Twitter Space event entitled Neo X Small Talk: Uptober Update. The event will take place on the official Neo X/Twitter account and will dive into recent Neo X developments.

Neo Foundation community manager Adela will moderate the Space, which will feature the following panelists: AxLabs founder Guil. Sperb Machado, Neo SPCC engineer and Neo core developer Anna Shaleva, and Neo Global Development leader Steven Liu.

The planned topics of discussion for the Space include the latest Neo X updates, the speed and fees of the sidechain, recent smart contracts launched on the network, ongoing community campaigns, and the next steps on the roadmap.

The Space will begin at 9:00 a.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Oct. 22, and will be recorded.
