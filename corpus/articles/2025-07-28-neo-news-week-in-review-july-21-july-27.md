# Neo News: Week in Review – July 21 – July 27

**Published:** Mon, 28 Jul 2025 15:44:34 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/week-in-review/neo-news-week-in-review-july-21-july-27/
**Tags:** Week In Review, AxLabs, COZ, FlamingoFinance, FLM, GAS, GrantShares, Meme2Earn, NeoSPCC, NRP, NUDES, SpoonOS, WarOnBugs

---

General Updates

SpoonOS released an update on Developer Call Season 1 Week 3, including 12 proposals and patches to the existing codebase submitted for approval, showcasing novel use cases and critical core enhancements. As Season 1 of the SpoonOS Developer Call enters its final stretch, the community has seen consistent growth in pitches and proposals toward AI agent innovation across DeFi, prediction markets, and developer infrastructure. DevCall Season 1 concludes next week and the team will release the final batch of proposals and closing reflections on Friday, Aug. 1.

AxLabs shared a video overview of GrantShares entitled ‘GrantShares DAO Members Responsibilities & Expectations.’ Core responsibilities for DAO members outlined in the video include proposal endorsement and voting, governance participation, due diligence and oversight, and community engagement and growth. 

Meme2Earn concluded the NUDES trading competition on Flamingo Finance, and announced the Top 10 winners. The competition offered a prize pool equivalent of US $694.20 in NUDES tokens. 

War on Bugs released an updated changelog, which introduced an items system, added new unique items available at game launch, streamlined the onboarding flow, improved the character selection process, and changed the combat methodology.

Neo Red Pill launched a user reward campaign, incentivizing participants to create a meme and post it on social media or create a tweet about the meme token. The prize pool for the event was 5 GAS and 110,000 NRP.

Flamingo Finance noted that Epoch 16 concluded, that the total payout was $4,862.39, and 84,926 FLM were burned.

Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the FrankCoin channel of the NeoF1 Discord server and distributed 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released NeoFS Panel v.0.7.0, which included a fix to objects pagination.

Neo SPCC released NeoFS REST Gateway v0.13.0, which included updates to the SDK, and fixed other bugs.

Neo SPCC released NeoFS S3 Gateway v0.40.0, which fixed lock object incompatibility for the v0.48.0 version of the NeoFS node.

NNT Catch Up

NNT hosted Crypto Coffee and Blockchain Beer Spaces #66 on the official The Smart Economy Podcast X account. Topics of discussion included SpoonOS DevCall Season 1 project proposals for the AI agentic framework, the state of activity across various blockchain networks, how Pudgy Penguins is attracting mainstream audiences, the new GrantShares Discord server platform for engagement, and AI agent use cases.
