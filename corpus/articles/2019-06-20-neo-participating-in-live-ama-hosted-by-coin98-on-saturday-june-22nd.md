# NEO participating in live AMA hosted by Coin98 on Saturday, June 22nd

**Published:** Thu, 20 Jun 2019 20:08:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-participating-in-live-ama-hosted-by-coin98-on-saturday-june-22nd/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

On Saturday, June 22nd, NEO is participating in a live Ask Me Anything (AMA) hosted by Vietnam-based blockchain research group, Coin98. Grace Pham of the KiwiGroup, a customer relations company, is the moderator of the hour-long event.

Coin98 was founded in October 2017 and has conducted 17 live AMA’s to date. The AMA will take place on Coin98’s Facebook page, which has more than 7,500 members at the time of press.

Source: Coin98 Twitter

Three NEO representatives are scheduled to participate in the AMA:

- Yuan Gao, NEO Global Development (NGD) marketing director

- Longfei Wang, NEO blockchain engineer

- John Wang, NEO director of Ecosystem Growth

The AMA is scheduled to begin at 8:00 pm (UTC +7).

The link to the upcoming live AMA can be found below:

https://www.facebook.com/groups/Coin98.Net/
