# Travala AVA token listed on Switcheo exchange

**Published:** Thu, 24 May 2018 15:06:31 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/ava-listed-on-switcheo/
**Tags:** Exchanges, AVA

---

On May 22, 2018 Travala’s token (AVA) was listed on the Switcheo decentralized exchange. The listing followed a successful airdrop of AVA tokens to users who held 2,000 or more Switcheo tokens (SWTH). The airdrop distributed 250,000 AVA tokens across 15,852 wallets.

Switcheo is Travala’s first exchange listing, and fulfils a promise from its whitepaper to list the token for trading within one month of the close the public token sale. 

Travala currently has an alpha version of its decentralized global travel booking platform live, and plans to launch version 1.0 sometime in Quarter 2 or 3 of 2018. 

The AVA token was also added to Blockfolio and Delta cryptocurrency portfolio applications the same day the token listed on Switcheo.
