# Neo returns to Hong Kong Web3 Festival, as Secondary Exhibition Sponsor for the 2025 event

**Published:** Thu, 27 Mar 2025 16:30:28 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-returns-to-hong-kong-web3-festival-as-secondary-exhibition-sponsor-for-the-2025-event/
**Tags:** Events, NeoX, Web3 Festival

---

Neo will serve as a secondary exhibition sponsor for the upcoming Hong Kong Web3 Festival, which will take place April 6 – 9 at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre. This marks the festival’s third consecutive year of operations, with Neo maintaining a presence at each.

Organized by Wanxiang Blockchain and HashKey Group, the 2025 Web3 Festival is expected to draw more than 50,000 visitors, feature 400 speakers, and include 200 side events. Scheduled speakers include Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin, representatives from Hong Kong’s legislative and regulatory bodies, and a wide array of participants from blockchain and Web3 projects.

At the event, the Neo team aims to speak with conference attendees about DeFi and AI in the blockchain space. Founder Da Hongfei said, “Thrilled to introduce Neo X and our MEV-free feature at the Hong Kong Web3 Festival 2025.”

Neo’s booth will be located at HKCEC Hall 5BCDE, booth G05.

Ticket options for the event include:

- Four-day general pass: US $599

- Four-day pass with OKX Web3 NFT ticket: $510

- Two-day pass: $299

- One-day pass: $199

- Student pass (at the door): HKD $100/day

The Hong Kong Web3 Festival serves as an event for connecting regional and international blockchain stakeholders, offering an opportunity for ecosystem participants to network, share insights, and showcase innovation.
