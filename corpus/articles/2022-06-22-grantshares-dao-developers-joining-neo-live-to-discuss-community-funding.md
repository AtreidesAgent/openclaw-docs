# GrantShares DAO developers joining Neo Live to discuss community funding

**Published:** Wed, 22 Jun 2022 18:07:59 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/dao/grantshares-dao-developers-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-community-funding/
**Tags:** DAO, General, GrantShares

---

Neo Global Development will host the developers of GrantShares on the next installment of Neo Live. Guil. Sperb Machado and Claude Muller, are joining the AMA to discuss the grant funding DAO and the application process. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn from a pool of 10 Neoverse blindbox NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, June 23, 2022.

GrantShares is the first DAO to launch on Neo N3 and seeks to distribute funding to grassroots developers and projects that will benefit the ecosystem. The initial funding limit for GrantShares is US $50,000 per proposal and can be used to fund local events, dApps, developer tools, education initiatives, and more. Voting members of the organization include COZ, AxLabs, Red4Sec, NGD Enterprise, NeoResearch, NEXT, Neo SPCC, Neo Foundation, NGD, and NNT.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
