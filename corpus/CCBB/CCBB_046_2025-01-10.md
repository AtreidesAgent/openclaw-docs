### Summary
Episode 46 features UTC (Unlocking the Chain), a longtime Neo content creator, discussing the Virtuals Protocol AI agent platform on Base. The conversation covers how Virtuals evolved from a basic AI concept to a tokenized agent launchpad, Edge's experience launching his own agent and the composability of the sandbox, the distinction between useful agents and pure meme tokens, Neo's Escape the Matrix adversarial AI game, and whether the AI agent narrative has peaked. This was the most-listened CC&BB to date with 469 total listeners.

### Topics Covered
- UTC's background: Neo content creator who got into Virtuals Protocol on Base in February 2024
- Virtuals Protocol evolution: from basic AI concept to tokenized agent launchpad (October 2024)

- Edge launching his first AI agent — got 110 holders within an hour without any promotion
- The sandbox: heartbeat settings, world information, goals, custom prompts, simulation testing
