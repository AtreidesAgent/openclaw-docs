# Neo and Ellipal launch giveaway campaign, Neo X-branded cold wallet reward

**Published:** Tue, 15 Apr 2025 22:14:39 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-and-ellipal-launch-giveaway-campaign-neo-x-branded-cold-wallet-reward/
**Tags:** General, ELLIPAL, NEO

---

Neo has partnered with hardware wallet provider Ellipal in a social media marketing campaign that offers participants the chance to win a limited-edition Neo X-branded air-gapped cold wallet. The campaign is currently live and will run through Wednesday, April 17.

To enter into the running, users must:

- Follow Neo and Ellipal on X/Twitter

- Like and retweet the announcement post

- Leave a comment in the announcement post

Participants must enter the campaign before the deadline to be eligible for the giveaway.

Source: Ellipal

Ellipal is known for its air-gapped hardware wallets, which are entirely isolated from the Internet and any wireless communications, offering enhanced security for digital assets. The company also provides a mobile application that supports token transfers and swaps whilst leveraging the air-gapped environment.

This promotion follows the initial announcement in Nov. 2024 of Neo’s integration into Ellipal’s cold storage ecosystem, enabling support for the Neo X blockchain on Ellipal devices.
