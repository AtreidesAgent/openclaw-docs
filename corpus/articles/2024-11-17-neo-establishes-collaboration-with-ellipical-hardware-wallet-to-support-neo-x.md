# Neo establishes collaboration with Ellipical hardware wallet, to support Neo X

**Published:** Sun, 17 Nov 2024 17:11:59 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-establishes-collaboration-with-ellipical-hardware-wallet-to-support-neo-x/
**Tags:** General, NeoX

---

Neo has announced a new collaboration with Ellipal, a hardware wallet provider that has been building tools since 2018. The coalition will see the integration of Neo X into Ellipal’s cold storage device.

Ellipal offers “air-gapped” hardware wallets, which are completely disconnected from the Internet and any form of wireless communication. The company has delivered three versions of its wallet solutions: the Titan, Titan 2.0, and Titan Mini.

Alongside the hardware devices, Ellipal offers a mobile app that allows users to swap or transfer tokens. The wallet and mobile app support more than 10,000 tokens across multiple blockchain networks, including Solana, Ethereum, Bitcoin, TRON, and Polkadot.

To date, Ellipal claims to have serviced more than 1 million users.

Zealy Campaign

To celebrate the collaboration, Neo is running a Zealy community campaign and is offering two limited-edition Neo X branded hardware wallets.

Interested individuals must follow the Neo and Ellipal X/Twitter accounts, successfully invite at least one friend to join the campaign, re-tweet the collaboration announcement, and create a unique tweet with the following words included: @Neo_Blockchain, @ellipalwallet, and #NeoXEllipalChallenge.

The Zealy campaign concludes on Nov. 21.
