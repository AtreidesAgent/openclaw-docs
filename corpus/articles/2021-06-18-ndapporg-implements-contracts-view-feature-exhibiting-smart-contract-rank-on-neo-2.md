# nDapp.org implements Contracts View feature exhibiting smart contract rank on Neo blockchain

**Published:** Fri, 18 Jun 2021 21:24:18 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/ndapp-org-implements-contracts-view-feature-exhibiting-smart-contract-rank-on-neo-blockchain/
**Tags:** General, NNT, NEO

---

Neo News Today has added a new smart contract rank feature to the nDapp Neo application discovery platform. The new feature addition is the first significant update since the platform’s 2021 relaunch, which included an updated design and new methods for exploring network activity.

The Contracts View page has been added to the dApps exploration section. It displays smart contract rank on Neo Legacy according to transaction activity over the past seven days. The page denotes dApps by corresponding contract name, hash, and displays a graph of activity over the past 14 days.

N3 support has also been completed and is ready to go live upon launch of the N3 MainNet.

nDapp has been a source for dApp information in the Neo ecosystem since early 2018. The Neo team initially managed the application discovery platform before handing ownership to NEXT in early 2020. Neo News Today assumed responsibility for nDapp.org in Dec. 2020 and relaunched the website in March 2021.
