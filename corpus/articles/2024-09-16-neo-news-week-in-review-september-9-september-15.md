# Neo News: Week in Review – September 9 – September 15

**Published:** Mon, 16 Sep 2024 15:12:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/week-in-review/neo-news-week-in-review-september-9-september-15/

General Updates

Neo will join TOKEN2049 in Singapore as a Platinum sponsor, hosting a booth where the second BlockSpirits activation will take place. BlockSpirits is a non-fungible item-enabled game from COZ that allows booth visitors to unlock an elemental spirit character and then battle against other players by tap-scanning a physical card. The conference runs Sep. 18 and 19 at the Marina Bay Sands resort in Singapore.

Poly Network announced the complete termination of its services. Poly Bridge cross-chain and other services will be shut down effective Sep. 30, 2024. The decision to fully shut down was made citing changing market conditions. The team will remain available to help projects transition away from the platform and answer any questions or concerns.

Neo announced its partnership with GoPlus Security, a Web3 security platform designed to protect users against various risks associated with blockchain usage. Neo X is the first network to integrate the GoPlus User Security Module at the node level, providing automatic transaction screening to protect users.

DeXFun announced plans to launch its gamified decentralized perpetual futures non-custodial exchange on the Neo X EVM sidechain. DeXFun’s development team plans to gamify the perps trading experience by offering mini-games and a leveling system to provide an interactive journey.

Encode Club launched its satellite event for phase one of the Neo X Grind Hackathon. The Grind Hackathon is a two-phase event aimed at bringing developers onto Neo’s new EVM-based network. In phase one, third-party event organizers will manage satellite hackathons, each offering its own prizes and support for participants. The second phase will be a global hackathon, open to both new entries and participants from the satellite events, with a focus on onboarding applications into the Elevate program. Developers have until Oct. 6, 2024, to submit Neo X-based projects for a chance to win prizes.

AxLabs founder Guil Sperb. Machado kicked off the Neo X Grind hackathon in an online presentation with an introduction into the Neo ecosystem, an overview of Neo X, the hackathon bounties and prize structure, common questions and resources, and key dates for workshops.

Carrot Swap and Asteroneo launched token generators on Neo X. Carrot Swap’s Launchpad allows users to create a new token on Neo X or Base (and plans for BNB Chain) with a few clicks, minimal coding knowledge, and a small amount of liquidity. Once created, the token becomes instantly tradeable on the Carrot Swap non-custodial exchange. AsteroLab is a token generator that serves as a memecoin token generator, but the team also envisions that the module can be used for projects intending to build a product around their token. Once a token is created on AsteroLab, users can create a liquidity pool on Asteroneo.

Asteroneo dropped the first 500 in its NeoNauts NFT collection, which sold out in 2 hours. The NFT launch resulted in the highest volume of daily transactions on the Neo X EVM sidechain since the release of its MainNet.

NeoLine’s development team added support for Carrot Swap’s non-custodial exchange and token generator on Neo X.

Flamingo Finance published the following contracts to the Neo N3 MainNet: Flamingo Swap-Pair, Migration Router, Proxy, pWINGv2, fCAKEv2, pONTv2, fWBTCv2, fWETHv2, fUSDTv2, and fBNBv2. Flamingo also released a brief article highlighting topics of discussion from the August Community Lagoon, which noted the August 2024 Poly Network exploit, the Asset Support Initiative, development progress on OrderBook+ and FLOCKS, platform improvements, and more.

NeoDashboard released a video overview showcasing how users can farm Intersect Points on the Intersect Finance platform and a video on staking GAS on the Neo X EVM sidechain.

Frank Coin hosted a community quiz on the team’s official Telegram channel, where the top three participants each won 80 FLM, and ten random winners each won 40 FRANK tokens.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released NeoFS S3 Gateway v0.31.1, which directly signs multipart objects, includes increased default stream timeout, and fixes for lock object deletion and memory issues.

Neo SPCC released NeoFS Panel v0.3.5, which added an Open Graph image tag, NeoLine auto-detection, links to transactions, and other updates.

Neo SPCC released NeoFS OAuthZ v0.5.2, which included Prometheus metrics and now hides log timestamps if not running in TTY.

NNT Catch Up

NNT released Episode 72 of The Smart Economy Podcast with Ken Timsit, head of Cronos Labs, a Web3 startup accelerator focusing on decentralized finance and blockchain games, and on developing the Cronos chain ecosystem. Topics of discussion included crypto as a viable solution to modern banking, relationships between centralized exchanges and their blockchain ecosystem counterparts, how politics influences the regulatory crypto environment, the Cronos tri-blockchain ecosystem, lessons learned from building on the zkEVM and Cosmos tech stacks, the forthcoming Cronos triple yield-bearing thesis for the zkCRO token, and more.

NNT editors co-hosted Crypto Coffee and Blockchain Beer Spaces #34 on the official Smart Economy Podcast X account. Topics of discussion included Poly Network’s announcement that the bridging service is ceasing its services, a review of the three Poly Network hacks, silver linings and positive outlook for the Neo ecosystem, the best blockchain bridging standards and practices, and the Block Spirits activation at the upcoming Token 2049 booth.
