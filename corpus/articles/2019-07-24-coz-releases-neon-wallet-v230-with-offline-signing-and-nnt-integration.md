# COZ releases Neon Wallet v2.3.0 with offline signing and NNT integration

**Published:** Wed, 24 Jul 2019 22:15:51 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/coz-releases-neon-wallet-v2-3-0-with-offline-signing-and-nnt-integration/
**Tags:** Development, General, COZ, NEO

---

COZ, an international NEO community developer group, has released version 2.3.0 of the Neon Wallet for the NEO blockchain. The Neon Wallet is an open-source desktop NEO wallet available for the Windows, Mac, and Linux operating systems.

Neon Wallet v2.3.0 went live on Tuesday, July 23rd, 2019.

Updates:

- Addition of NEO News Today RSS feed.

- Addition of support for offline signing and offline functionality.

- Validation for fee amounts required on transactions based on SimplePolicyPlugin.

- New ‘import wallet’ options available without authentication.

- Addition of QR codes for encrypted key generation.

- Addition of the ability to scan encrypted key for login.

- Addition of watch only authentication mechanism.

Fixes:

- Dependency updates.

- Security enhancements.

Neon Wallet version 2.3.0 can be downloaded at the following link:

https://github.com/CityOfZion/neon-wallet/releases/tag/2.3.0
