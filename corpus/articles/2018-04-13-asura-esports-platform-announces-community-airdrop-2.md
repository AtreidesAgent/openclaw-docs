# Asura eSports platform announces community airdrop

**Published:** Fri, 13 Apr 2018 15:32:06 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/asura-coin-announces-community-airdrop/
**Tags:** General, ASA

---

On April 10, 2018, Asura Coin announced a community airdrop program, which users can register for prior to an upcoming public token sale, set to take place in May of 2018. 

About Asura Coin

Asura Coin is an eSports platform to be built on the NEO blockchain that allows gamers the opportunity to “bet, compete, learn, discuss, and earn.” The platform will allow for live streaming, community betting, and commissions to further fund future events.

Users will be able to choose which tournaments are hosted on the platform through monthly voting, and will also have the ability to place bets through a game-oriented immersive betting system. To maintain Asura’s ability to host monthly tournaments, a percentage of winnings will be deposited into a monthly prize pool. 

Asura will utilize smart contracts to enable ‘community-voted unique betting rules,’ which will be created by users. Users will be able to suggest betting markets based on any functionality supported by a game’s API and submit them for voting. The most highly rated markets will be audited to automatically provide odds, using the smart contract to transparently provide the information.

Asura also aims to provide professional in-depth game guides to coach players through the intricate details of high level gameplay. This will include teaching professional decision-making processes, predictive gameplay, gameplay psychology, winning strategies, situational item-builds, situational skill-builds, situational decision making, and many more.

The Asura platform will primarily focus on four games in the initial phases of development, which include Counter Strike: Global Operations, Dota 2, King of Glory, and League of Legends. Further games will be evaluated to be hosted on the platform in the future.

Asura Coin chose to go with the NEO blockchain because of NEO’s quick transaction times, and scalability opportunities.

The Asura Coin white paper can be read here.

Asura Coin Token Sale

ASA is the currency of the Asura platform and will be of the NEP-5 token standard. It will be used for betting, tournament fees, rewarding content and paying for access to guides and professional content.

A total of 1 billion ASA coins will be created. ASA will be distributed in the following manner:

- 30% will be available for purchase during the presale, 

- 35% will be available for purchase during the crowdsale,

- 25% for the referral program and to help incentive companies to grow the Asura World network, and

- 10% for the core team and advisors.

Funds raised during the token sale will be used as follows:

- 60% for development needs

- 25% for marketing needs

- 10% for legal needs

- 5% for security needs

Asura Coin Community Airdrop

Asura Coin will airdrop 250 ASA to participants who register, and will be awarded on a first come, first serve basis. To register for the community airdrop users must:

- Complete the airdrop registration and subscribe to the Asura Coin mailing list, and 

- Join the Asura Coin Telegram chat.

Participants who make fake accounts, create multiple accounts, or are found manipulating the airdrop, will be disqualified to receive ASA coins from the airdrop campaign.

The community airdrop tokens will be delivered no more than 10 days after the public crowd sale, which is currently set to take place on May 21, 2018. 

Asura Coin whitelist registrations will open on Sunday April 22nd at 9AM Central European Time, and participants will be able to sign up for the Whitelist by visiting the Asura Coin website.

For more information about Asura Coin, visit their website, Telegram, Twitter, Facebook, Medium, Github, or Reddit.
