# DeFiLlama adds Neo X to the TVL aggregator, lists Asteroneo and Carrot Swap

**Published:** Thu, 29 Aug 2024 20:36:33 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/defi/defillama-adds-neo-x-to-the-tvl-aggregator-lists-asteroneo-and-carrot-swap/
**Tags:** DeFi, Asteroneo, CarrotSwap, DefiLlama, NeoX

---

Multichain TVL aggregator and data provider DeFiLlama has added support for the Neo X EVM sidechain. The integration offers users greater transparency and accessibility within the rapidly expanding DeFi landscape on Neo X.

Source: DeFiLlama

DeFiLlama seeks to serve as a single-stop solution for real-time statistics on various DeFi protocols across multiple blockchain networks. The data platform offers a centralized location for users to monitor various DeFi activities, such as non-custodial exchanges, lending platforms, yield farming opportunities, and other Web3-enabled financial applications. The platform tracks over 300 blockchain networks, including Neo N3, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, and Solana.

Within hours of integrating Neo X into DeFiLlama, Asteroneo was added to the platform, followed by Carrot Swap. Users can now view TVL data for each DEX, as well as US dollar and token inflows to the exchanges, and Tweets/X posts about the products.

About Asteroneo

Asteroneo is a non-custodial exchange built on the Neo X EVM sidechain. The DEX is designed to offer users the ability to provide liquidity to pools, swap tokens between pools, and take advantage of high-yield farming opportunities. Its codebase is modeled off Uniswap V2’s code, deploying the long-standing exchange’s automated market maker. AMMs allow users to trade tokens permissionlessly and automatically through liquidity pools. Other products in the Asteroneo pipeline include a project launchpad, a farming module, and an NFT series.

About Carrot Swap

Carrot Swap is an AMM-based non-custodial exchange built on Neo X. The development team created its Discord server in Q3 2023 and won an AWS award in the Neo APAC Hackathon. In August 2024, Carrot Swap launched on the Neo X MainNet and currently offers trading between the following tokens: GAS, xMATIC, xBNB, stGAS, DX, NDMEME, and NOT.
