# Cobinhood listing NEO and GAS on April 6th

**Published:** Thu, 29 Mar 2018 14:11:46 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/cobinhood-lists-neo-gas/
**Tags:** Exchanges, GAS, NEO

---

On March 28, 2018 Cobinhood announced NEO and GAS pairings on the zero-fee exchange platform. Cobinhood hosts a large variety of cryptocurrencies and offers iOS and Android apps that feature full exchange functionality.

In addition to listing coins and tokens for trading, Cobinhood also underwrites for companies seeking to launch a token sale. The company specializes in fundraising, lists the underwritten tokens on the exchange, provides legal assistance, and delivers advertisement support.

Beginning April 6, 2018 at 12:00 am (UTC +0), NEO and GAS will individually be paired with BTC, ETH, and USDT. Withdraw will be enabled on April 13 12:00 am (UTC +0). 

For more information about Cobinhood, visit their website, Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, Medium, or Telegram.
