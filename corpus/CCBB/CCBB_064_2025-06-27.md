## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 64 covers the Neo X TestNet v0.4 update introducing ZK-based DKG verification for MEV resistance, the urgent Neo Legacy to N3 migration deadline (October 31), the launch of Neo Dashboard's game "War on Bugs" on Neo X testnet, and a GrantShares proposal from the NDMEME team to enable meme coin staking. Brief updates from AxLabs founder Guil and COZ CEO Tyler Adams round out the episode.

### Topics Covered
- Neo X TestNet v0.4 update and ZK-based DKG verification for anti-MEV support
- Neo Legacy deprecation timeline (testnet halted June 1, mainnet halting October 31)
- Flamingo Finance trading competition for the Nudes token
- War on Bugs game launch on Neo X testnet (single-player strategy game with maze exploration and card combat)
- NDMEME meme coin staking GrantShares proposal (allowing meme coin holders to earn gas)
- Spoon OS t-shirt giveaway and Blockchain Rio preparations
- AxLabs anti-MEV SDK progress update
- Neon wallet EVM compatibility for Neo X

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- **On MEV resistance**: Described NeoX's anti-MEV feature as the chain's "main value prop" and explained sandwich attacks as a key problem being solved. Views this as a major differentiator.
- **On Neo Legacy deprecation**: Strongly advocates community responsibility to spread migration awareness: "it really is our job as much as it is Neo's." Predicts frustrated Reddit posts from people who lost assets starting November/December.
- **On meme coins and gaming**: Expressed genuine excitement about War on Bugs as "a reason for people to come check out what's going on here every day." Drew parallels to Farcaster mini-games he plays daily. Views casino games and gambling as legitimate onboarding use cases despite industry stigma.
- **On taking breaks**: Shared that he "unplugged completely, went on vacation and touched grass" for two weeks — advocates strongly for stepping away from the 24/7 industry.
- **On market caution**: Warned listeners against over-leveraging during euphoric green markets: "Don't go mortgaging your house to buy super hot, highly volatile assets."

- **Rob (Snakey Rob / Neo Dashboard)**: Explained the NDMEME staking proposal — allowing meme coin holders to stake and earn gas rewards, addressing the lack of traction for meme coins on Neo.
