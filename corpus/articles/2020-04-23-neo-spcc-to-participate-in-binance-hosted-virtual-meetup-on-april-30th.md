# Neo SPCC to participate in Binance-hosted virtual meetup on April 30th

**Published:** Thu, 23 Apr 2020 18:14:11 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-spcc-to-participate-in-binance-hosted-virtual-meetup-on-april-30th/
**Tags:** Events, NEO SPCC

---

Neo St. Petersburg Competence Center (Neo SPCC) is participating in the upcoming Binance Online Super Meetup scheduled to take place on Thursday, April 30th for a Russian speaking audience. Neo SPCC co-founder and CIO, Stanislav Bogatyrev, will deliver a keynote speech and participate in a panel alongside other blockchain projects such as Cardano, Waves, and Solana.

The event will begin with a speech and ask me anything (AMA) session with the founder and CEO of Binance, Changpeng Zhao (also known as CZ). 

Following CZ’s presentation, Bogatyrev will deliver a keynote speech about the development of the Neo blockchain and the ecosystem’s distributed, decentralized object storage system, NeoFS.

Afterward, the representatives of the projects mentioned above will participate in a panel discussion, which roughly translates as the “crypto-economy during COVID-19 and how projects cope within their organizations.”

There will also be a “speed-networking session” that aims to allow attendees and project representatives to communicate with one another and network. 

At the end of the event, participants will be eligible to receive rewards from Neo and Cardano.

The presentations will be shared via YouTube. However, to participate in the AMA, networking, and receive prizes, users are required to be present at the virtual event, which has limited space.
