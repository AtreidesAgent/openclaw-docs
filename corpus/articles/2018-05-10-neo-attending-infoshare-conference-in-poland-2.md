# NEO attending infoShare conference in Poland

**Published:** Thu, 10 May 2018 18:38:25 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-attending-infoshare-conference-in-poland/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO will be presenting at the upcoming infoShare conference in Gdańsk, Poland on May 22 and 23. infoShare is the biggest technology conference in Central and Eastern Europe. Registration for the conference can be found here.

The conference, which was first held in 2014, aims to create a space for developers, startups, investors, executives, innovation leaders, marketers and media to share experiences with technology and growth. The event will provide a platform for workshops, technical speeches, and opportunities to network.

Attendance for the event is expected to exceed 6,000 individuals and 500 startups, with more than 150 speakers presenting.

NEO Presentation and Workshop

Malcolm Lerider, senior research and development manager at NEO, will make a presentation at the “Tech Plus I” stage from 9:30am to 10:20am on Wednesday, May 23, 2018. Malcolm’s talk will cover decentralized applications (dApps) and building on top of public blockchain platforms. His presentation will discuss architectural differences, and how to evaluate those differences while comparing platforms.

NEO will also host a workshop that will cover smart contract development in C#, Java, Python, JavaScript, Kotlin, and other programming languages. Additionally, workshop attendees will develop a dApp using Python and deploy it onto the NEO TestNet. The workshop will take place from 9:30 am to 1:00 pm on Tuesday, May 22, 2018. Registration for the workshop can be found here.

For more information about infoShare, visit their website, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, or GooglePlus.
