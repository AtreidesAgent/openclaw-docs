# Bored Doge Club NFT mint event to begin soon, future airdrop rewards for token holders

**Published:** Thu, 19 May 2022 22:23:45 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/airdrop/bored-doge-club-nft-mint-event-to-begin-soon-future-airdrop-rewards-for-token-holders/
**Tags:** Airdrop, Gaming, NFT, DOGEF, DOGER

---

The DogeRift team is launching its Bored Doge Club NFT series with the mint beginning at 9:00 a.m. (UTC) on Friday, May 20. The limited NFT series is designed to reward early participants that will play the forthcoming play-to-earn game on Neo N3. The game will incorporate non-fungible, governance, and utility tokens built using Neo standards into its 2D and 3D universe.

It costs 500 million DOGER to mint one Bored Doge NFT. If a user doesn’t have DOGER tokens at the time of mint, a Flamingo Convert API will offer the ability for participants to convert any NEP-17 into DOGER. All DOGER spent on minting a pack will be burnt, and there’s a 3% royalty fee on future re-sales, which will also be converted to DOGER and burnt.

NFTs from the Bored Doge Club collection.

Each Bored Doge NFT will have one of three rarities: basic, rare, or legendary. The Bored Doge Club series has a total supply of 5,000 NFTs, broken down accordingly:

- Basic Bored Doge: 3,400 NFTs

- Rare Bored Doge: 1,520 NFTs

- Legendary Bored Doge: 80 NFTs

After the mint, each Bored Doge NFT holder will also receive an airdrop of the game’s rewards token, DOGEF. The size of the airdrop will vary by NFT rarity: basic (100 million DOGEF), rare (300 million DOGEF), and legendary (1 billion DOGEF).
