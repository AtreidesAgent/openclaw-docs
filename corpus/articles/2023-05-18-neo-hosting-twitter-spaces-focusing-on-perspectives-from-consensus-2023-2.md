# Neo hosting Twitter Spaces focusing on perspectives from Consensus 2023

**Published:** Thu, 18 May 2023 01:53:11 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-hosting-twitter-spaces-focusing-on-perspectives-from-consensus-2023/
**Tags:** Events, General, AxLabs, COZ, FlamingoFinance, Greenfinch, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting a Twitter Spaces dedicated to the Neo presence at Consensus 2023. The panel of guests will feature Neo booth participants to discuss their experience at North America’s premier blockchain conference. NNT will moderate the Spaces, featuring AxLabs, Greenfinch, Flamingo Finance, and COZ representatives. The discussion will begin at 5:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, May 19.

Consensus is among the longest-running blockchain conferences, dating back to 2015. This year’s three-day event in Austin, Texas, provided a forum for industry experts, investors, and developers to network, discuss industry trends, and showcase their products and services.

The theme of Neo’s booth at Consensus 2023 was Resilience, meet innovation. The booth space was staffed by eight Neo ecosystem groups that showcased a diverse range of Web3 technologies encompassing NFT, DeFi, DAO, and metaverse projects. Over 750 OneBand NFT ring phygital items and LIZARD NFTs were distributed to Neo booth visitors onboarded into the ecosystem through Neo’s activation.

An overview of the event can be found here, numerous in-person interviews and a OneBand NFT ring demo video can be found here, and a special episode of the Smart Economy Podcast dedicated to Neo’s footprint at Consensus can be found here.

The Twitter Spaces will be recorded for those unable to attend the event.
