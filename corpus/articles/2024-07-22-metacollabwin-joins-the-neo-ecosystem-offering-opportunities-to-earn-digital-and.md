# MetaCollabWin joins the Neo ecosystem, offering opportunities to earn digital and real assets in Web3 game

**Published:** Mon, 22 Jul 2024 15:12:37 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/metacollabwin-joins-the-neo-ecosystem-offering-opportunities-to-earn-digital-and-real-assets-in-web3-game/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, MCW, MetaCollabWin, NeoX

---

MetaCollabWin, a Web3 metaverse and real-world asset game, is joining the Neo X ecosystem. MetaCollabWin is a 3D world game being built by MetaSphere Technology Co. Ltd., in which players build a digital version of Bitcoin City on parcels of land and participate in side quests to earn points to upgrade digital land and characters.

The MetaCollabWin team states that game players can be rewarded with MCW platform tokens, El Salvador’s digital treasury bonds, physical land in El Salvador, or a digital identity. The game is being designed in collaboration with El Salvador and the country’s “Bitcoin City,” a planned Smart City located in La Unión, El Salvador, that will offer a tax haven to Web3 builders and be powered by geothermal energy.

MetaCollabWin’s team comprises veteran game designers and art directors with more than 20 years of experience in AAA gaming, cross-chain blockchain developers, and the Senior Vice President of Polkadot and former VP at Activision Blizzard. In addition to Neo, the project’s partners include Binance, MEXC, Pay.Cool, and others.

Galxe Quest

To celebrate the collaboration, Neo has launched a Galxe Quest in coordination with MetaCollabWin as part of the “Neo X Summer Fiesta.” The campaign offers an equivalent of US $200 in rewards—$100 in GAS provided by Neo and 10 MetaCollabWin NFTs provided by the team. Both GAS and NFTs will be distributed to winners after Neo X MainNet launches.

Ways in which users can earn points for the MetaCollabWin Galxe Quest include following Neo and the partners’ X accounts, creating and sharing posts on X, joining the Neo and partner Telegram channels, and obtaining the “Neo Knight” role in the Neo Discord server.

Galxe Quest is a platform for building and engaging with Web3 communities through a turn-key, no-code solution for connecting projects with individuals and running activities through social media platforms. Per Galxe’s Dune Dashboard, the campaign platform has attracted approximately 24.85 million unique addresses across 34 blockchains through more than 58,000 quests.
