# GhostMarket and Blockchain Cuties collaborate on Valentine’s Day giveaway campaign

**Published:** Sun, 05 Feb 2023 22:47:50 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/nft/ghostmarket-and-blockchain-cuties-collaborate-on-valentines-day-giveaway-campaign/
**Tags:** General, NFT, BlockchainCuties, GhostMarket, GM

---

GhostMarket and Blockchain Cuties are collaborating on a Valentine’s Day giveaway campaign. Five users will each receive a holiday-themed NFT minted on Neo N3 and another five will each win 2,000 GM tokens. The contest is live and concludes at 1:59 a.m. (UTC) on Feb. 14, 2023.

Source: Blockchain Cuties

To participate, users must perform tasks on the Gleam growth marketing platform to earn entry tickets. The more tickets an individual has, the more likely they will win a prize. Task examples include following official GhostMarket and Blockchain Cuties social media channels, visiting their respective websites, referring friends to the campaign, and more.

About Blockchain Cuties

Blockchain Cuties is a blockchain collectible adventure game launched in Dec. 2017. Players can collect, breed, and take pets on adventures where characters accumulate in-game items. In-game play allows users to level up characters by testing their skills in battle and gaining experience points.

Blockchain Cuties integrated Neo support in 2019 with the release of the first Neo-based Cuties NFTs designed after four members of the Neo ecosystem. In Sep. 2022, the team enabled the migration of Cuties NFTs from Neo Legacy to Neo N3.
