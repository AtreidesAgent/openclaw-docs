# Everstake’s Neo blockchain manager joining Neo Live to discuss staking services

**Published:** Wed, 17 Nov 2021 15:00:44 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/everstakes-neo-blockchain-manager-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-staking-services/
**Tags:** Events, General, Everstake, NEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host staking services provider, Everstake, on the next installment of Neo Live. Aleksandr Tetiukhin, Everstake’s Neo blockchain manager, is joining the AMA to introduce and discuss staking services. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn GAS tokens from a pool of US $200 in equivalent value.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Nov. 18, 2021.

Everstake is a staking services provider that aims to run highly secure and reliable nodes for Proof of Stake protocols using enterprise-level hardware. The company offers users the ability to delegate Everstake as a voter across various PoS networks. Everstake was the first non-Neo ecosystem entity to deploy an N3 TestNet consensus node in Jan. 2020 and currently serves as a Neo Council member.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
