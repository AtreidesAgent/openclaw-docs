# TOTHEMOON to conduct six-day auction of 120 Moon Duo NFTs

**Published:** Wed, 02 Feb 2022 23:03:56 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/tothemoon-to-conduct-six-day-auction-of-120-moon-duo-nfts/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, Metaverse, NFT, NEO, TTM

---

(Edit Feb 8: TOTHEMOON has rescheduled its auction. The changes have been updated in the article below)

TOTHEMOON is hosting an upcoming series of daily auctions for 120 unique NFTs. The auctions begin on Thursday, Feb. 10, 2022, with one set of 20 auctions per day through Saturday, Feb. 12. Each auction starts at 6:00 p.m. (UTC) and concludes after 24 hours.

Users will be bidding for Moon Duo NFTs, which comprise two character types and offer both Moon and Cryptonaut Points. In TTM Universe, points are needed to advance a character to its next level of capability, or to help a team cumulatively access “The Dark Side of the Moon.” Moon Points allow users to build on their Moon Plot more quickly, whereas Cryptonaut Points are used to gain the respect of Moon Creatures that are vital for a team to succeed.

The Moon Duo NFTs up for auction are:

- Feb. 10: 20 Moon Baby and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

- Feb. 11: 20 Moon Baby and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

- Feb. 12: 20 Moon Trainer and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

- Feb. 13: 20 Moon Trainer and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

- Feb. 14: 20 Moon Creature and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

- Feb. 15: 20 Moon Creature and Moon Creature “Moon Duo” NFTs

Bidding for the auctions will be conducted using NEO. Each bid on the Moon Duo NFTs requires a reserve price of 1 NEO plus the additional NEO the user wishes to bid. Each time a user is outbid, the NEO will be returned to the original bidder’s wallet.

Interested users can visit TOTHEMOON’s marketplace to participate in the auction:

https://tothemoonuniverse.com/
