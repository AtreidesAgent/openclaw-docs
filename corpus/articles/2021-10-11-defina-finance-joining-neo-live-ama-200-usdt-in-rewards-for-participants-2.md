# Defina Finance joining Neo Live AMA, 200 USDT in rewards for participants

**Published:** Mon, 11 Oct 2021 20:44:22 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/defina-finance-joining-neo-live-ama-200-usdt-in-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** DeFi, Events, Gaming, General, Metaverse, NFT, FINA, NEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host NFT and DeFi-based game, Defina Finance, on the next installment of Neo Live. Defina VP of Marketing, Juli Chung, will join the AMA to talk about the game’s unique monetization model and how it incorporates blockchain technologies into gameplay. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn rewards from a pool of 200 USDT.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 2:00 p.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, Oct. 13, 2021.

Defina was recently accepted into the N3 Early Adoption Program, joining numerous other projects in the Neo ecosystem. In Defina, players own their gaming assets and can monetize them via yield farming in DeFi protocols or trading NFTs. Looking forward, Defina plans to release a set of Neo-based Defina NFT characters, launch a Neo-exclusive script, and invent more gaming modes on N3.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
