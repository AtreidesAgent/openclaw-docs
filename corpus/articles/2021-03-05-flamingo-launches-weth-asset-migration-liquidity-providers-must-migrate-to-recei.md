# Flamingo launches WETH asset migration, liquidity providers must migrate to receive FLM rewards

**Published:** Fri, 05 Mar 2021 15:12:46 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/flamingo-launches-weth-asset-migration-liquidity-providers-must-migrate-to-receive-flm-rewards/
**Tags:** DeFi, General, FLM

---

The Flamingo team has launched WETH migration for users to convert from pnWETH to fWETH. Liquidity providers must migrate WETH to continue receiving FLM rewards on the Flamingo vault module. 

Rewards for pnWETH concluded at 8:00 a.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, March 3. FLM rewards began for the new FLP-fWETH-nNEO liquidity pool at 12:30 p.m. the same day.

The migration is part of an asset flow redesign announced in Feb. 2021, voted into action in FIP #2. FIP #2 outlined a mechanism to correct price disparities between synthetic assets, among other changes to make the Flamingo platform more user-friendly.

Flamingo has released a step-by-step guide to assist users through the migration process.

Looking forward, Flamingo intends to migrate WBTC and USDT during the week of March 8.
