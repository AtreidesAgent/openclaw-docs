# NEO listed in new markets on the Demex and Crypto.com exchanges

**Published:** Sat, 20 Mar 2021 03:46:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/neo-listed-in-new-markets-on-the-demex-and-crypto-com-exchanges/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General

---

NEO has been listed against new pairs on the Crypto.com and Demex trading platforms. NEO will pair against USDC on crypto.com and BUSD on Demex.

Demex listing

Switcheo recently upgraded TradeHub to v1.15 following a unanimous Yes vote from its community on TIP #10. The most notable feature of the update is the ability for SWTH to be withdrawn as a BEP-20 token to BSC via the Poly Network interoperability protocol. 

Switcheo will offer five new markets for BUSD on Demex, including the Switcheo and Neo tokens. Further, TIP #10 extended LP rewards for liquidity providers for another six months. Demex will add the following markets (and reward bonuses):

- SWTH / BUSD (2x rewards)

- nNEO / BUSD (1x rewards)

- CEL / BUSD (1x rewards)

- USDC / BUSD (1x rewards)

- wBTC / BTCB (1x rewards)

Crypto.com listing

Crypto.com is a custodial exchange that first listed NEO in July 2020 and currently offers USDT, BTC, and USDT markets for NEO. 

The exchange and mobile application offers services to more than 10 million customers. The exchange also supports more than 100 cryptocurrencies and accepts more than 20 fiat currencies for onboarding. At the time of press, Crypto.com has a 24-hour trading volume of approximately US $205.55 million.
