# Neo calls for content creators to contribute to ecosystem

**Published:** Mon, 17 Feb 2020 21:00:08 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-calls-for-content-creators-to-contribute-to-ecosystem/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development (NGD) has launched a campaign calling for content creators to join the Neo ecosystem. NGD is seeking creatives such as bloggers, vloggers, and podcasters to be featured in various online and offline Neo campaigns and events.

NGD claims that content partners can gain access to more than 500,000 people within the Neo social network. Additionally, applicants will receive professional design, translation, and marketing assistance from the NGD marketing team.

NGD intends to establish an on-going relationship with content creators on a weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis. Subject matter covered is at the discretion of the applicant, which can include topics such as blockchain adoption, DeFi, dApp development, regulations and policies, or governance and economic models.
