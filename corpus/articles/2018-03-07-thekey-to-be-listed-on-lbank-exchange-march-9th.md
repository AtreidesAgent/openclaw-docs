# THEKEY to be listed on LBank exchange March 9th

**Published:** Wed, 07 Mar 2018 03:36:11 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/tky-to-be-listed-on-lbank/
**Tags:** Exchanges, TKY

---

On March 5, 2018, LBank Exchange announced they will list THEKEY (TKY) tokens. The exchange will begin listing TKY/NEO and TKY/ETH pairs on March 9,2018 at 6:00 pm (GMT +8). TKY deposits and withdrawals will begin on March 8, 2018 at 6:00 pm (GMT +8).

LBank Exchange is a Hong Kong based exchange founded in 2016. Primary trading pairs on the exchange include Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), QTUM, BitShares (BTS), and NEO. 

Additionally, the exchange offers a mobile phone application for both Android and Apple iOS, has an active bulletin board, system notifications of wallet notifications and withdrawal/deposit suspensions, and upcoming events.

Transaction fees for both the buyer and the sellers remain at a flat rate of 0.1% per trade across all trading pairs. 

As of 5:00 am (UTC) on March 7, 2018, LBank is listed as the 16th largest 24-hour cryptocurrency trading exchange.

For more information about THEKEY, visit their website, Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, Medium, GitHub, or Telegram.
