# Neo partners with Inflectiv to support AI-driven dApp and agent activity

**Published:** Tue, 08 Apr 2025 17:09:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-partners-with-inflectiv-to-support-ai-driven-dapp-and-agent-activity/
**Tags:** AI, General, Inflectiv

---

Neo has partnered with Inflectiv, a company specializing in transforming datasets into tokenized, tradeable, and revenue-generating assets. This collaboration is aimed at leveraging AI-empowered tools to improve data management, drive ecosystem growth, provide data markets, and utilize quantum-resistant encryption.

Inflectiv’s platform offers AI-powered compression technology, achieving compression ratios of up to 200:1, real-time data access, and advanced encryption for enhanced security. These features are designed to support a scalable, trust-certified AI data marketplace, enabling seamless exchange between contributors, developers, and enterprises.

Through this partnership, Neo and Inflectiv plan to collaborate in four key areas:

- Technical integration and on-chain data access: Integrating Inflectiv’s decentralized data pipeline with Neo X to enable AI-driven applications to access, retrieve, and process structured blockchain data.

- Ecosystem growth and user adoption: Introducing Inflectiv’s dataset solutions to Neo’s dApp ecosystem to drive innovation and expand the adoption of AI-powered dApps and agents.

- Data privacy and security: Enhancing data integrity by combining blockchain encryption with Inflectiv’s quantum-resistant security framework for improved privacy, security, and compliance.

- Revenue and resource sharing: To support scalable growth by developing sustainable economic models through revenue-sharing mechanisms and pooling resources, such as computational power and AI-driven analytics.

This strategic alliance reflects Neo’s commitment to advancing AI-enabled blockchain solutions and fostering innovation within its ecosystem.
