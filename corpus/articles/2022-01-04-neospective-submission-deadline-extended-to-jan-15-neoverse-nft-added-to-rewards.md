# Neospective submission deadline extended to Jan. 15,  Neoverse NFT added to rewards

**Published:** Tue, 04 Jan 2022 18:42:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neospective-submission-deadline-extended-to-jan-15-neoverse-nft-added-to-rewards/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO, Neospective

---

Neo Global Development has extended the first round Neospective submission deadline to Saturday, Jan. 15, 2022. Additionally, each submission will receive a Neoverse blind box NFT, along with the previously announced prize package.

Neospective is a long-term content collaboration program aiming to enable another way for community members to engage with Neo and make meaningful contributions. It was designed to drive the adoption and growth of Neo by leveraging the wide range of skills and unique perspectives that community members have to offer.

Each round of Neospective will focus on a different type of content. Round one encourages participants to share their knowledge and passion for Neo N3 through a diagram shaped by their perspective.

When the first round concludes on Jan. 15, NGD will host a public poll to allocate prizes to the top submissions. Up to 24 submissions will be rewarded, with a first-place prize of US $600 awarded in $NEO. Voters will also be entered into a random drawing to receive one of 20 Neoverse NFT Blind Boxes.
