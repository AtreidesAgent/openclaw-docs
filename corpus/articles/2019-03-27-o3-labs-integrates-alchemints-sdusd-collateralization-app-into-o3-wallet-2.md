# O3 Labs integrates Alchemint’s SDUSD collateralization app into O3 wallet

**Published:** Wed, 27 Mar 2019 16:04:07 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/o3-labs-integrates-alchemints-sdusd-collateralization-app-into-o3-wallet/
**Tags:** General, O3, SDS, SDUSD

---

O3 Labs has announced the integration of the Alchemint stablecoin collateralization service into the Apps page of its O3 wallet on desktop and mobile. The Alchemint integration follows the addition of For The Win to the O3 store and NEO Economy’s integration with the O3 dAPI earlier in March.

Through the O3 wallet, users can access Alchemint’s One-Click Mint page to collateralize NEO for SDUSD directly from their wallet. The SDUSD can then be traded on exchanges or held in storage.

On the integration, O3 states they hope “the introduction of stablecoins can open up opportunities and markets in the O3 and NEO ecosystem.”

Further, Alchemint iterated “it was a great experience collaborating with the O3 team. We believe that collaboration among teams on NEO blockchain, such as ours with O3, will enrich the the smart economy.”
