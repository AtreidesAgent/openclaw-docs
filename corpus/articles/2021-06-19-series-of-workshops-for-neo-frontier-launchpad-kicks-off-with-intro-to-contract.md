# Series of workshops for Neo Frontier Launchpad kicks off with intro to contract development

**Published:** Sat, 19 Jun 2021 15:02:23 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/series-of-workshops-for-neo-frontier-launchpad-kicks-off-with-int-to-contract-development/
**Tags:** Events, General, FLM, NEO

---

Neo Frontier Launchpad workshops kicked off on June 19, 2021, with an Introduction to Contract Development on Neo and Q&A session hosted by COZ co-founder, Tyler Adams. The Zoom-based event was the first of four planned over six days to assist participants in the Development phase of the Launchpad.

In the workshop, Adams delivered a walkthrough of the contract development introduction tutorial from the COZ Dojo. Other ecosystem developers were also on hand to answer questions in the chat during the event.

Neo Global Development, Neo SPCC, and NGD Enterprise will also host workshops on DeFi, NeoFS, and NFT development, respectively. Times, topics, and hosts include:

- Monday, June 21 from 1:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m. (UTC)

DeFi project development on Neo, highlighting Flamingo Finance as an example

Li Chen, NGD digital asset team

- Tuesday, June 22, from 1:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m. (UTC)

NeoFS technical workshop

Alexey Vanin and Stanislav Bogatyrev, NeoSPCC

- Friday, June 25, from 1:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m. (UTC)

NFT development on Neo

Harry Pierson and John deVadoss, NGD Enterprise

The Neo Frontier Launchpad encourages developers to start new projects on Neo N3 and offers more than US $100,000 in prizes and incubation opportunities. There are two phases to the Neo Frontier Launchpad — planning and development. The Planning phase concluded June 7 with approximately 60 submissions. The Development phase opened on June 12 and closes on July 12, 2021.
