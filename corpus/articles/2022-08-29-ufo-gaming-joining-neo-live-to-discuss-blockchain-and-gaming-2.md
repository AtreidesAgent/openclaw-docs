# UFO Gaming joining Neo Live to discuss blockchain and gaming

**Published:** Mon, 29 Aug 2022 19:01:57 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/ufo-gaming-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-blockchain-and-gaming/
**Tags:** Events, Gaming, General, UFOGaming

---

Neo Global Development will host Ace, a community representative of UFO Gaming, on the next installment of Neo Live. Participants will be eligible to earn one of ten spots to access the alpha version of the studio’s debut game, Super Galactic.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 12:00 p.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Aug. 30.

The UFO Gaming team aims to create an interactive environment where players can find play-to-earn games, NFT marketplaces, and other blockchain-related features. UFO Gaming will offer support for transferring in-game assets (represented as NFTs) across games. The platform will also incorporate DAO governance and offer IDOs for gaming projects. The team intends to launch an upcoming game on Neo with the support of a recently awarded grant.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
