# NEO Colorado to host informal meetup in Denver, September 26th

**Published:** Tue, 17 Sep 2019 16:27:36 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-colorado-to-host-informal-meetup-in-denver-september-26th/
**Tags:** Events

---

NEO Colorado has announced its next event, NEO and Beer, which will take place on Thursday, September 26th. The event is co-hosted with Denver Blockchain, a local “group of blockchain and distributed ledger technology (DLT) entrepreneurs, developers, and evangelists.” The NEO and Beer event will be an informal “meet and greet” with the local Colorado Blockchain community and NEO ecosystem member, Dylan Grabowski.

NEO and Beer is part of NEO Colorado efforts to offer a variety events, which range between informal gatherings, developer workshops, panels, and classroom-style meetups. The event aims to offer an opportunity for the NEO community, local blockchain developers, and general interest parties to interact with individuals who work within the NEO ecosystem.

At the upcoming community hosted event, Grabowski aims to steer conversations around the NEO Community Assembly recently hosted in Shanghai, China. NEO and Beer attendees will have the opportunity to learn about the issues and solutions for NEO and NEO3 that developers, researchers, and other NEO community members discussed at the Assembly.

Event Details

NEO and Beer will take place at River North Brewery in Denver, Colorado.

The event has no cost to attend.
