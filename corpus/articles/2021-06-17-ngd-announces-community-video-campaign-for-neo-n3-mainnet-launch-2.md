# NGD announces community video campaign for Neo N3 MainNet launch

**Published:** Thu, 17 Jun 2021 21:21:27 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/ngd-announces-community-video-campaign-for-neo-n3-mainnet-launch/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development has announced a global community video competition to raise awareness of the forthcoming Neo N3 MainNet launch. NGD will select 11 winning entries and compile them into a single video. Prizes will be distributed accordingly:

- 1st place: $800 in NEO

- 2nd to 4th place: $500 in NEO each

- 5th to 11th place: $100 in NEO each

Participants must register via a Google Form to participate in the #NeoIsHere community video campaign. Submissions have to be 30 seconds or less and filmed in 16:9 aspect ratio. Participants will need to share the video in a Tweet, including a #NeoIsHere hashtag and tag @Neo_Blockchain. If the video is created in a language other than English, it must have English subtitles.

Interested individuals have until Wednesday, June 30, 2021, to submit their final video. NGD will review all submissions July 1 through July 9 and release the winning videos when Neo N3 MainNet launches.
