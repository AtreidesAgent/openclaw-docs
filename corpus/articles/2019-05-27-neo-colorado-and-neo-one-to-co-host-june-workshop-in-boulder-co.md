# NEO Colorado and NEO-ONE to co-host June workshop in Boulder, CO

**Published:** Mon, 27 May 2019 22:45:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-colorado-and-neo-one-to-co-host-june-workshop-in-boulder-co/
**Tags:** General, NEO-ONE, NEOColorado

---

On Tuesday, June 18th, NEO Colorado is coordinating with NEO•ONE to deliver a workshop on designing a simple token smart contract. Alex Fragapane of NEO•ONE will conduct the workshop and aims to educate attendees on how to develop a token contract using TypeScript. COZ has sponsored the workshop, which will offer incentives for participation; 1 GAS token for completing the pre-workshop assignment, and 1 GAS token for participating in the workshop.

The event will take place at the Galvanize Boulder campus in Boulder, Colorado. Galvanize offers a variety of co-working facilities across the US, as well as courses in software engineering and data science. In addition to providing space, they will also be sponsoring the food and beverages for the event.

As the workshop aims to be hands-on, participants are urged to complete a pre-workshop setup, listed under the “Confirm The Token Works” section. Upon passing the tests, participants can take a screenshot and send a copy to dylan@neonewstoday.com to claim the first of two GAS tokens for the event.

Example screenshot showing passed tests.

The workshop will begin with an overview of the basic workings of a token smart contract. Following this, participants will have the opportunity to choose from a list of various tracks for the development of the smart contract. Attendees will have the option to connect their token to a front end, add initial coin offering (ICO) functionality to the token contract, or add a different smart contract to interact with the token.

The NEO•ONE workshop is oriented towards TypeScript developers, but all levels of coding ability are welcome.

It is highly recommended that participants bring their computer to the workshop.

Time and Location

Tuesday, June 18th

6:30pm – 8:30pm

Galvanize – Boulder Campus

1023 Walnut Street

Boulder, CO 80302

There is no cost to attend the event.
