## Analysis

### Summary
Dylan chats with Odd Roland and Adrian Felberg, lead maintainers of Flamingo Finance, a DeFi platform in the Neo ecosystem. The conversation covers their web2 backgrounds, the decision to build on Neo based on long-term technology viability, the impact of the Terra/Luna collapse on their stablecoin launch timing, cross-chain plans, a novel on-chain order book that combines with the AMM, the FLUND staking token, reverse liquidity pools as a new form of IDOs, and the Nudes meme coin as an ecosystem onboarding tool.

### Topics Covered
- Odd and Adrian's background: 14 years working together building SaaS, CRM systems, crypto mining rigs, and trading bots
- Why they chose Neo: long-term technology viability, surviving bear markets, Neo N3 as foundation for "skyscrapers"
- Path from community members to lead maintainers via the MyMingo dashboard
- Terra/Luna collapse impact: shifted focus to bear market survival planning rather than panic
- Released algorithmic stablecoin on same day as Luna collapse
- On-chain order book innovation: combining limit orders with AMM to fill the spread automatically
- No bots needed: orders execute directly through smart contracts without bot networks
- NEP-17 token standard: requires locking assets (no allowance function like ERC-20), which is actually more secure
- Reverse liquidity pools: projects provide staking rewards while the market sets the price, replacing ICOs/IDOs
- FLUND token: single-sided staking backed by trading fees (not inflationary emissions)
- Tokenomics: step-down in minting rewards every six months
- Cross-chain plans: adding DOGE, SHIB, more stablecoins, but "A before B" - fix trading experience first
- Nudes meme coin: started as joke, became powerful onboarding tool bringing new users to Neo and Flamingo
- Team growth from 2 to ~8 people, mostly recruited from the community
- BlockDown 2022 conference: only ~500 attendees but high-quality conversations with core crypto people

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- Dylan sees conferences during bear markets as superior for meaningful conversations: "the concentration of individuals that were there were probably much higher because... the long-term folks."
- He shared that he went full-time at Neo News Today "at the depths of the bottom of the last bear in October 2018."
- Dylan finds it "refreshing to hear" that the Flamingo team's first reaction to Terra/Luna was planning how to build for the next two years on a tenth of their funds.
- He noted the irony that sending Nudes generates gas fees that are burned on the Neo network, creating actual network utilization.
- Dylan quipped about being in his "mid-30s and my job is to write about sending Nudes."

### Guest Perspectives
**Odd Roland & Adrian Felberg (Lead Maintainers, Flamingo Finance):** They advocate for a 10-15 year building plan rather than hype cycles. Their on-chain order book innovation eliminates the need for bot networks by combining limit orders with the AMM's mathematical formula to automatically fill the spread. They argued that single-sided staking pools are "a bad thing" because they're inflationary, which is why FLUND distributes actual trading fees instead. They described reverse pools as a democratized listing process that lets the market set prices rather than project teams. On meme coins, they view Nudes as analogous to "pennies you spend" - people are more willing to send and share them, creating viral onboarding effects.

### People & Projects Mentioned
- **Flamingo Finance** - Neo ecosystem DeFi platform (swapping, staking, IDOs)
- **Neo / Neo N3** - The blockchain foundation Flamingo is built on
- **FLUND** - Flamingo's single-sided staking token backed by trading fees
- **Nudes** - Meme coin on Neo that became a community engagement and onboarding tool
- **DogeFT** - New project in Neo ecosystem referenced
- **Pancake Swap** - Referenced for fCake integration and permissionless pool listing comparison
- **Robin Hood** - Referenced as lowering barriers to stock trading, parallel to DeFi goals
- **Terra/Luna** - Collapse coincided with Flamingo's algorithmic stablecoin launch

### Recurring Themes
- Building on conviction during bear markets with long-term (10-15 year) plans
- Making DeFi accessible to non-technical users ("building for grandmas")
- The shift from people passively saving to actively managing their own finances
- Innovation through combining existing mechanisms (AMM + order book) in novel ways
- Community-driven team building: recruiting contributors from the user community
- Meme coins as legitimate onboarding tools rather than purely speculative assets
- Security-first approach to token listings and protocol features

### Content Ideas & Future Topics
