# NGD launches NeoPod initiative, seeks passionate and knowledgeable Neo community members

**Published:** Tue, 27 Sep 2022 18:28:06 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/ngd-launches-neopod-initiative-seeks-passionate-and-knowledgeable-neo-community-members/
**Tags:** General, NeoPod

---

Neo Global Development has launched the NeoPod initiative, designed to empower dedicated and knowledgeable community members to help expand the reach of the Neo blockchain. In the announcement, NGD noted:

We want to take our developer base, our ecosystem, and our community to the next level. This is where you come in. You and the other members of our community know Neo like no one else, and you can help drive our growth in an authentic way that no one else can.

Anyone is encouraged to apply for the initiative. NGD envisions potential support in the following forms:

- Community operations/management

- Moderation of online/offline events

- Content creation

- Social media promotion

- Graphic design

- Video editing

- Technical development/support

- Or other efforts that could benefit Neo

NGD believes NeoPod can positively impact participants by connecting them with other passionate members from across the globe, deepening their knowledge of the Neo ecosystem, enabling contributors to get their voices heard, and much more.

NeoPod Application Process

NGD encourages anyone who wants to participate to apply for the NeoPod initiative.

Once an application is submitted, NGD will review the applicant’s social media and biography. Applicants may then be invited to interview with NGD. Those accepted will then be invited to the NeoPod Discord server to align with other members on how to contribute to Neo’s growth.
