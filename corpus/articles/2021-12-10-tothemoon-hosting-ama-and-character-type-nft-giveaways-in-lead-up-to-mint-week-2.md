# TOTHEMOON hosting AMA and Character type NFT giveaways in lead up to Mint Week

**Published:** Fri, 10 Dec 2021 19:51:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/tothemoon-hosting-ama-and-character-type-nft-giveaways-in-lead-up-to-mint-week/
**Tags:** Events, General, Metaverse, NFT, GAS, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON is hosting a week of giveaways leading up to the first minting of its Character type NFTs. Each day until Dec. 16, TOTHEMOON will release a new Twitter post with a teaser video and select a winning entrant from the post threads.

Each post will require participants to follow the TOTHEMOON Twitter account, like and retweet the post, and leave a comment tagging two friends with three hashtags. Participants can enter as many times as they like, with the caveat that different Twitter accounts are tagged in each new entry.

Source: TOTHEMOON

TOTHEMOON Character type NFT prizes will be distributed on the following dates, with the Character’s name in parenthesis:

- Thursday, Dec. 9th: One Cryptonaut (#.012)

- Friday, Dec. 10th: One Moon Creature (Wynter)

- Saturday, Dec. 11th: One Cryptonaut (#.016)

- Sunday, Dec. 12th: One Moon Creature (Fulton)

- Monday, Dec. 13th: One Cryptonaut (#.029)

- Tuesday, Dec. 14th: One Moon Creature (Bromley)

- Wednesday, Dec. 15th: One Moon Trainer (GIN)

The winners of each giveaway will be announced within 24 hours after each post via Twitter and the TOTHEMOON Discord server.

Next Steps

The minting of Character type NFTs begins Thursday, Dec. 16. A community poll on the TOTHEMOON Twitter account will determine when the minting window opens. At the time of press, 8:00 p.m. (UTC) leads with 35.6% of the vote, with 5:00 p.m. in second place at 33.9%. Voting ends at 10:00 p.m. (UTC) on Saturday, Dec. 11, 2021. (Participants should note the end of the voting window on the Twitter poll is incorrect.)

The Character NFTs will be available for purchase on a first-come, first-serve basis via The Market – TOTHEMOON’s in-game NFT marketplace. Character NFTs will each be minted with a fixed supply and different costs per character type. Character NFTs can only be purchased using GAS via the NeoLine or O3 Wallet.

Looking forward, an AMA will be held in the TOTHEMOON Discord Server beginning at 5:00 p.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, Dec. 15, 2021. Participants who join the AMA will be eligible to win rewards from the team.
