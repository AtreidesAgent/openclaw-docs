# Upcoming NEO Colorado workshop to cover NEO-ONE’s simple token contract development

**Published:** Fri, 05 Apr 2019 13:58:56 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/upcoming-neo-colorado-workshop-to-cover-neo-ones-simple-token-contract-development/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

NEO Colorado is hosting a workshop from 6:00pm to 8:00pm on Tuesday, April 23rd. The developer-oriented event is organized in collaboration with NEO-ONE and Galvanize. The workshop, taught by Alex Fragapane of NEO-ONE, aims to educate attendees on how to develop a simple token contract using TypeScript.

NEO-ONE is an end-to-end smart contract framework that seeks to make it simple for users to create dApps on the NEO platform. In addition to the framework, NEO-ONE includes documentation and interactive tutorials.

The event will take place at the Galvanize Platte campus in Denver, Colorado. Galvanize offers a variety of co-working facilities across the US, as well as courses in software engineering and data science. In addition to providing space, they will also be sponsoring the food and drink for the event.

The NEO-ONE workshop is oriented towards JavaScript developers, but all levels of coding ability are welcome.

Time and Location

Tuesday, April 23rd – 6:00pm to 8:00pm

1644 Platte Street

Denver, Colorado 80202

Agenda

- 6:00pm – Arrive and settle in

- 6:10pm – Intro to NEO-ONE (by Dylan Grabowski)

- 6:20pm – Demo NEO-ONE Lesson 1: How to Build a Token (by Alex Fragapane)

- 7:05pm – Q&A

- 7:30pm – Free work time – Continue building your token or try a new idea.

It is highly recommended participants bring their computer to the workshop.

There is no cost to attend the event.

Interested participants can RSVP at the following link:

https://www.meetup.com/NEO-Colorado/events/260284754/
