# NGD Seattle, COZ, and NEO-ONE to participate in REIMAGINE 2020 virtual conference

**Published:** Sat, 18 Apr 2020 15:13:15 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/ngd-seattle-coz-and-neo-one-to-participate-in-reimagine-2020-virtual-conference/
**Tags:** Events, COZ, NEO-ONE, NGD Seattle

---

The Neo ecosystem will be represented at the upcoming REIMAGINE 2020 virtual conference scheduled to take place May 4th through May 8th, 2020. Individuals from Neo Global Development (NGD) Seattle, COZ, and NEO-ONE will be sharing presentations about developing on Neo and relevant business topics.

REIMAGINE 2020 is a virtual blockchain conference that aims to connect individuals and entities within the academic, industry, and enterprise communities. The hosts of the event intend to assist in the creation and development of solutions with developers, academics, and students.

The virtual conference will offer three tracks: inspiration, creation, and implementation. Each track offers unique lectures, round tables, workshops, symposiums, and more. The online event aims to offer a 24-hour livestream, so that participants in timezones across the globe might have a keynote or presentation to watch live. There is no cost to attend REIMAGINE 2020.

Neo is among the many blockchain industry partners with representatives in attendance, ranging from private enterprise to public blockchain entities such as Hedera Hashgraph, Stellar, Ontology, plus more. Additionally, REIMAGINE 2020 has a growing list of collaborations with various universities internationally that will also participate.

Neo ecosystem representatives that have confirmed their participation include:

- NGD Seattle: John deVadoss (office lead) and Harry Pierson (chief architect)

- COZ : Tyler Adams (co-founder)

- NEO-ONE: Daniel Byrne (software engineer)

The schedule is tentatively expected to be finalized and live by April 27th, 2020.

More information about REIMAGINE 2020 can be found at the link below:

https://www.ri2020.io/
