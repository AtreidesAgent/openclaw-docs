# Travala listed on KuCoin, trading and withdrawals begin December 11th

**Published:** Tue, 11 Dec 2018 16:44:26 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/travala-listed-on-kucoin-trading-and-withdrawals-begin-december-11th/
**Tags:** General, AVA

---

On December 11th, KuCoin listed Travala’s AVA token with ETH and BTC trading pairs. Deposits were available immediately following KuCoin’s announcement. Buying orders began at 6:30 pm (UTC +8), selling orders at 7:00 pm (UTC +8), and withdrawals at 9:00 pm (UTC +8).

Travala is an online travel booking platform built on top of the NEO platform, that aims to “become the first decentralized global travel marketplace that will be a one-stop shop for all travel related services.“

The full announcement can be read at the following link:

https://news.kucoin.com/en/travala-com-ava-gets-listed-on-kucoin/
