# Switcheo lists FLM shortly after becoming claimable from Vault V2 module

**Published:** Mon, 28 Sep 2020 16:34:57 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/switcheo-lists-flm-shortly-after-becoming-claimable-from-vault-v2-module/
**Tags:** General, FLM, NEO

---

Switcheo has become the first non-custodial exchange to list the Flamingo Finance token (FLM), which occurred when the FLM token became claimable from the Vault V2 module. Switcheo listed the FLM token at 5:00 am (UTC) on Monday, September 28th, 2020, and is currently trading against NEO.

Flamingo Finance platform users were able to claim FLM from the platform beginning at 5:00 am (UTC) on September 28th, and the token subsequently became tradable on Switcheo and Binance.

At the time of press, the FLM/NEO pair has witnessed approximately US $21,400 in 24-hour trading volume on the Switcheo platform, and US $54.33 million in 24-hour volume on the Binance custodial exchange.
