# Alchemint announce early bird whitelisting

**Published:** Sat, 02 Jun 2018 20:49:49 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/alchemint-early-bird-whitelisting/
**Tags:** General, SDS

---

Alchemint has announced early bird whitelisting for its upcoming token sale. There will be two one-hour windows for users to submit their Know Your Customer (KYC)/Anti-Money Laundering (AML) information, scheduled to take place on:

- Sunday, June 3, from 23:00 – 23:59 (UTC +8), and

- Monday, June 4, from 11:00 – 11:59 (UCT +8)

Whitelisting information will be capped at 3,000 participants. Once 1,500 submissions have been collected in each window, the Alchemint team will stop accepting applications.

KYC/AML whitelist forms will only be released to participants via the Alchemint Telegram channel listed below: 

https://t.me/Alchemint

After the whitelist submissions have been reviewed and approved, successful applications can participate in the early bird token sale beginning at 2:00 (UTC+10 on Sunday, June 10, and ending at 13:59 (UTC+10) on Tuesday, June 12.

The early bird token sale will only accept Ethereum (ETH), which will provide participants with a 5% bonus. (Note: Alchemint’s public token sale will accept both ETH and NEO.)

Nationality limitations for the early bird sale are the same as those for the public sale; individuals from China, Japan, USA, Singapore, and Australia will not be allowed to participate in the token sale.
