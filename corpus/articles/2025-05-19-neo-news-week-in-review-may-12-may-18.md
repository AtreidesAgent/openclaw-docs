# Neo News: Week in Review – May 12 – May 18

**Published:** Mon, 19 May 2025 22:11:42 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/week-in-review/neo-news-week-in-review-may-12-may-18/
**Tags:** Week In Review, APE, AxLabs, Cassette, FlamingoFinance, FLM, FRANK, FUSD, GAS, ITEM Systems, Meme2Earn, NEO, NeoSPCC, NUDES, SpoonOS, UnlockingTheChain, WorldOfElements

---

General Updates

Neo participated as a block sponsor and operated a booth activation at Consensus Toronto. The activation introduced an interactive experience powered by ITEM Systems, where visitors engaged with NFI-powered waypoints by visiting designated locations throughout the booth. Each waypoint highlighted a Neo-related project: Neo core, ITEM Systems, Flamingo Finance, SpoonOS, and Cassette. After collecting five taps, users could redeem for merchandise from the Neo booth, including a hat or t-shirt.

Neo distributed GAS accumulated in the Neo Bond multi-sig wallet during its 30-day lockup period. 4,900 GAS was distributed to all Neo Bond participants, proportionally based on each participant’s share of the 5 million NEO pool.

ThirdWeb added support for Neo X to its full-stack Web3 development platform. The platform assists developers in building dApps, games, and AI agents on Neo X with tools, wallet infrastructure, and on-chain data.

SpoonOS announced a partnership with 4EVERLAND, a Web3 infrastructure powered by blockchain technology that provides developers with convenient, efficient, and low-cost storage, network, and computing capabilities. 4EVERLAND’s Bucket storage system is now integrated into SpoonOS, purportedly making it easier for users to upload, store, and access data to power AI agents.

MEXC, a custodial exchange, announced the permanent suspension of deposits and withdrawals for the NEP-5 FLM tokens on the Neo Legacy network, which will be deprecated in 2025.

Unlocking the Chain released a video overview of the first day of Centre Point, a gathering of Neo’s international developer communities, contributors to the ecosystem, and possible new entrants. The video covered topics discussed on the first day, including presentations by Neo Core development, the Neo Foundation, AxLabs, Neo SPCC, NNT, and Lunar Strategy.

Frank Coin released updates to the World of Elements game that re-enabled users to create Neo wallets and use all of the former crypto features. Additionally, Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the FrankCoin channel of the NeoF1 Discord server and distributed 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released new updates for NeoFS and the S3 Gateway, introducing various improvements and fixes. The team also patched NeoGo to resolve a TestNet state inconsistency.

Events

May 23: NNT hosting CC & BB #61 on The Smart Economy Podcast official X account.

May 27: Meme2Earn hosting a Community Fireplace on the official Discord Server.

May 27: Flamingo Finance hosting a Community Lagoon on the official Discord Server.

Token Listings

NEO is now listed on the BaltEX.io non-custodial swapping platform.

APE/FUSD pair is now listed on Flamingo Finance.
