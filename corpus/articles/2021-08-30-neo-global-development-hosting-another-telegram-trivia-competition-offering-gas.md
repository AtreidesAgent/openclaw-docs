# Neo Global Development hosting another Telegram trivia competition offering GAS rewards

**Published:** Mon, 30 Aug 2021 20:05:40 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-global-development-hosting-another-telegram-trivia-competition-offering-gas-rewards/
**Tags:** Events, General, GAS, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting Neo Trivia, its second community-oriented competition of the sort, in as many weeks. The event will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel beginning at 10:00 a.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Aug. 31, 2021. The competition will take place over a span of ten minutes.

Participants will compete for a pool equivalent to US $200 in GAS rewards. Winners will be chosen depending on the speed and accuracy of answering questions.
