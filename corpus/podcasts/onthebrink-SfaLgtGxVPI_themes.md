# On the Brink: Final Episode - DeFi Evolution and Future Outlook

## 1. DeFi Maturation from Permissionless to Curated
- Description: DeFi's transition from fully permissionless protocols to curated, risk-managed ecosystems with institutional-grade interfaces.
- Evidence: Yearn Finance's evolution from simple vaults to sophisticated strategy curation.

- Description: Onchain transparency enabling verifiable risk management over traditional opaque hedge fund strategies.
- Evidence: Contrast with XUSD collapse highlighting opacity risks in centralized issuers.

- Description: Compliant wrappers and vaults designed to attract trillions from TradFi into DeFi yields.
