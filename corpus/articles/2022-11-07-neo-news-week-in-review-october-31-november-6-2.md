# Neo News: Week in Review – October 31 – November 6

**Published:** Mon, 07 Nov 2022 17:28:38 +0000

General Updates

Quirky Soul College launched a series mint on Oct. 31 for the first dynamic NFT-based game on the Neo N3 blockchain. Designed as a Tamagotchi-like game where characters grow over time, the objective is to acquire QSC NFT characters and level them up to win missions more efficiently and earn rewards. There is a total supply of 10,000 QSC NFTs, each of which carries a mint cost of 15 GAS.

TOTHEMOON extended the giveaway event at the onset of its First Quarter Phase Mint, which began on Nov. 1. A total of 7,800 new NFTs are up for grabs, minted randomly for 15 GAS plus blockchain fees via the TTM Website. TTM also announced that each third phase Cryptonaut will receive one fourth phase Cryptonaut via airdrop, after the fourth phase mint.

Mega Oasis’ next NFT drop will be in collaboration with world-renowned artist Yuan Jinhua. The collection will be available on Nov. 24.

NudesToken concluded the Quantum Resistant Ledger meme competition on its meme-to-earn website. Additionally, the team released platform statistics, noting an increase in total users.

GhostMarket opened a Christmas Giveaway, with five prizes of 1,500 GM and 10 prizes of 750 GM available. Participants can complete various social media tasks to collect entries. GhostMarket also launched a new monthly NFT art contest for November, with 10,000 GM up for grabs.

NNT Catch Up

Neo Soccer launched its open beta, with limited availability for new players to join a league. In Neo Soccer, players use bNEO to purchase or loan squad members, managers, and stadiums in order to compete against teams established by other users. While different game modes are available, the second 38-day season of Neo Soccer begins at 10:00 a.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, Nov. 9.

Lounge recently launched an app on Neo N3 that provides projects in the ecosystem with a place for community-driven governance. Projects and communities can create their own Spaces on the platform, where users can then open or vote on proposals. Lounge is functionally simple, providing the polling infrastructure for decentralized decision-making. The platform introduces no new token; projects may integrate their own choice of token with their Spaces.

Forthewin Network plans to launch its first profile picture NFT collection, Neo Boyz, on the N3 blockchain. The mint begins on the official Neo Boyz website at 5:00 p.m. on Friday, Nov. 11. There will be a total supply of 3,333 Neo Boyz NFTs, each of which will cost 5,000 NEP per mint. Users can participate in the Neo Boyz NFT mint with a Neon, Neo Line, OneGate, or O3 wallet.
