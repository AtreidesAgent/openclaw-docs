# Asura Coin to begin trading on Switcheo October 19th

**Published:** Wed, 17 Oct 2018 08:17:25 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/asura-coin-to-begin-trading-on-switcheo-october-19th/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, ASA, SWTH

---

Asura Coin (ASA), will be listed on Switcheo, at 12:00 (UTC +8) on Friday, October 19th. Asura Coin is an eSports platform to be built on the NEO blockchain that aims to allow gamers the opportunity to “bet, compete, learn, discuss, and earn.” The platform will allow for live streaming, community betting, and commissions to further fund future events.

The ASA token will be paired with NEO, GAS, and SWTH.

Users will be able to immediately trade ASA once it is listed.

Note: The ASA market is not available to VPN users and users in Singapore.
