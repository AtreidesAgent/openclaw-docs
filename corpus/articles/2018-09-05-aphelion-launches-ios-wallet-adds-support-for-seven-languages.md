# Aphelion launches iOS wallet, adds support for seven languages

**Published:** Wed, 05 Sep 2018 02:01:17 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/aphelion-launches-ios-wallet-adds-support-for-seven-languages/
**Tags:** General, APH

---

The Aphelion iOS wallet is now available in the Apple App Store. The wallet offers “real-time trading chart data, customizable address book and complete NEP-5 token integration.”  

Aphelion is a decentralized, peer-to-peer exchange that allows users to trade NEO, GAS, and NEP-5 token assets. Trading is currently only available on the Aphelion desktop wallet on TestNet, with mobile trading planned for the future.

To access Aphelion on the iOS app store, visit the following link:

https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/aphelion-mobile-wallet/id1418366948?mt=8 

Language support

Aphelion has also added support for seven languages to its products. The Aphelion wallet and decentralized exchange (DEX) now allows users to interface in English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Dutch, and German.

After receiving requests from its users who sought to use the platform in their native language, Aphelion coordinated with the community on a translation initiative. The translation initiative is part of Aphelion’s mission to “help bring the Smart Economy to the world.”

The desktop wallet also supports the seven languages in its commit/claim/compound functions of the wallet-based DEX. To access any of the seven languages, users can navigate to the settings page, and select their preference.
