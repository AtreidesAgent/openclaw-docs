# NekoHit crowdfunding platform co-founders joining Neo Live, Neoverse NFTs in rewards for participants

**Published:** Wed, 10 Nov 2021 14:25:06 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/nekohit-crowdfunding-platform-co-founders-joining-neo-live-neoverse-nfts-in-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, CAT, Nekoin, Neo Live, Neoverse

---

Neo Global Development will host crowdfunding platform, NekoHit, on the next installment of Neo Live. NekoHit co-founders, Yannick Koitzsch and Harry Hu, are joining the AMA to discuss the new funding platform that aims to connect patrons and content creators. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn one Neoverse NFT blind box from a pool of ten.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Nov. 11, 2021.

NekoHit was conceptualized in the Neo Frontier Launchpad, and is designed to connect content creators with individuals who can offer funding on a per-project basis instead of the subscription model used by other solutions such as Patreon. NekoHit’s Work Contract Agreement is a smart contract designed to give both creators and sponsors more control over the crowdfunding process through staking and paying via NEP-17 CAT tokens. The NekoHit team is also developing a community DAO token called Nekoin, which will be used to vote on the direction of the platform.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
