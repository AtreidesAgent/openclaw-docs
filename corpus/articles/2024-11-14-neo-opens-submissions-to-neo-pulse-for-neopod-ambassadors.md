# Neo opens submissions to Neo Pulse for NeoPod Ambassadors

**Published:** Thu, 14 Nov 2024 02:24:32 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-opens-submissions-to-neo-pulse-for-neopod-ambassadors/
**Tags:** General, GAS, NEO

---

Neo has announced a new vision for its Medium account, which will include curated content from the Neo community. The new directive, Neo Pulse, will be open to submissions from members of the NeoPod Ambassador program. Neo aims to mold the Neo Pulse page into a collective library of content that features contributions from the community on blockchain technology, token economics, and other ecosystem insights.

Contributors can receive up to US $500 per published article. The proposed content must be at least 500 words and focus on Neo N3, Neo X, or blockchain technology. Examples can include real-world use cases for Neo, general blockchain knowledge that applies to Neo, in-depth reviews of the ecosystem, and more.

To submit content, individuals must first reach the Sentinel level on the NeoPod program. Once this is achieved, the contributor can submit their draft through Neo’s Zealy account. The Neo team will review the content and provide edits if necessary. After the article is ready, the contributor must publish the article to their personal Medium account and submit it to the Neo Pulse publication for approval.

About NeoPod

NeoPod is a structured rewards program that empowers dedicated community members to help expand the reach of the Neo multi-chain ecosystem. It offers numerous tasks for individuals with varied backgrounds, skill sets, and availability. From sharing social media posts to spinning up a regional community, there are many opportunities earn GAS rewards.

There are three primary roles in the NeoPod Ambassador program: Operator, Sentinel, and Architect.

Once the Operator role has been achieved, participants can compete for monthly GAS rewards. Each month the Top 10 Operators will share from a pool of GAS valued at US $1,000.

The next rank is the Sentinel, offered to Operators who’ve achieved a Top 10 Zealy position. Sentinels can perform more advanced tasks such as technical “missions” like hosting workshops, writing tutorials, and contributing to Neo documentation. They can also conduct community missions such as helping new members onboard into Neo, proposing social events (on or offline), translating Neo content, and writing articles or X threads.

The highest NeoPod rank is the Architect, which grants the individual a monthly stipend from Neo Global Development (determined on a case-by-case basis). An Architect is a member who has demonstrated leadership, their capability to contribute to the ecosystem, and a track record of seeing ideas through from beginning to completion.
