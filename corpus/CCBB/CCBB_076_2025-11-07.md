### Summary
Solo episode 76 celebrates the Smart Economy podcast reaching 100 episodes and announces the rebranding of the NNT YouTube channel to "Smart Economy Network." Dylan shares takeaways from ETH Mexico in Monterrey — stablecoins/remittances as killer use cases, and Nuevo Leon's government using decentralized identity (Sovra). The episode also covers Neo Legacy deprecation, NeoX open-sourcing its code, Flamingo Finance's new analytics page, War on Bugs' upcoming NFT mint, and a meeting with Colorado politicians who admitted Democrats' crypto policy failures.

- Smart Economy podcast 100th episode milestone and YouTube rebranding to "Smart Economy Network"

- Meeting with Colorado Democratic candidates who acknowledged anti-crypto policy failures
- Neo Legacy officially deprecated
