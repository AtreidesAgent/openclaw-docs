# Trinity (TNC) to be listed on KuCoin with trading promotions

**Published:** Tue, 23 Jan 2018 06:13:46 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/trinity-kucoin/
**Tags:** Exchanges, TNC

---

On January 22, 2018, KuCoin exchange  announced they will begin trading Trinity (TNC) paired with BTC, NEO, and ETH. Investors will be able to begin trading the token at 10:00 pm (UTC +8). KuCoin is the first cryptocurrency exchange that will list TNC for trading.

KuCoin announced they’re offering three separate promotions to spur investors to begin trading the recently launched coin. The promotional competition period will take place beginning at 10:00 pm (UTC +8), January 22, 2018 and ending at 11:59 pm (UTC +8), January 27, 2018.

The first promotion will rank traders by the total amount of TNC traded on their account, which includes both buys and sells of the token. The total reward pot for this competition is 3 BTC plus 3,880,000 TNC, and will be split among the top 100 traders.

The second promotion will rank TNC net deposits (deposits minus withdrawals), and will reward 7 BTC to be shared among the top 100 ranked users. The top 100 winners from the first promotion will not be able to partake in the second promotion. Note: KuCoin reserves the right to interpret the final results of the competition as they deem appropriate.

The third promotion is a social media-based competition that will reward participants with 5 BTC to be shared among the winners. The first step of the third promotion is to retweet this tweet, then take a screen capture and fill-in/submit this form.

With the listing of TNC, KuCoin now trades four NEP-5 tokens through the NEO platform.
