# Neo to co-host AMA with OKEx custodial exchange, NEO trading comp open

**Published:** Mon, 22 Jun 2020 21:51:50 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-to-co-host-ama-with-okex-custodial-exchange-neo-trading-comp-open/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development (NGD) and OKEx, a centralized custodial exchange, have announced the co-hosting of an AMA on the official Neo Telegram channel. The event is scheduled to begin at 6:00 pm (UTC+8) on Tuesday, June 23rd.

In its announcement, OKEx claims there will be an airdrop for those who join and participate in the upcoming AMA.

OKEx Trading Campaign

In conjunction with the AMA, OKEx is currently conducting a trading campaign that rewards users for registration and NEO trading activity on the exchange through 8:00 pm (UTC +8) on Sunday, June 28th.

Users who complete KYC level one verification will receive 3 USDT as a reward; though, they must register through a unique campaign page. Participants that trade in a volume equivalent to, or greater than, 20 USDT in NEO will receive additional equivalent of 6 USDT in NEO.

In addition, OKEx will also distribute rewards to users from a 20,000 USDT prize pool. However, at the time of press, specific trading volume requirements are unclear, as are the portions users will receive from the campaign prize pool.

OKEx tentatively expects to distribute rewards for KYC verification within five business days and prizes for trading activity within ten business days following the event’s conclusion.
