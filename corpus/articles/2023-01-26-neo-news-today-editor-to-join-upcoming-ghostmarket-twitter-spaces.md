# Neo News Today editor to join upcoming GhostMarket Twitter Spaces

**Published:** Thu, 26 Jan 2023 18:34:27 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-news-today-editor-to-join-upcoming-ghostmarket-twitter-spaces/
**Tags:** Events, General, GhostMarket, GM

---

Neo News Today is joining the next GhostMarket Twitter Spaces to discuss the Neo ecosystem. NNT editor Dylan Grabowski will participate in the event to talk about the latest activities involving the Neo blockchain, what NNT has been working on, and other topics. The Spaces begins at 8:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Jan. 26, on the official GhostMarket Twitter account, and will be recorded.

GhostMarket is a multi-chain NFT marketplace that allows users to mint, trade, and purchase NFTs. In 2022, the team began hosting Twitter Spaces to showcase artists and projects across the blockchain ecosystems supported by the platform. Since then, the Spaces has been host to various Neo ecosystem projects, such as Quirky Soul College, COZ, community-sprung projects like FrankCoin, and many more.
