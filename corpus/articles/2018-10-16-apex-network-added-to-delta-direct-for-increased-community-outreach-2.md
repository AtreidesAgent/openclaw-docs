# APEX Network added to Delta Direct for increased community outreach

**Published:** Tue, 16 Oct 2018 16:21:55 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/apex-network-added-to-delta-direct-for-increased-community-outreach/
**Tags:** General, CPX, LRN, SWTH

---

APEX Network was recently added to Delta Direct, a function of Delta portfolio tracking application version 1.4. The APEX blockchain infrastructure aims to provide consumer internet and business-to-customer (B2C) interactions with privacy and ownership of data, interactive consumer experiences, and a connectivity of value. 

The Delta Direct function strives to “[empower] users to get better informed about their current and potential future investments.” The application seeks to act as a direct link between coin and token projects, with existing and future investors. Through the application “users will get instantly notified about new blog posts or direct messages from the [project] team.” 

In addition to APEX, other NEO ecosystem projects coordinating with Delta Direct include Switcheo and Loopring.
