# GhostMarket meme campaign live on NudesArmy platform, GM and NUDES token rewards

**Published:** Sat, 23 Jul 2022 22:03:20 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/airdrop/ghostmarket-meme-campaign-live-on-nudesarmy-platform-gm-and-nudes-token-rewards/
**Tags:** Airdrop, Meme, GM, NUDES

---

NudesToken is distributing approximately US $6,150 in GM and NUDES token rewards for its second meme campaign, focusing on GhostMarket content. The top ten qualifying submissions will share from a pool of 100,000 GM tokens in rewards, and 1 trillion NUDES will be airdropped to NudesArmy platform users. The contest is live and will run through Aug. 1, 2022.

The first NudesArmy meme competition took place in June 2022 and distributed approximately $10,000 in rewards to participants. Qualifying meme submissions included any that were related to DogeRift.

Like the first competition, there will be rewards for users who complete at least one daily task before Monday, July 25, 2022. Daily task examples include making, sharing, voting, and commenting on memes, or inviting friends. The airdrop will be split evenly amongst all qualifying wallets.

For the current GhostMarket meme campaign, users must first connect their Twitter account to the Nudes.Army website to participate. Then, they can upload meme-based images or videos up to 10 MB in size. When entering a meme for the contest, users must provide a title and select the “GhostMarket [CONTEST]” category. Once the meme has been uploaded, the entrant must then Tweet a link to their meme using the following text template:

Look at this #meme I created for the @SendnudesToken x @GhostMarket contest where there’s 100,000 GM in prizes! Check out my meme here:

LINK

#contest #giveaway #NudesArmy $NUDES $GM #GhostMarket #crypto #memes

Source: NudesArmy

The NudesToken team suggests participants use the referral link (purple link icon) from their uploaded meme on the website, as it rewards the meme creator whenever anyone clicks it.

GM rewards for the meme campaign will be distributed accordingly:

- 1st place: 25,000 GM

- 2nd place: 20,000 GM

- 3rd place: 15,000 GM

- 4th place: 10,000 GM

- 5th place: 9,000 GM

- 6th place: 8,000 GM

- 7th place: 6,000 GM

- 8th place: 4,000 GM

- 9th place: 2,000 GM

- 10th place: 1,000 GM
