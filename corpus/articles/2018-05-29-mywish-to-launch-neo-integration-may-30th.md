# MyWish to launch NEO integration May 30th

**Published:** Tue, 29 May 2018 23:23:04 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/mywish-integration-with-neo-may-30th/
**Tags:** Development, General, NEO

---

In a recent progress report, Vladimir Tikhomirov, CEO of MyWish, announced their development team has nearly finalized the smart contract platform’s integration with NEO.

MyWish is a service that provides users the ability to easily create, deploy and manage smart contracts. The application seeks to provide a user-friendly interface to “bring common money-related practices into the crypto world,” which includes wills, marriage contracts, annuities, and automatic regular payouts.

MyWish is protocol agnostic and has the ability to develop smart contracts for various blockchains. The development team has submitted their code to the NEO team for review prior to launch.

The open source code can be seen on Github:

https://github.com/MyWishPlatform/neo-python/blob/master/MyWishMethods.py

The MyWish application has been placed on the NEO decentralized application (dApp) list, and plans to provide the ability to create NEO smart contracts beginning Wednesday, May 30.
