# Mega Oasis and Zhenchen Liu joining Neo Live to discuss NFTs and art

**Published:** Wed, 16 Feb 2022 23:13:12 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/mega-oasis-and-zhenchen-liu-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-nfts-and-art/
**Tags:** Events, General, NFT, bNEO, MegaOasis, NEO

---

Mega Oasis and its first featured artist are joining the next edition of Neo Live to discuss the NFT marketplace and its inaugural collection. Neo Global Development is hosting the AMA with Mega Oasis team member Ricardo Prado and artist Zhenchen Liu to discuss the platform and the Meta Panacea collection. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn two Meta Panacea NFTs and 10 Neoverse blind box NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, Feb. 18.

Mega Oasis is a new NFT marketplace launched on Feb. 14 that aims to bring traditional artists into Neo’s NFT ecosystem. The Mega Oasis marketplace is designed to offer collectors access to unique NFTs explicitly made for the platform. It is targeted at both existing NFT collectors and art lovers who haven’t yet ventured into the blockchain world.

The platform launched with the sale of a collection by Zhenchen Liu, a renowned artist whose works have been shown in prestigious venues all over the world. The first NFT series for sale on Mega Oasis, Meta Panacea, is based on an existing collection of physical art pieces, Panacea, created by Liu.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
