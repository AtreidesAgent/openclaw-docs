# Humswap to release BOWL NFTs, will carry unique attributes for upcoming DeFi platform built on Neo

**Published:** Wed, 17 Nov 2021 18:53:33 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/humswap-to-release-bowl-nfts-will-carry-unique-attributes-for-upcoming-defi-platform-built-on-neo/
**Tags:** DeFi, Exchanges, General, NFT, BOWL, NEO

---

Humswap will launch Neo’s first algorithmically generated NFTs, BOWL, at 8:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, Dec. 3, 2021. The team intends for BOWL NFTs to be the staple of the Humswap platform, offering owners exclusive benefits such as access to a members-only portal, games, and airdrops. BOWL NFTs will be minted on www.Humswap.org.

Humswap is a DeFi platform built on Neo N3 that will offer liquidity pools for various tokens, a marketplace for NFTs, and token swapping. It will also provide an opportunity for users to play games and win prizes. Developed by Mario Lopez and Jason Tezanos, Humswap was a Neo Frontier Launchpad Excellence award winner.

Humswap bowls

BOWLs

In addition to special access rights, BOWL NFTs have a chance to mint with special attributes that provide functional benefits on the Humswap DeFi platform. Examples include reduced swapping fees, bonus stacking yields, and LP boosters.

The algorithmically generated BOWL NFTs can have millions of variations, with more than 200 individual assets and 150 different food toppings that can be uniquely combined for each NFT. There will be a total supply of 10,000 BOWL minted – each with a cost of 30 GAS. Users can purchase a BOWL NFT on the Humswap website via NeoLine or WalletConnect-enabled wallets, or through the GhostMarket cross-chain NFT marketplace.

Looking forward, the team will be giving away one BOWL each day in the official Humswap Discord server until the Dec. 2021 release day. Humswap will also reserve 100 BOWLs to distribute in future giveaways and contests. The first airdrop for BOWL owners will occur sometime in Q1 2022, though there is no further information at the time of press.
