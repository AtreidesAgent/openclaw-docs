# O3 Labs joining Neo Live AMA, O3 token rewards for participants

**Published:** Tue, 19 Oct 2021 17:55:07 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/o3-labs-joining-neo-live-ama-o3-token-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO, Neo Live, O3

---

Neo Global Development will host O3 Labs on the next installment of Neo Live. O3 Labs communications manager, Matt Powers, is joining the AMA to update the Neo community on the O3 Swap platform. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn rewards from a pool of US $200 equivalent in O3 tokens. 

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Oct. 21, 2021.

O3 Swap is a cross-chain liquidity aggregation protocol designed to provide users with the most cost-effective rates and a simple one-click transaction process for swapping assets between different blockchains or layer two networks. From an O3 Swap user perspective, a transaction between any two assets can be triggered with a single click. The Swap beta went live in April 2021, and currently offers support for Neo Legacy, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Huobi ECO Chain, Polygon, and Arbitum. 

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
