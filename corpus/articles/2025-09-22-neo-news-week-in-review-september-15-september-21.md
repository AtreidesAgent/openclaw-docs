# Neo News: Week in Review – September 15 – September 21

General Updates

Neo upgraded Neo X MainNet to v0.4.2, enabling its Anti-MEV feature following a successful deployment on TestNet. The Neo X Anti-MEV system combines dBFT consensus with cryptographic methods, where enveloped transactions essentially keep details hidden until inclusion in a block. This approach prevents block producers from front-running trades, executing sandwich attacks, or reorganizing blocks to capture value. Developer resources have been made available to support dApp builders interested in testing or deploying Anti-MEV-compatible applications.

Neo Global Development BD manager Raymon Chen will participate on a panel at RootData Connect Night, a side event at Korea Blockchain Week 2025, entitled, The Future of Public Blockchains: Opportunities and Challenges in a Multi-Chain Era.

SpoonOS launched SpoonGraph, a structured execution engine designed to support deterministic control flow, intelligent routing, parallel execution, and integrated memory management for AI agents. The framework is intended to improve reliability and auditability in agent workflows and address several limitations in current LLM-based agents.

SpoonOS announced a strategic collaboration with dLife, an open-bio infrastructure that puts individuals at the center of genomic data ownership. The team aims to integrate dLife into SpoonOS and enable AI agents with trusted biological context while preserving privacy.

SpoonOS announced a strategic partnership with Zypher Network, a blockchain and ZK-powered computing layer focused on adding cryptographic trust and verification to AI agents and decentralized applications. Zypher’s ZK-powered AI Agent verification will be integrated into the SpoonOS core framework as part of the collaboration.

Flamingo Finance noted that Epoch 24 concluded, that the total payout was US $4,708.93, and 52,822 FLM were burned.

GrantShares witnessed the release of funding for Pixudi’s travel grant request for Centre Point #2.

nDapp released a weekly N3 GAS check-in, which noted that network users claimed approximately 72,125 GAS in the past week, and the on-chain activity burned about 585 GAS.

Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the FrankCoin channel of the NeoF1 Discord server and distributed 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released NeoFS OAuthZ v0.5.5, which included upgrades to dependencies and requirements for Go version to v1.24 across the entire stack.

Neo SPCC released NeoFS v0.41.2, which incorporates metric improvements and bug fixes.

The inaugural episode aired on Sep. 9 and featured team members from across AxLabs, including founder Guil. Sperb Machado.
