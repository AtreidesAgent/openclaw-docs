# NEO Colorado meetup to host Tyler Adams of City of Zion

**Published:** Fri, 08 Mar 2019 22:12:39 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-colorado-meetup-to-host-tyler-adams-of-city-of-zion/
**Tags:** Events, NEOColorado

---

On Wednesday, March 27th, NEO Colorado is hosting a meetup with Tyler Adams, co-founder of City of Zion (CoZ) and Moonlight. NEO Colorado is a local community that seeks to establish relationships with developers and non-developers, and offer information about the NEO blockchain.

At the March meetup, Adams plans to discuss City of Zion open source environment, the impact of the Colorado Digital Token Act, and his experience running hackathons and workshops.

Prior NEO Colorado meetups include an in-depth 101 of the NEO blockchain, NEO’s path to network decentralization, and a recap of NEO DevCon 2019.

Additionally, NEO Colorado is participating in the twelfth Colorado Community Lightning Talk to discuss tools available for on-boarding developers into the NEO ecosystem. This event is going to be live-cast as a webinar.

For those unable to attend the NEO Colorado meetup (or live-cast), a video and audio stream will be made available following the event(s).

Time and Venue

Time: 6:30 pm – 8:00 pm (MST)

Date: Wednesday, March 27th, 2019

Location: Enterprise Coworking

3000 Lawrence Street

Denver, CO 80205

Registration

Registration for the NEO Colorado meetup is free.

Seating is limited to 20 attendees.

Individuals can register for the meetup via the following link:
