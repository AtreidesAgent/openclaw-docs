# Quirky Soul College Wave 1 mint concluding on May 14

**Published:** Sun, 07 May 2023 22:44:12 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/quirky-soul-college-wave-1-mint-concluding-on-may-14/
**Tags:** Gaming, NFT, FDE, QSC, QuirkySoulCollege

---

Quirky Soul College will conclude the Wave 1 mint of QSC NFTs on Sunday, May 14. Following the conclusion of this mint wave, the team intends to perform an airdrop to all addresses that hold at least one Wave 1 QSC NFT.

The mint waves are open for a limited time, allowing game players to create new Quirky Soul characters. After each mint concludes, users can’t mint Quirky Soul NFTs until the launch of the next wave. Each wave thus far has had a different amount of NFTs available to mint, but the total supply of the series will remain at 10,000 NFTs.

The first mint, Wave 0, ran from Oct. 31, 2022 through Jan. 22, 2023. The Wave 1 mint opened on March 13, and 1,005 NFTs were made available at 15 GAS each. At the time of press, 185 Quirky Soul NFTs remain in the Wave 1 mint.

About Quirky Soul College

QSC is the first dynamic NFT-based game on the Neo N3 blockchain. It was developed by Rentfuse founder Michael Fabozzi and was designed to be a Tamagotchi-like game where characters grow over time. The objective of the game is to acquire QSC NFT characters and level them up to win missions more efficiently and earn rewards.

Dynamic NFTs have updatable metadata that can be altered when triggered by smart contracts. The character images are dynamically generated onchain and are updated according to certain conditions. Example conditions include when a character is on a mission, when a character dies, and more.
