# Zeepin announce strategic partnership with SDChain

**Published:** Wed, 14 Feb 2018 21:36:23 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/zeepin-partners-with-sdchain/
**Tags:** General, ZPT

---

On February 13, 2018, Zeepin announced a strategic partnership with SixDomainChain (SDChain), a decentralized public blockchain ecosystem focused on developing an Internet of Things (IoT) architecture. 

SDChain seeks to develop their platform to suit common business needs, which includes digital asset issuance, user credit and identity management, peer-to-peer (p2p) communication, encryption algorithm, consensus algorithm, smart contract deployment, cross-chain contracts, market consensus-based incentives, decentralized applications (dApp), and rapid access to businesses. 

The IoT blockchain project uses the Six-Domain Model, a globalized standard to establish IoT operation and to ensure effective operation and sustainability for businesses. The Six-Domain Model is a culmination of the following integrated entities and processes: user, service provider, sensing and actuating, object, operation management, and resource interchange.

Zeepin and SDChain have developed a partnership to work mutually on research and development (R&D), standards stipulation, and community maintenance for long-term, sustainable development. 

More details about the strategic partnership will be released in the upcoming months.
