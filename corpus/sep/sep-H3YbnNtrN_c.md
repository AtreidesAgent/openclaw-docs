## Analysis

### Summary
Dylan speaks with Satrage Bram, CEO and co-founder of Rails (a PERPS trading platform on Kraken's Ink L2) and managing partner at Round 13 Capital. The conversation covers Satrage's journey from early iOS development through building a wallet during the 2017 ICO boom, how the FTX collapse inspired the hybrid centralized/decentralized Rails platform, the mechanics of on-chain custody with centralized order execution, institutional interest from traditional finance, and a bearish macro outlook predicting the market cycle top is near.

### Topics Covered
- Satrage's background: early mobile app development, 2017 ICO wallet building, running Canada's largest liquid crypto fund
- How FTX's collapse directly inspired the Rails hybrid model: centralized execution with on-chain custody
- Hybrid architecture: centralized matching engine + decentralized smart contract custody; users control deposits/withdrawals
- Building on Kraken's Ink L2: relationship advantages, USDT availability, OP stack interoperability
- Transaction rollups posted to Ink every 30 seconds for tamper-proof verification
- ZK proofs for privacy and provability; Merkle trees for tamper resistance
- Kraken as an investor in Rails' token round
- SEMA license: regulatory compliance, KYC/KYB requirements
- Institutional/tradfi onboarding: algo firms, market makers who won't touch unregulated platforms
- Perps trading value prop for tradfi: leverage, short-selling, spread trading, Bitcoin dominance trades
- Trading competition (11+ weeks): top P&L growing from hundreds to $20-30K weekly
- Token listing via community votes: Bitcoin, ETH, SOL, XRP plus Hype, Fartcoin, Pump
- Rails Play: 8-month pre-launch testing period with own liquidity
- Prop trading feature coming: educational funded-account model
- Ethereum outlook: purely a momentum trade driven by digital asset treasuries, not fundamentals
- Bearish macro call: Bitcoin Strategic Reserve vote failure in Congress as the pin that deflates the cycle
- Stripe + Paradigm building a new blockchain as bearish for Ethereum long-term
- Late-stage bull market: expects peak before November/December
