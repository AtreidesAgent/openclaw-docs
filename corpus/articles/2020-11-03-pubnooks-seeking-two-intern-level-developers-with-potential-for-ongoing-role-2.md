# Pubnooks seeking two intern-level developers with potential for ongoing role

**Published:** Tue, 03 Nov 2020 18:01:47 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/pubnooks-seeking-two-intern-level-developers-with-potential-for-ongoing-role/
**Tags:** General, NRVE

---

Pubnooks, a content sharing platform in the Neo ecosystem, seeks to bring two “self-taught” application developers on board. The positions are listed as intern level, with “potential for future employment maintaining the application.”

The Pubnooks platform seeks to reward content creators for uploading written and visual content, and for participating within the community. It is built upon assets purchased in early 2020 from the developers of the previous codebase.

Pubnooks leadership seeks to meet with individuals who “have or are teaching themselves the skills, are able to work with limited guidance, and who believe in their abilities to learn ‘on the fly’ while being productive and meeting goals.”

The first position will be responsible for various tasks such as portal integration, screen redesigns and adjustments, graphics development, table developments, and other projects as requested.

Developers familiar with MySQL, Redis, Solr, or Java, and other front-end skills such as React, Typescript, GraphQL, or Apollo are encouraged to apply.

The second position will assist in deploying the Pubnooks platform to the Google Cloud Platform.

Developers with experience in Google Cloud, Kubernetes, Docker, Jenkins, Ansible, Single-page application architecture (e.g. React), MySQL, Solr, or Zookeeper are encouraged to apply. Familiarity with the Java/Maven build process is a plus.

Interested applicants can submit their CV or resume to info@pubnooks.com.
