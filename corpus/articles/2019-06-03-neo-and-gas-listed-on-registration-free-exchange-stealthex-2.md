# NEO and GAS listed on registration-free exchange, StealtheX

**Published:** Mon, 03 Jun 2019 20:19:15 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/neo-and-gas-listed-on-registration-free-exchange-stealthex/
**Tags:** Exchanges, GAS, NEO

---

StealtheX, a Malta-based and self-described “anonymous and limitless instant cryptocurrency exchange,” has listed NEO and GAS on its trading platform. The exchange does not require registration or KYC/AML and claims that it does not share customer information with third parties.

At the time of press, StealtheX offers support for more than 200 cryptocurrencies, and plans to “add new coins whenever it’s possible.” In the future, StealtheX aims to explore the addition of NEO-based NEP-5 tokens and states, “some new [NEP-5] tokens may be added soon.”

A StealtheX representative answered various questions from the NEO community in a Reddit announcement which can be found here.
