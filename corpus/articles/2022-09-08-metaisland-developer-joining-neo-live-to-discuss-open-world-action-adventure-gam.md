# Metaisland developer joining Neo Live to discuss open-world action-adventure game

**Published:** Thu, 08 Sep 2022 18:11:43 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/metaisland-developer-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-open-world-action-adventure-game/
**Tags:** Gaming, Metaisland

---

Neo Global Development will host Nicolas Choukroun, a Metaisland game developer, on the next installment of Neo Live. Neo Live participants will be eligible to win from a pool of 10 Neoverse blindbox NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 12:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, Sep. 9.

Metaisland is an upcoming multiplayer open-world action-adventure game that will include Neo alongside other blockchains in its gameplay environment. Within the platform, players will be able to swap the game’s native ISL token between supported blockchain networks. Ultimately, the development team intends to support in-game assets and characters represented as NFTs across multiple chains. Metaisland is being built by a team of gaming veterans with experience at EA, Ubisoft, SEGA, Nintendo, and others.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
