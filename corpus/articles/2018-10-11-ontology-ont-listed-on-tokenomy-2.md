# Ontology (ONT) listed on Tokenomy

**Published:** Thu, 11 Oct 2018 04:05:05 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/ontology-ont-listed-on-tokenomy/
**Tags:** Exchanges, ONT

---

Ontology (ONT) was recently listed on Tokenomy, a Singapore-based cryptocurrency exchange. ONT deposits opened on October 8th, and trading began on October 9th. 

At the time of this writing, Tokenomy is ranked #139 in 24-hour trading volume on Coin Market Cap, with $451,000 in trading. Tokenomy offers nearly 60 trading pairs split among Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), and the exchange’s native token (TEN). 

There are currently no NEP-5 tokens offered for trading on the Tokenomy exchange.

In addition to its exchange, the Tokenomy Launchpad aims to “enable [the] easy transformation of any valuable offering into blockchain tokens, allowing companies and organizations to distribute [and] exchange their tokens.”
