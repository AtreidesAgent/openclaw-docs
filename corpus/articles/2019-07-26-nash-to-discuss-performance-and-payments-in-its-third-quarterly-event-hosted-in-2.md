# Nash to discuss performance and payments in its third quarterly event hosted in Boston

**Published:** Fri, 26 Jul 2019 20:50:45 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/nash-to-discuss-performance-and-payments-in-its-third-quarterly-event-hosted-in-boston/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, NEX

---

On Saturday, July 27th, Nash is hosting its Quarterly Event in Boston, US, scheduled to take place from 5:00 pm to 9:00 pm (UTC -5). The event will offer an overview of Nash’s progress in the second quarter of 2019, with a focus on performance and payments.

The upcoming Quarterly Event will be the third that the fintech company has held so far in 2019. The first event took place this past January in Amsterdam, and the second event took place in April in London.

Agenda

5:00 pm – Doors open

5:15 pm – Presentation begins

6:30 pm – Break

6:45 pm – AMA with audience

7:00 pm – Drinks and networking

8:00 pm – End

Location

The Samuel and Brookes Room

Kimpton Nine Zero Hotel

90 Tremont Street

Boston, MA 02108

Ticket Deposits

The cost to attend is an initial deposit of US $10, which is to be fully refunded within the following three days of the event. To qualify for the refund, a name must be provided on check-in. The deposit system was implemented to minimize the number of no-shows as “space is limited.”

Individuals interested in attending must register, or they will not be allowed into the event. Attendance is open to all ages.

Full event details and tickets can be found at the following link:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/nash-quarterly-report-performance-and-payments-tickets-64401284977
