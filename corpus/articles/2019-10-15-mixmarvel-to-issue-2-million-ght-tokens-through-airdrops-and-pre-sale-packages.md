# MixMarvel to issue 2 million GHT tokens through airdrops and pre-sale packages

**Published:** Tue, 15 Oct 2019 18:29:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/mixmarvel-to-issue-2-million-ght-tokens-through-airdrops-and-pre-sale-packages/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, GHT, MIX, NEO

---

MixMarvel, a blockchain-powered game publishing platform, is conducting a series of token giveaways in October 2019 to promote the upcoming launch of Ground Hunter. Ground Hunter is a first-person shooter (FPS) that will incorporate the NEP-5 GHT token into its in-game economic model, as well as utilize non-fungible tokens (NFT) that represent ownership of weapons and other assets.

In October, MixMarvel stated it intends to offer pre-sale packages, host giveaways, and run a referral campaign to distribute an equivalent to 2 million GHT.

There are two ways to participate in the token giveaway campaign: registration giveaway and staking giveaway. Both events will conclude on Wednesday, October 16th.

How to register for the airdrops

For the registration giveaway, individuals must visit the Ground Hunter website at http://ght.one, and register an account. Then, the participant must visit and join the Ground Hunter Telegram channel. Lastly, the participant must fill out the following form:

https://jinshuju.net/f/kdU2Yt

Individuals who participate in the registration giveaway will receive 10 GHT when the game launches. The registration giveaway is limited to the first 3,000 registrants.

For the staking giveaway, users can choose to stake in two tiers for this campaign. The first, if a participant stakes 1 NEO or 100 MIX, then there will receive 100 GHT once the game officially launches. The second tier requires users to stake 6 NEO or 600 MIX; in addition to the 100 GHT, the second tier participants will also receive a random level-1 weapon (NFT).

The GHT and NFT rewards will be issued via airdrop once the game launches, and staked NEO/MIX can be withdrawn three days following the beginning of the pre-sale of on-chain weapon assets. At the time of press, there was no official pre-sale date.

Currently, Ground Hunter is slated for release before the end of 2019.
