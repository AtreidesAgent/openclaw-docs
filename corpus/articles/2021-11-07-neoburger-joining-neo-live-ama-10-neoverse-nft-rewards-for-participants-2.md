# NeoBurger joining Neo Live AMA, 10 Neoverse NFT rewards for participants

**Published:** Sun, 07 Nov 2021 19:41:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neoburger-joining-neo-live-ama-10-neoverse-nft-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, Governance, bNEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host N3 governance dApp NeoBurger on the next instalment of Neo Live. NeoBurger early contributor and developer, Vang1ong7ang, is joining the AMA to discuss the new NEO wrapping protocol. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn one Neoverse NFT from a pool of ten.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Nov. 9, 2021.

NeoBurger is a new voting tool and NEO token wrapper called burgerNEO, or bNEO. The project was designed to compensate for the usability issues of indivisible NEO in applications such as DeFi, while also providing an avenue for maximized GAS rewards for holders. NeoBurger was formed by a mixture of Neo researchers and community developers. Ultimately, the long-term intention for NeoBurger is to evolve into DAO, which would become the controlling party of the system of smart contracts.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
