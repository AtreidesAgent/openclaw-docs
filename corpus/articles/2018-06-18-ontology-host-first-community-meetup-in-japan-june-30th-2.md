# Ontology host first community meetup in Japan – June 30th

**Published:** Mon, 18 Jun 2018 14:01:27 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/ontology-meetup-in-tokyo-june-30th/
**Tags:** Events, ONT

---

Ontology will host a meetup in Tokyo on Saturday, June 30, 2018 from 14:30 to 17:00. The team will introduce the company’s vision, projects, and community development with the audience. 

The key speaker, Mathias Glintborg, has professional experience in artificial intelligence (AI) and analytics with a Fortune 500 company, financial advising, and new product development. Mathias has been involved in several blockchain initiatives and has developed AI/blockchain partnerships between private companies and educational institutions. 

The agenda for the event is as follows:

- 14:30 – Check-in

- 15:00 – Welcome speech by Daniel Assab

- 15:10 – “Ontology Tech, Vision, and Community” by Mathias Glintborg

- 16:00 – Q&A

- 16:30 – Networking and refreshments

- 17:00 – End

The event will take place at Forum 8, Dogenzaka, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo Shintaiso Building reception desk 8F (663 Conference room). For more information about the venue visit the following link: 

https://www.forum-8.co.jp/access

There is no registration fee for the event.

To register for the event visit the following link:

https://www.meetup.com/Ontology-Japan-Meetup-in-Tokyo/events/251810394/
