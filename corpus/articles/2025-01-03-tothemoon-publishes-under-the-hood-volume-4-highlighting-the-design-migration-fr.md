# TOTHEMOON publishes Under The Hood: Volume 4, highlighting the design migration from 2D models to 3D

**Published:** Fri, 03 Jan 2025 19:58:18 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/tothemoon-publishes-under-the-hood-volume-4-highlighting-the-design-migration-from-2d-models-to-3d/
**Tags:** Gaming, NFT, TOTHEMOON, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON has unveiled the fourth volume of its Under The Hood series, offering a detailed overview of its ongoing development efforts. This latest installment focuses on key advancements, including transitioning 2D game characters to 3D models and developing land plots.

Transitioning from 2D to 3D Models

The team detailed its process for migrating 2D game assets to 3D, a shift designed to enable modularity, scalability, and future-proofing.

Source: TOTHEMOON

This upgrade involves refining default poses, adjusting character perspectives, and addressing complex design challenges. TOTHEMOON showcased three case studies from over 200 characters undergoing the transformation as part of this update.

Source: TOTHEMOON

TOTHEMOON also included an in-depth look at the transition of 2D to 3D models for specific assets, such as the racecar and several other construction vehicles (i.e., forklift, dump truck, excavator). The construction vehicles are important to the gameplay because they are integral to building a sustainable outpost on the moon.

Source: TOTHEMOON

The team noted the time-intensive nature of this process, emphasizing the hurdles faced during the migration of the model designs. These challenges, however, are outweighed by the long-term benefits of enhanced modularity and expanded implementation opportunities offered by 3D modeling.

Algorithmic Painting for Lunar Land Plots

TOTHEMOON’s update also introduced an algorithmic painting system for the game’s lunar land plots. This innovative system generates unique visual elements while embedding metadata for potential export into physical-world applications.

Source: TOTHEMOON

The aim is to create a balance between artistic expression and a virtual experience that reflects real-world authenticity (i.e., trees, shrubs, craters).

Under The Hood Series

Launched in late 2024, the Under The Hood series aggregates TOTHEMOON’s weekly development updates. It serves as a comprehensive resource for community members tracking progress on the blockchain-based game.
