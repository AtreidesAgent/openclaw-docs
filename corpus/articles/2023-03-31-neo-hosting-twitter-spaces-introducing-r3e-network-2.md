# Neo hosting Twitter Spaces introducing R3E Network

**Published:** Fri, 31 Mar 2023 23:18:37 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-hosting-twitter-spaces-introducing-r3e-network/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO, R3E

---

Neo is hosting a Twitter Spaces entitled Speedy, Secure and Seamless: Unlocking the Power of Real-Time Oracles. The Spaces event will be hosted by NF Senior Associate Adela Zhang, featuring Neo Global Development Head of Development Steven Liu and R3E Network co-founders Liao Jinghui and Gong Borui. The 45-minute event will begin at 2:30 a.m. (UTC) on Monday, April 3, 2023.

R3E Network is a new Neo developer entity that leverages trusted execution environments and real-time oracles to create a secure, interactive blockchain ecosystem. The GitHub repository states that the project is an AI- and zero-knowledge-proof-powered blockchain service layer.

Neo Twitter Spaces is an initiative to showcase community projects and members from within and adjacent to the ecosystem. The initiative launched in Dec. 2022 and has since held Spaces dedicated to the Decoding Web3 US Campus Tour, AI-generated content, and AI and blockchain.
