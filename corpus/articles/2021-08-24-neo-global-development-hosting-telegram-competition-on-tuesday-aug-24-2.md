# Neo Global Development hosting Telegram competition on Tuesday, Aug. 24

**Published:** Tue, 24 Aug 2021 03:07:39 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-global-development-hosting-telegram-competition-on-tuesday-aug-24/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting a community-oriented competition, Neo Trivia. The event will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel beginning at 10:00 a.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Aug. 24, 2021. The competition will take place over a span of ten minutes.

Participants will compete for a pool equivalent to US $100 in rewards. Winners will be chosen depending on the speed and accuracy of answering questions.
