# Sky Hut joining Neo Live to discuss NFT marketplace with NeoFS support

**Published:** Tue, 25 Jan 2022 20:01:52 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/sky-hut-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-nft-marketplace-with-neofs-support/
**Tags:** Events, General, NFT, NEO, NeoFS, SkyHut

---

Sky Hut is joining the next edition of Neo Live to discuss the NFT marketplace. Neo Global Development is hosting the AMA with Sky, Sky Hut’s pseudonymous founder, on the official Neo Telegram server. The hour-long event begins at 3:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, Jan. 28.

Sky Hut launched in Jan. 2022 and is the Neo ecosystem’s first NFT platform to utilize NeoFS functionality. It allows users to mint, auction, trade, transfer, and burn NFTs that store data on the distributed storage network. Behind NeoFS Send, Sky Hut is the second dApp to utilize NeoFS.

Sky is Sweden-based and has a Bachelor of Computer Systems Engineering from an Australian University. He has been developing for five years, primarily in C#, and followed the Neo project for some time before building Sky Hut. The prototype of the platform, Meerkat, was submitted for the 2021 Neo Frontier Launchpad hackathon.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
