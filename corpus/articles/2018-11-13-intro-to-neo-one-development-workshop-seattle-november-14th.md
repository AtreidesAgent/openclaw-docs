# Intro to NEO-ONE – Development Workshop – Seattle – November 14th

**Published:** Tue, 13 Nov 2018 23:11:56 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/intro-to-neo-one-development-workshop-seattle-november-14th/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

On Wednesday, November 14th, NEO Blockchain Seattle is hosting a developer’s workshop entitled “Intro to NEO-ONE” in Seattle, Washington. The workshop style event will be directed by Alex DiCarlo, creator of NEOTracker.io and NEO-ONE, and will teach attendees how to build smart contracts and dApps in TypeScript on the NEO blockchain using the NEO-ONE platform. 

NEO-ONE aims to make “coding, testing and deploying NEO blockchain solutions easier, faster and more efficient.” Users will be able to “create a private network, build smart contracts in TypeScript, write unit tests and debug with ease.” It is claimed users will be able to develop and deploy a smart contract on NEO-ONE within 30 minutes. 

Participants are encouraged to bring their computer to the workshop to start developing during the event.

Event information is as follows:

Date

Wednesday, November 14th.

Venue

WeWork South Lake Union in Room 1B

500 Yale Avenue North

Seattle, Washington 98109

Workshop

Time: 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm

Cost: Free

Registration
