# NEO and DeepBrain to attend Blockchain Connect Conference

**Published:** Sat, 13 Jan 2018 08:38:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/blockchain-connect-conference/
**Tags:** Events, DBC, NEO

---

On January 26, 2018, the Blockchain Connect Conference (BCC) will be held at the Palace of Fine Arts Theater, near the Presidio, in San Francisco, CA. The BCC is among the first US – China coordinated conferences and aims to provide a Chinese perspective about the future of blockchain, market dynamics, and technological innovations. The theme of the BCC is “The Evolution of Blockchain Technology in the United States and China.” The conference will connect innovators in blockchain for a day of keynote speakers, interactive panel discussions, and brainstorming.

Attendance is expected to attract over 1,000 blockchain practitioners comprised of scientists, entrepreneurs, investors, thought leaders, and developers from China, Japan, Singapore and the U.S. who are interested and engaged in the blockchain industry.

DeepBrain Chain, a decentralized Artificial Intelligence open platform and one of NEO’s first token sales, is listed a ‘Platinum Sponsor’ for the event among a wide variety of partners and sponsors who operate in the blockchain space.

Among the list of 39 speakers (and growing) are two with ties to NEO, Da Hongfei, the founder of NEO, and Yong He, CEO of DeepBrain Chain, along with many other established leaders in the field. On the BCC agenda, Yong He is listed to speak on ‘Artificial Intelligence Computing Platform Driven By Blockchain Technology.’

Tickets are available at two different price points, $499 (which includes keynote address, exhibit access, and breakfast and lunch) and $999 (which includes the previously mentioned along with VIP area access, VIP lunch, and VIP reception with the speakers).

You can find more information about the other speakers and registration at – http://www.goblockchainconnect.com/
