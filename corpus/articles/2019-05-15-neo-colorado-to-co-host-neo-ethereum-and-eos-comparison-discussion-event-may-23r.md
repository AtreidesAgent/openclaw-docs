# NEO Colorado to co-host NEO, Ethereum, and EOS comparison discussion event – May 23rd

**Published:** Wed, 15 May 2019 22:09:09 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-colorado-to-co-host-neo-ethereum-and-eos-comparison-discussion-event-may-23rd/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEOColorado

---

On Thursday, May 23rd, NEO Colorado is co-hosting an event where community representatives will compare and contrast the NEO, Ethereum, and EOS public blockchains. The event is to be co-hosted among four Colorado blockchain communities: NEO Colorado, EOS Denver, Ethereum Denver, and Denver Blockchain.

A subject matter expert from each ecosystem will offer perspectives on the various cultural and technical differences of their respective blockchains. Representatives include:

- Dylan Grabowski of NEO News Today and NEO Colorado

- James Young of MolochDAO and Ethereum Boulder

- Ashe Oro of EOS Radio and EOSDenver

Each participant will deliver a brief presentation, followed by a Q&A session moderated by a neutral representative from the Denver Blockchain community.

The event will be recorded for those who are unable to attend.

Time and Location

Thursday, May 23rd

6:30pm to 8:30pm

Enterprise Coworking

3000 Lawrence Street

Denver, Colorado 80205

There is no cost to attend this event.
