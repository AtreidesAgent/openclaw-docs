# New Year Moments Giveaway campaign to distribute prizes from pool of 320 GAS

**Published:** Sun, 22 Jan 2023 10:17:42 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/new-year-moments-giveaway-campaign-to-distribute-prizes-from-pool-of-320-gas/
**Tags:** Events, General, GAS, GasBot

---

Neo Global Development is running the New Year Moments Giveaway campaign, which will distribute 320 GAS shared among winning entrants. Participants can enter the campaign in two ways now through Jan. 24.

First, contestants can compete in the Gleam Points Collection by fulfilling various tasks to earn entry tickets. Task examples include following official Neo social media channels, Tweeting about the campaign, referring friends to the campaign, and more. 120 GAS has been allocated as rewards for this pool, and will be distributed accordingly:

- 40 GAS for the entrant with the most entry tickets

- 10 GAS each for the second through fifth most entry tickets

- 2 GAS each for the rest of the top 25 participants with the most entry tickets

Second, entrants can again entry into the Community Lucky Draw by submitting original photos of New Year’s celebrations in the #share-moment channel on the official Neo Discord server. Doing so will see the entrants awarded a special role that will admit them access to a #giveaway channel hosting GasBot-powered lucky draws on Jan. 21, 22, 23, and 24. Each draw provides participants with a chance to share from 50 GAS.
