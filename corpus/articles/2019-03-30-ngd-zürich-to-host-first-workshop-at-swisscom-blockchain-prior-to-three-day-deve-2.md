# NGD Zürich to host first workshop at Swisscom Blockchain, prior to three-day developer academy

**Published:** Sat, 30 Mar 2019 16:37:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/ngd-zurich-to-host-first-workshop-at-swisscom-blockchain-prior-to-three-day-developer-academy/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO Global Development (NGD) Zürich is hosting its first developer workshop on the evening of Thursday, April 4th. The workshop was organized by Swisscom Blockchain AG, who are developing a NeoID candidate and also holding a three-day developer academy the following week.

The purpose of the first workshop is to teach developers about digital assets in the NEO ecosystem. The following NGD Zürich workshop will focus on smart contract development.

According to director of NGD Zürich Lili Zhao, the goal is to host an ongoing series of workshops, tentatively scheduled to occur on the first Thursday of every month. The topics for the workshops will center around participant suggestions in the weeks leading up to the events.

The first NGD Zürich workshop will be co-taught by Dr. Guil. Sperb Machado, Neow3j project lead, and Steven Liu, an NGD software developer.

Before the event, participants are encouraged to create a NEO wallet, as there will be a lucky draw for one participant to win NEO.

Date and Location

Time: 5:30 pm (UTC +1) to 8:00 pm (UTC +1).

Location: Müllerstrasse 16, 8004 Zürich, Switzerland

Agenda

5:30 pm to 6:00 pm — Arrival and Greetings

6:00 pm to 6:20 pm — An Introduction to the NEO Ecosystem by Lili Zhao

6:20 pm to 7:00 pm — Digital Asset in NEO: Unspent transaction output and NEP-5 tokens by Guilherme Sperb Machado and Steven Liu

7:00 pm to 8:00 pm — Q&A and Networking

The attendee list will be finalized the day before the event to provide guest access to the Swisscom Blockchain building. Parking for the event is also limited, to request a parking space attendees should contact sarah.murphy@swisscom.com.

There is no cost to attend the workshop event.
