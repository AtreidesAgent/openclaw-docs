# TranslateMe joining Neo Live AMA, TMN token rewards for participants

**Published:** Tue, 02 Nov 2021 10:40:59 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/translateme-joining-neo-live-ama-tmn-token-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, Neo Live, TMN

---

Neo Global Development will host instant translation services provider, TranslateMe, on the next installment of Neo Live. TranslateMe founder, Ryan Lloyd, is joining the AMA to talk about the latest updates on and plans for the project. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn rewards from a pool of 100,000 TMN tokens.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Nov. 2, 2021.

TranslateMe is an instant translation service that has been in the Neo ecosystem since 2018, and was recently accepted into the N3 Early Adoption program. The service utilizes a trustless protocol that leverages machine translation and blockchain technology. Through machine learning and human validation, content is said to be translated with 99.9% accuracy.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
