# KuCoin exchange removes various KCS, USDT and NEO trading pairs

**Published:** Sun, 24 Jun 2018 02:58:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/kucoin-removes-8-neo-trading-pairs/
**Tags:** General, APH, DBC, NEO, ONT, QLC, RPH, ZPT

---

On June 22, 2018, KuCoin announced the removal of 8 NEO pairs, amongst various other KCS and USDT trading pairs. The exchange will remove the following NEO trading pairs: 

- Ontology (ONT)

- Litecoin (LTC)

- Zeepin (ZPT) 

- Aphelion (APH) 

- Oyster Pearl (PRL) 

- DeepBrain Chain (DBC) 

- Red Pulse (RPX)

- QLC Chain (QLC)

The decision was made after KuCoin staff analyzed trading pairs and developed a comprehensive assessment on where to utilize resources for best user experience.

Pending orders of the aforementioned trading pairs were closed at 18:00 (UTC +8) on June 22, 2018, and the trading pairs were removed from the exchange at 18:00 (UTC +8) on June 23, 2018.

The official KuCoin announcement can be viewed by following the link below:

https://news.kucoin.com/en/kucoin-streamline-trading-pairs-announcement/
