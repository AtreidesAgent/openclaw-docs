# CEX.IO the first exchange to support Neo N3

**Published:** Fri, 10 Sep 2021 17:54:02 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/cex-io-the-first-exchange-to-support-neo-n3/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, NEO

---

CEX.IO has become the first exchange to support Neo N3. Deposits and withdrawals went live on Friday, Sep. 10, 2021. CEX.IO notes that Legacy NEO assets held on the exchange will automatically be migrated to Neo N3. 

CEX.IO will also honor the Mass Migration rewards for users who migrate to Neo N3 before Oct. 31, 2021. The Token Migration Incentive Program rewards the early migration of NEO tokens with up to a 1% bonus, based on the following timeline:

- 1% bonus: 10:00 a.m. (UTC), Sep. 1 – 9:59 a.m., Sep. 15

- 0.75% bonus: 10:00 a.m. (UTC), Sep. 15 – 9:59 a.m., Oct. 1

- 0.5% bonus: 10:00 a.m. (UTC), Oct. 1 – 10:00 a.m., Oct. 31

At the time of press, CEX.IO ranks 55 in global trading volume with US $119.5 million in 24-hour volume, according to CoinMarketCap.

More information about the listing can be found at the link below:
