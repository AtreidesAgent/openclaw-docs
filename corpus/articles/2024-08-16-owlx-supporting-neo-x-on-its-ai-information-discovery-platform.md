# OwlX supporting Neo X on its AI information discovery platform

**Published:** Fri, 16 Aug 2024 03:16:48 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/owlx-supporting-neo-x-on-its-ai-information-discovery-platform/
**Tags:** General, NeoX, OwlX, Web3Labs

---

OwlX, an AI information discovery platform, has added Neo X to its list of supported blockchain networks. The AI-powered media aggregator allows users to track and explore Web3 projects built on Neo X and other ecosystems.

OwlX is a blockchain analytics tool that helps users discover new projects and perform due diligence on digital assets across various blockchains. The platform aggregates data from news and social media platforms to track and analyze sentiment and discourse around tokens and networks.

The information platform leverages AI to offer deeper insights into a project and tailor content that meets a user’s specific set of inquiries. It does this by building mind maps that create visual organizations of information in a hierarchical manner, allowing individuals to scroll through and view the highlights of an article. Further, users can help the platform learn more about their needs by participating in a multiple-choice questionnaire at the end of each article.

In March 2024, the AI-powered education platform became one of eight projects to join the Web3.0 Global Acceleration Program. The acceleration program is a collaboration with Web3Labs, a gateway for blockchain startups in Hong Kong, which seeks to support crypto industry growth by funding innovative projects. The Acceleration Program was announced at CyperPort, Hong Kong’s premiere Web3 business park, at the 2023 Neo APAC Hackathon tour finale. As a member, OwlX has received mentorship, investment incubation, technical and marketing support, resource integration, and legal consultation.

Since its July 16 launch, OwlX has amassed over 100,000 users and supports Web3 projects like Bitlayer, NFTScan, Star Protocol, and more.
