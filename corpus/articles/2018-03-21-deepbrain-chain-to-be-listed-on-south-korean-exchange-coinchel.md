# DeepBrain Chain to be listed on South Korean exchange Coinchel

**Published:** Wed, 21 Mar 2018 13:46:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/dbc-listed-on-coinchel/
**Tags:** Exchanges, DBC

---

On March 20, 2018, DeepBrain Chain announced they will be listed on Coinchel, a South Korean exchange. Coinchel is one of a small group of exchanges in South Korea that directly accept fiat currency to purchase cryptocurrencies.

Other South Korean exchanges that accept fiat-to-cryptocurrency conversions include Upbit, Bithumb, Coinone, and Korbit. 

Coinchel has four levels of authentication methods, which increase the amount a user can deposit or withdraw at a given time. The levels of verification include e-mail, SMS, account captcha, and Google authenticator. 

The DeepBrain Chain token will be the first NEO-based NEP-5 token to be traded on the exchange, which does not yet list NEO or GAS.

For more information about DeepBrain Chain, visit their website, Twitter, Medium, Reddit, or Telegram.
