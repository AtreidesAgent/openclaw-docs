# NudesToken to conduct NUDES airdrop to Save The Flamingo and Neoverse N-, E-, and O-series NFT holders

**Published:** Mon, 28 Mar 2022 21:29:31 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/airdrop/nudestoken-to-conduct-nudes-airdrop-to-save-the-flamingo-and-neoverse-n-e-and-o-series-nft-holders/
**Tags:** Airdrop, General, Meme, GAS, NUDES

---

The NudesToken project announced an upcoming airdrop of NEP-17 NUDES tokens to Save The Flamingo and Neoverse N-, E-, and O-series NFT holders. The snapshot for the airdrop will take place at 9:00 a.m. (UTC) on April 11, 2022. The NUDES tokens will be airdropped accordingly:

- 50 Billion NUDES to each Save the Flamingos NFT

- 50 Billion NUDES to each Neoverse N-series NFT

- 25 Billion NUDES to each Neoverse E-series NFT

- 5 Billion NUDES to each Neoverse O-series NFT

NUDES is the first memecoin to land on the Neo N3 network and has a total supply of 10 quadrillion. NudesToken is based on the joke that token holders can “send NUDES” to one another and, in return, “get NUDES.” Through the NudesToken distribution bot, users can receive 1 million NUDES for sending 0.069 GAS and 100,000 NUDES for sending NUDES to a new wallet, and receive 1,000 NUDES for any transaction with a minimum of 100 NUDES. Rewards from the NudesToken bot are made daily at 12:00 a.m. (UTC). NUDES are simply tokens, and in reality, no adult content is exchanged.

In Feb. 2022, Flamingo hosted its first NFT charity auction titled ‘Save the Flamingos,’ in partnership with Guarav Sirola, Ph.D., a researcher at the Wildlife Institute of India. The initiative, intended to support flamingo research, involved a collection of ten NFTs that were minted and auctioned on GhostMarket. All proceeds went to Dr. Sirola to finance the institute’s flamingo research in India.

At the time of press, NUDES tokens are currently listed on the Flamingo Finance non-custodial exchange.
