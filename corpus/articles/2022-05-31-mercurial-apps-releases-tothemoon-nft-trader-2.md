# Mercurial Apps releases TOTHEMOON NFT trader

**Published:** Tue, 31 May 2022 19:49:57 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/mercurial-apps-releases-tothemoon-nft-trader/
**Tags:** Exchanges, NFT, Mercurial Apps, N3Trader, TTM

---

Mercurial Apps has released the TOTHEMOON NFT token trader, allowing users to trustlessly trade for TTM NFTs. As part of the recently established partnership, TTM and Mercurial Apps will split fees, 0.1 GAS each, accrued in any trade on the platform.

N3 Trader is a decentralized peer-to-peer trading platform that allows users to establish direct trades for NEP-11 and NEP-17 tokens. Users can create trades with specific wallet addresses, or make open trades enabling anyone to participate as a taker.

Source: Mercurial Apps

The token listing page exhibits trades for TTM NFTs that are currently open, letting users propose trades using other TTM NFTs in their wallets.

In the announcement, TTM said, “The main goal is to get this new feature out and available as soon as possible for the community to enjoy. The teams are working on increasing the NFT loading times for larger collection holders. Minor adjustments will be announced in Discord in the near future.”

The TTM NFT Trader can be found at the link below:

https://n3trader.mercurialapps.com/tothemoon
