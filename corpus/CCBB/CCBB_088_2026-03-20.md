## Analysis

### Summary
Dylan delivers a detailed walkthrough of the finalized Neo Foundation financial report — the first in nearly six years — which reveals $460.8 million in consolidated treasury assets. He examines the asset composition (30% NEO, 25% cash, 23% Bitcoin, 18% GAS), custody concerns (86% of NF assets in a single-sig wallet, 51% of total in custodial exchanges), forward governance commitments, and compares Neo's treasury position favorably against Ethereum Foundation, Algorand, and Solana. While cautiously optimistic about the treasury's size, he remains skeptical of follow-through on governance reform promises.

### Topics Covered
- Neo financial report finalized with Hong Kong auditing firm verification
- First report in nearly six years (last was August 2020)
