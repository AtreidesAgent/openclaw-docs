# DogeRift suspends development of Neo blockchain-based game indefinitely

**Published:** Wed, 08 Jan 2025 03:51:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/dogerift-suspends-development-of-neo-blockchain-based-game-indefinitely/
**Tags:** Gaming, NFT, DOGEF, DOGER, DogeRift

---

DogeRift founder Marko Silko (aka Zoxyi) has announced the team is indefinitely suspending any development on the 2D/3D Play-to-Earn game and its associated Bored Doge Club NFTs. Zoxyi claimed the team will help compensate DOGER/DOGEF and NFT token holders with NEO. Silko’s statement, published in the official DogeRift Discord server, reads below:

Hey everyone,

We understand that some of you have invested in our NFTs or tokens and may have experienced losses. While our projects are currently paused, we want to offer partial compensation to our community members. Although we cannot refund the full amount of your investments (because funds did not go into our hands) we are distributing some NEO tokens as a gesture of support before the real bull run starts. Send me a PM (your wallet address, links or proof of your previous/current holdings).

If we resume development in the future, we would appreciate your continued support, but there’s no obligation.

Thank you for being part of our community.

Neo News Today contacted DogeRift shortly after the announcement to learn more about the team’s decision, but did not receive a response.

About DogeRift

The DogeRift team launched its GameFi 3D metaverse built on Neo N3 in May 2022, won the Best NFT Project at the Polaris Launchpad hackathon in 2022, and joined the Neo EcoBoost program in April 2023. The team sought to impact the blockchain gaming industry by providing an immersive gaming experience that would integrate Play2Earn mechanics, NFT collections, and a strong social community.

DogeRift was established by a team of experienced game developers and blockchain enthusiasts passionate about crafting unique gaming experiences. The platform offered various gameplay options, an in-game economy powered by DogeRift-native tokens, and exclusive NFT collections.

The game featured an adventure mode and minigame activities, including parkour and football. The experience was accompanied by a Play2Earn system aimed at addressing the limited earning opportunities found in traditional gaming. Players could earn rewards and exclusive NFTs through gameplay and community engagement.
