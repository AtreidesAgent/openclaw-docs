### Summary
Episode 31 covers Dylan's roundtable with Senator Hickenlooper's staff on crypto policy, Polygon's MATIC-to-POL token migration and its parallels to Neo's legacy deprecation, final takeaways from Rare Evo 2024 (Cardano's DeFi limitations but die-hard community), the Flamingo Finance Asset Support Initiative compensating Poly Network hack victims, COZ's official participation in Denver Walls 2024, and Sony launching the Soneium L2.

### Topics Covered
- Sony announcing the Soneium L2 — potential entertainment/rights management use case
- Senator Hickenlooper roundtable: Colorado builders meeting with senate staff on crypto policy
- The 2021 infrastructure bill that would have criminalized validators for not collecting KYC
- Rare Evo 2024 final takeaways: Cardano DeFi is not viable (UTXO model), but community builders are unshakeable
- Cardano's Midnight chain — ZK-enabled, interoperable; potential rift with existing Cardano builders

- On Sony's L2: "why wouldn't they need an L2? They're an entertainment conglomerate" — sees it as evidence the industry isn't dead despite bearish sentiment.
