# UFO Gaming joins EcoBoost program, to launch upcoming game on Neo

**Published:** Thu, 11 Aug 2022 22:12:07 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/ufo-gaming-joins-ecoboost-program-to-launch-upcoming-game-on-neo/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, UFO

---

UFO Gaming is joining the Neo EcoBoost program to build games on Neo due to the chain’s stability, security, and seamless user experience. The UFO Gaming team aims to create an interactive digital environment where players can find intriguing play-to-earn games, NFT marketplaces, and other blockchain-related features.

The UFO Gaming team is composed of passionate gamers who seek to empower other gamers through ownership of in-game assets. UFO Gaming will offer support for transferring in-game assets (represented as NFTs) across games. The team also aims to incorporate DAO governance and offer IDOs for gaming projects within its platform.

UFO Gaming CEO UFoger said:

Neo N3 offers improved platform capabilities and a refined economic model, including a flexible framework for developing dApp applications. This makes N3 the perfect protocol improvement for UFO Gaming. It will allow us to build some of our upcoming games on a quick, cheap, and seamless protocol.

Looking forward, the UFO Gaming team intends to launch an upcoming game on Neo with the support of a recently awarded grant.

About EcoBoost

The EcoBoost program launched in 2019, and was initially seeded with US $100 million to focus on recruiting partners. In 2022, the program relaunched with an increased treasury of $200 million and is seeking to assist in onboarding dApps, projects, and teams into the Neo ecosystem. Ultimately, the program is designed to incubate businesses that drive the growth and success of projects utilizing the Neo blockchain.

Once onboarded, members can become eligible for lifecycle support, including funding of up to $50,000, technological assistance, community access, marketing support, token listings, stimulation of liquidity, and access to other industry resources. Interested parties can learn more about the application process on Neo’s Eco Support page.

UFO Gaming joins Hacash.Diamonds, and Supernova as the latest entrants into the relaunched EcoBoost program.
