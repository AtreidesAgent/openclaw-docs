# Pink Paper joining Neo Live Telegram AMA to discuss its decentralized blogging platform

**Published:** Mon, 12 Dec 2022 19:01:48 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/pink-paper-joining-neo-live-telegram-ama-to-discuss-its-decentralized-blogging-platform/
**Tags:** Events, General, NeoLive, PinkPaper

---

Neo Global Development will host Rupin Mathur, Founder and CEO of Pink Paper, on the next installment of Neo Live. Participants will be eligible to win one of ten Pink Panda NFTs. The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, Dec. 13.

Pink Paper allows individuals and communities to publish blog-based content while retaining complete ownership. The decentralized content publishing platform lets users tip creators, establish a reputation system, and participate in crowdfunding opportunities. Currently, Pink Paper supports Ethereum, IPFS, Polygon, and Neo N3, and has acquired more than 1,500 users. Looking forward, Pink Paper intends to integrate NeoFS into its storage layer.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
