# Alphacat enters into strategic partnership with The Blockchainer

**Published:** Fri, 06 Jul 2018 03:54:00 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/alphacat-enters-into-strategic-partnership-with-the-blockchainer/
**Tags:** General, ACAT

---

Alphacat has formed a strategic partnership with The Blockchainer, a global marketing company that offers solutions for blockchain projects at different stages in their development. Alphacat has entered the partnership in an effort to expand into overseas markets, receive assistance in building its global ecosystem, and to access The Blockchainer’s exchange resources and relationships. 

The Blockchainer is a Singapore-based technology company that focuses on the development of blockchain enterprise ecosystems. The company’s services offered include media and social media casting, overseas industrial summits, industry ratings, global meetup hosting, and global digital currency exchange listing services. 

The Blockchainer’s connections and relationships with exchanges may provide ACAT the opportunity to list on larger international exchanges, which could help to improve the liquidity and visibility of ACAT. 

Bin Li, Alphacat’s founder and CEO, hopes the partnership with The Blockchainer will provide the platform with an expansive global strategy to grow its target user base. He stated, “our goal is to make ACAT’s financial technology benefit more users around the world, so that the ACAT ecosystem will be fully rooted into the global market.”
