# Upcoming Polaris workshop to focus on NeoFS development

**Published:** Mon, 25 Apr 2022 20:06:12 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/upcoming-polaris-workshop-to-focus-on-neofs-development/
**Tags:** Development, Events, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting a Polaris Workshop event that will focus on developing with NeoFS. Neo community developer Alex Walker will deliver a presentation entitled An Adventure using NeoFS. The hour-long event begins at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Tuesday, April 26, 2022.

Walker was the recipient of a NNT grant and has been developing Greenfinch, a Dropbox-like application utilizing NeoFS.

Recordings of all Polaris technical workshops can be found on Neo’s official YouTube channel.
