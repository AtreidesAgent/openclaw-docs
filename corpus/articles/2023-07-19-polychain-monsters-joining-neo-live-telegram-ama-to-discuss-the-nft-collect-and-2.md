# Polychain Monsters joining Neo Live Telegram AMA to discuss the NFT collect and battle game

**Published:** Wed, 19 Jul 2023 23:51:43 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/polychain-monsters-joining-neo-live-telegram-ama-to-discuss-the-nft-collect-and-battle-game/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, NFT, NeoLive, Polychain Monsters

---

Neo Global Development will host Polychain Monsters co-founder Sebastian Gronewold on the next installment of Neo Live. The conversation will focus on integrating Neo N3 with the character collection and battle game. Participants will have the opportunity to share from a prize pool of 10 Neosphere Pioneers NFTs. The discussion is scheduled to begin at 3:00 p.m. (UTC) on July 20.

Polychain Monsters is a cross-blockchain, play-to-earn game based on NFT collectibles incorporating DeFi elements. Polychain Monsters offers collectible, animated character NFTs, each with random attributes that differ in rarity. Users can collect Polychain Monster NFTs by purchasing digital booster packs.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
