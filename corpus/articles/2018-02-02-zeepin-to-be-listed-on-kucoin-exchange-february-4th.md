# Zeepin to be listed on KuCoin exchange February 4th

**Published:** Fri, 02 Feb 2018 04:20:41 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/zeepin-to-be-listed-on-kucoin/
**Tags:** Exchanges, ZPT

---

On January 30, 2018, at 2:00 pm (UTC +8) Zeepin Tokens (ZPT) will be tradable on KuCoin. Zeepin Chain, a decentralized creative economy, recently completed their presale in less than 5 hours on January 18, 2018.

Kucoin stated, “Zeepin is a professional and trustworthy team with infinite imagination and proven ability to realize all its vision. In an era of fast-moving digital progress, the pioneering spirit is the drive to create a new ecosystem by revolutionizing the structure of current industries.”

The Zeepin community currently expands to 186 countries, has 28,000 Telegram users, 43,000 Twitter followers, and 90,000 registered users. 

KuCoin will have a competition for traders, which will take place from 10:00 pm (UTC +8), January 30, 2018 to 11:59 pm (UTC +8), February 4, 2018. There will be three different levels of competition, which include a trading, deposit, and social media competition. 

The ZPT trading competition will rank traders from 1 through 100, and will be gauged on the volume traded in the users account (including both buys and sells). A prize pool of 3 Bitcoin and 2,000,000 ZPT tokens will be shared among the winners.

The second competition, will rank users on their total net ZPT deposits (deposits minus withdrawals), and will reward the Top 100 participants with 5 Bitcoin to be shared among the winners.

The last competition, asks users to follow the official KuCoin Twitter account, retweet the competition tweet, then fill out the official form found here. A total allotment of 3 Bitcoin will be shared among the winners.

More information about Zeepin can be found on their website, Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, Telegram, or Medium.
