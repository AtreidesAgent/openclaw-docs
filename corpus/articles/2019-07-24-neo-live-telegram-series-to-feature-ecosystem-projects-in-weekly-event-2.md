# NEO LIVE Telegram series to feature ecosystem projects in weekly event

**Published:** Wed, 24 Jul 2019 03:59:16 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-live-telegram-series-to-feature-ecosystem-projects-in-weekly-event/
**Tags:** Events, GUARD, NEO, SWTH

---

NEO Global Development (NGD) has announced an upcoming weekly Telegram event called “NEO LIVE”, that will feature a different ecosystem project each week. NEO LIVE is an Ask Me Anything (AMA) styled community event that takes place on the official NEO Telegram. Recently, NGD representatives conducted a Telegram AMA to discuss the EcoBoost program.

The first project to participate in NEO LIVE will be Travala, with Matt Luczynski, founder, and Steve Hipwell, co-founder and COO, representing the next-generation travel booking platform. The event will be held on Thursday, July 25th at 8:00 pm (UTC +8).

Additionally, NGD has scheduled the following projects to participate on NEO LIVE:

- August 1st – Guardian Circle, with Mark Jeffrey

- August 8th – Switcheo, representative TBC

- August 15th – Cobo, representative TBC
