# SafePal joins the Neo ecosystem, celebrates with NEO airdrop and gift giveaway

**Published:** Fri, 15 Nov 2019 00:03:03 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/safepal-joins-the-neo-ecosystem-celebrates-with-neo-airdrop-and-gift-giveaway/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Safepal, a hardware wallet provider, has announced the completion of NEO and NEP-5 token integration for its S1 wallet. To celebrate the integration, Safepal has partnered with Neo to launch an airdrop and reward campaign, with an equivalent of US $10,000 available in rewards.

SafePal currently offers support for more than 1,000 cryptocurrencies, has integrated mobile support, and claims to be “the first hardware wallet invested by Binance.” The hardware wallet is “100% offline,” meaning funds are transferred and stored via the use of QR codes, rather than via USB, Bluetooth, WiFi, near field communication (NFC), or other radio frequencies.

Source: SafePal

The team has outlined the steps required to enter the NEO airdrop, as well as the SafePal and Neo gift giveaway. The Neo airdrop is open to those that purchase a SafePal hardware wallet; the first 1,000 individuals that buy a SafePal hardware wallet can write “NEO” on the order form to register for the airdrop.

Upon receiving the SafePal wallet, each individual will need to tweet their opinion of the wallet alongside an image or video, tagging the SafePal and Neo Twitter accounts. Each successful registrant will receive one NEO coin upon completion of the steps and verification.

The gift giveaway will include merchandise with SafePal and Neo branding, including apparel, stickers, and a tote bag. To enter in the gift giveaway, individuals must follow the SafePal and Neo Twitter accounts, and retweet the NEO airdrop announcement.

The airdrop and gift giveaway contest will run from November 11th through December 11th.

The airdrop and gift giveaway announcement can be found at the link below: https://twitter.com/iSafePal/status/1194963209855340544?s=20
