# Asura Coin holding six-week community Telegram gaming event

**Published:** Mon, 18 Mar 2019 21:16:23 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/asura-coin-holding-six-week-community-telegram-gaming-event/
**Tags:** General, ASA, GAS

---

Asura Coin has announced a six-week competition, which began Monday, March 18th. At the beginning of each week, Asura will announce a Telegram game for participants to compete in. Participants with the three highest scores at the end of each week will receive GAS tokens as a reward.

The games can be played inside the Telegram application with the leaderboard automatically updating as community members achieve higher scores. The first available game is ColoorHit.

Rewards for the Top 3 participants include:

- First Place: 20 GAS

- Second Place: 12 GAS

- Third Place: 8 GAS

Each Sunday, the Asura team will announce the winners of the previous week, with the new game being announced on Monday.

Players can participate in the community competition by joining the Asura Telegram via the following link:

https://t.me/AsuraCoin
