# Neo partners with Supra Labs to integrate oracle and randomness services into X EVM sidechain

**Published:** Thu, 23 Jan 2025 15:27:26 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-partners-with-supra-labs-to-integrate-oracle-and-randomness-services-into-x-evm-sidechain/
**Tags:** Development, General, NeoX, SupraLabs

---

Neo has announced a partnership with Supra Labs, a L1 blockchain known for its high throughput, native oracle services, and flexibility for developers to build composable ecosystems using their preferred programming languages.

Neo plans to integrate Supra’s advanced oracle and Verifiable Random Function services as part of the collaboration. These features are designed to enhance the capabilities of Neo X-based dApps and strengthen its position as a developer-friendly blockchain ecosystem.

Supra’s cross-chain oracle network provides secure, decentralized, and scalable data solutions for dApps. Its infrastructure is oriented to deliver fast and reliable data, supporting a wide range of blockchain ecosystems. In addition to its oracle services, Supra’s VRF generates verifiable random numbers, which are essential for applications such as gaming and prize drawings, where fairness and transparency are critical.

In the announcement, Neo emphasized the strategic value of integrating Supra’s services, noting that they align with Neo’s goal of offering robust tools for developers and fostering innovation within the ecosystem.

Beyond Neo, Supra Labs’s oracle and VRF solutions are utilized across multiple blockchain platforms, including Ethereum, several EVM Layer 2 networks, Aptos, SUI, and more.
