# Da Hongfei to speak at upcoming NEO meetup in Berlin, Germany

**Published:** Sat, 13 Jul 2019 20:50:59 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/da-hongfei-to-speak-at-upcoming-neo-meetup-in-berlin-germany/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO Global Development (NGD) has announced a meetup in Berlin, Germany as part of its global “Powers Up” community engagement event series. The NEO Powers Up Berlin meetup will take place on Tuesday, August 20th and features NEO co-founder Da Hongfei in addition to other NGD representatives. Other global events as part of the series include a NEO meetup recently held in Sao Paulo, Brazil, and an upcoming meetup in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam.

LongHash, a blockchain-related project incubator and data journalism platform, is co-hosting the NEO event in Berlin. Jasmine Zhang, CEO of LongHash Germany, will offer a welcoming note and introduce Da, who will discuss the next phase of NEO’s development.

Additionally, John Wang, head of NEO ecosystem growth, and Longfei Wang, NGD blockchain developer, will offer presentations at the event. J. Wang plans to discuss the recently launched EcoBoost program, and L. Wang will provide an introduction to the NEO development experience and available toolsets.

Agenda and Registration Details

The event begins at 6:30 pm (UTC +2) and is tentatively planned to run until 9:00 pm on Tuesday, August 20th.

Agenda

6:30 pm – Registration

7:00 pm – Welcome Note, Jasmine Zhang

7:10 pm – The Next Phase of NEO, Da Hongfei

7:30 pm – Eco Boost Initiative, John Wang

7:50 pm – NEO Development Experience Intro, Longfei Wang

8:10 pm – Question and Answer session

8:30 pm – Networking

Location

Factory Berlin Mitte

Rheinsberger Str. 76/77

Berlin, Germany

There is no cost to attend the NEO Powers Up Berlin meetup.
