## Analysis

### Summary
A post-ETH Denver conversation with Felipe Cevallos, a Mexico-based crypto builder, discussing the industry's maturation through the bear market. Felipe argues that DeFi is the only vertical with proven product-market fit, that "vibe coding" with AI has democratized startup building by eliminating the need for large teams, and that crypto's most impactful real-world use case is already happening: Bitso handling 10% of US-Mexico remittances. The conversation also covers the gap between AI agent hype and actual delivery.
