# Switcheo and O3 Labs to co-host AMA on October 28th

**Published:** Wed, 28 Oct 2020 02:39:27 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/switcheo-and-o3-labs-to-co-host-ama-on-october-28th/
**Tags:** General, O3, SWTH

---

Switcheo and O3 Labs are co-hosting an upcoming Ask Me Anything and offering SWTH rewards for participants. The AMA will begin at 3:00 pm (UTC+8) on Wednesday, October 28th, in the #tradehub channel of Switcheo’s official Discord.

Participants can either leave a question in the Switcheo Twitter thread, the O3 Labs Twitter thread, or ask them directly in the Discord channel during the AMA.

Switcheo and O3 Labs are offering a total rewards pool of 3,000 SWTH to community members who participate in the AMA and meet the eligibility rules. O3 Labs and Switcheo will choose the top three questions from Twitter and reward 250 SWTH to each of the six participants. The remaining 1,500 SWTH will be distributed to participants who ask questions during the live event.

For Switcheo’s Twitter rewards, participants must follow both the O3 Labs and Switcheo Twitter accounts, join the Switcheo Discord channel, and register an O3 Wallet address.

For O3 Labs’ Twitter rewards, participants must follow both the O3 Labs and Switcheo Twitter accounts, tag three of their friends, and like and retweet the announcement post.

The two entities will select six questions from the live portion of the AMA, and the askers will receive 250 SWTH per question. Each participant can submit up to two questions, and O3 Labs and Switcheo will select six to answer during the event.
