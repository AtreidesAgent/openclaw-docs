# Carrot Swap launches non-custodial exchange on Neo X MainNet

**Published:** Thu, 22 Aug 2024 01:37:17 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/defi/carrot-swap-launches-non-custodial-exchange-on-neo-x-mainnet/
**Tags:** DeFi, General, CRT, GAS, NDMEME, NeoX, xBNB, xMATIC

---

Carrot Swap has launched its AMM-based non-custodial exchange on the Neo X EVM sidechain MainNet. The Neo X-based DEX offers users cross-chain bridging, swapping, and liquidity pool provision.

Source: Carrot Swap

Currently, users can swap or provide liquidity with MainNet assets, including GAS, NDMEME (the NeoDashboard memecoin), xMATIC (wrapped MATIC), and xBNB (wrapped BNB). Those who provide liquidity to the pools will earn CRT tokens as rewards. Additionally, individuals can migrate MATIC or BNB from their respective blockchains to the Neo X network. 

Carrot Swap also noted that incentivized TestNet campaign beta testers can view the platform’s leaderboard and their positioning based on points earned for participation to qualify for future rewards, such as airdrops and NFTs. The campaign comprises various missions available via the CarrotFi leaderboard, such as connecting social accounts, inviting friends, using the faucet, and making transactions.

Looking forward, the team intends to release a Launchpad module, and requests users to subscribe to Carrot Swap’s social media accounts for future announcements.
