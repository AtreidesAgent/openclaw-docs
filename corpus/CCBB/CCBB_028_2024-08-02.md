## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 28 marks the second week of Neo X with 126 contracts deployed (vs. 408 on N3 since 2021). The conversation covers Edge building a Neo X indexer, the Bitcoin conference with US politicians talking about strategic BTC reserves, the Neo X soft launch and lessons learned from Legacy and N3, new dApps popping up organically (Astero Neo, Turing Finance), the NeoPod V2 ambassador program, and Vitalik's warning against single-issue crypto voting.

### Topics Covered
- Edge building a Neo X indexer extending the Shrike project
- Bitcoin conference: Trump, RFK Jr. talking about strategic Bitcoin reserves, mining in America
- Kamala Harris and Democratic resurgence shifting prediction markets
- Vitalik's post warning against voting solely based on pro-crypto stances
- Neo X week two: 126 contracts live, infrastructure partners onboarded pre-launch
- Missing infrastructure on Neo X: The Graph, unified indexing across chains
- Lessons from three network launches (Legacy, N3, Neo X) — organic projects emerging
- New projects: Astero Neo (DEX), Turing Finance (liquid staking), CarrotSwap
- The composability advantage: existing EVM tools easily redeployed to Neo X
- NeoPod V2 ambassador program: three-tier system (Operator, Sentinel, Architect)
- Polymarket coverage in mainstream news
- Edge using Shrike indexer data for NNT articles and tax preparation

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- Dylan sees the Bitcoin conference politician talk as a double-edged sword: while positive sentiment from Trump boosted markets, Democratic resurgence now creates "a negative kind of externality that drags down the price of Bitcoin."
- On Neo X development, Dylan is "becoming more bullish by the day" as projects like Astero Neo and Turing Finance are reaching out directly rather than being funneled through NGD — a first in his three network launches with Neo.
- He characterized the NeoPod V2 program extensively, emphasizing the three-tier progression from Zeali point competitions to monthly stipends and NCA invitations.
- Dylan sees the program as "doing better than Neopod 1" because it extends beyond Discord to all social platforms.
- He highlighted stablecoins as the killer use case and prediction markets (Polymarket appearing on mainstream news) as an emerging second use case.

### Guest Perspectives
- **Edge** — Building a Neo X indexer as an extension of Shrike for both personal use and NNT articles. Noted that unified infrastructure (The Graph, common indexing) is critical for developer adoption — "if you're using one index from Ethereum mainnet, another one on base, and another one on NeoX, it's not really a good experience." On the soft launch, Edge emphasized that infrastructure partners being ready at launch "helps bring a little bit of faith." He listed user-facing priorities: DEXs, bridges to other ecosystems (not just N3), betting markets (Polymarket-style). On NeoPod, Edge appreciated the hierarchical structure vs. V1's "by way of fire" approach, and valued the gas incentives for social media activity while recognizing it's the standard playbook for ecosystem marketing.
