## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 44 is the final CC&BB of 2024, covering Edge's experiments with lower block times on Neo N3 (potentially 3 seconds), Dylan's 2025 prediction that GAS will be the breakout Neo ecosystem asset, the status of enterprise blockchain adoption (innovation comes from startups, not enterprises), the wave of new crypto users after Bitcoin's $100K milestone, a strong focus on customer service and UX for 2025, and Flamingo Finance's OrderBook+ 2.0 launch on testnet.

### Topics Covered
- Edge experimenting with lower block times on Neo N3 — 3 seconds as the prime candidate (to maintain 1 gas/block issuance)
- The separate issue of converging expected vs. real block time (currently averaging ~17s)
- Gas deflationary blocks: possible via NNS name registration (burns gas) and candidate registration (burns 1000 gas)
- Dylan's 2025 prediction: GAS as the breakout Neo asset, driven by Neo X adoption and APAC speculation
- Edge's 2025 prediction: Neo's prescient design decisions will finally be showcased — fast finality, account abstraction, NFIs
- Enterprise adoption reality: innovation comes from startups (Coinbase), not enterprises; they follow the herd
- Dylan's 2022 campus tour thesis revisited: institutional chains need proven uptime and track record
- New wave of users post-Bitcoin $100K — Dylan's 70-year-old family member asking about Bitcoin for the first time
- The UX problem: "if I can't figure out how to use your product in three minutes, I just won't" (Dylan never used Avalanche because of this)
- Edge's concern about security for new users — "known grifter promotes grifting" creating new targets
- Enclave as the happy medium between Coinbase custody and Ledger self-custody
- Flamingo Finance OrderBook+ 2.0 on testnet: unified liquidity (order book = underlying swap mechanism), limit/market orders, ~20,000 lines of code
- Cassette V2 launch on NeoFS; DexFun perps launch; Comet Protocol adding NeoX support
- 2025 resolution: bring more guest voices and perspectives to CC&BB

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- 2025 prediction: "we're going to see this explosion of focus or attention placed on gas as an asset" — GAS up 85% vs. NEO up 65% in 2024, expects APAC speculation to drive further.
- On enterprise adoption: "I always thought that blockchain networks that have a multi-year proven track record of uptime and resiliency... will ultimately attract the attention of large scale enterprise."
- On the UX problem: "if I can't figure out how to use your product in three minutes or three to five minutes, I just won't" — never used Avalanche because of this, and the same applies to a new Neo X dApp.
- On new users post-$100K: his 70-year-old retired family member asking about Bitcoin is proof "we've now hit that next level of perception."
- UX as the #1 priority for 2025: "the number one thing that as an industry we could do in 2025 is circle back around to the customer and the user experience."
- On OrderBook+ 2.0: excited about limit/market orders on-chain — "for the DGENs out there, you have a new way to be on-chain DGENs."
