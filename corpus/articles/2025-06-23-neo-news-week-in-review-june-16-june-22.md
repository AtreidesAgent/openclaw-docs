# Neo News: Week in Review – June 16 – June 22

**Published:** Mon, 23 Jun 2025 15:58:45 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/week-in-review/neo-news-week-in-review-june-16-june-22/
**Tags:** Week In Review, COZ, FlamingoFinance, FLM, FLOCKS, FRANK, Meme2Earn, NeoSPCC, SpoonOS, TOTHEMOON

---

General Updates

Neo joined the AI Unbundled Alliance, an industry-wide effort aimed at supporting Web3 AI evolution. The coalition is spearheaded by Aethir, a purported global leader in decentralized GPU cloud computing with over 120 ecosystem partners and more than 425,000 state-of-the-art GPU containers. The alliance seeks to empower developers at every stage of their journey—from early experimentation to global product deployment.

Neo announced that the Neo X TestNet will be upgraded to v0.4.0 on June 23, which will include a ZK-based DKG verification preview, ZK-based DKG execution support at the dBFT level, a New NeoXEthSig fork, and various optimizations and fixes.

Flamingo Finance introduced a new community engagement initiative, THE FLOCK, replacing the legacy FLM Hodlers Program. The new platform-specific Discord channel is accessible to holders of the FLOCKS token and is designed to facilitate open dialogue and lay a platform for community governance. Additionally, Flamingo Finance noted the conclusion of Epoch 11, with a total payout of US $11,677.03 and total burning of 250,593 FLM.

TOTHEMOON sold out the Waxing Gibbous (Phase 4) mint, and the team noted they will be airdropping the extra NFTs/refunding 1 GAS to participants who minted an odd number of tokens. TOTHEMOON also changed its primary website and deprecated the staking of TTM tokens for Infra Deeds, staking Resource tokens for Resource Deeds, the TTM Marketplace, and My Collection Access on TTM Marketplace. The project intends to migrate to the Base blockchain shortly.

Neo Vietnam announced the winners of the NeoPod Ambassador Cup, with first place receiving 25 GAS, second place receiving 15 GAS, and third place receiving 10 GAS.

Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the FrankCoin channel of the NeoF1 Discord server, distributing 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released a hotfix patch-release for NeoFS, which fixed a BoltDB panic.

NNT Catch Up

NNT released Episode 92 of The Smart Economy Podcast, featuring Aaluxx Myth, co-founder of Maya Protocol, a decentralized liquidity solution that enables native L1-to-L1 token swaps. Topics of discussion include how Maya Protocol’s approach to cross-chain liquidity differs from THORChain, the real-world impact of crypto in Latin America, how Prop 6 transformed a potential $500M ecosystem collapse into a sustainable recovery solution, the framework for creating sustainable tokenomics that prioritize swap volume over speculative value, how crisis management in DeFi can be handled transparently through community governance, and more.

In Case You Missed It

Neo established a technical collaboration with Arichain, an L1 public blockchain that supports multiple execution environments under a single unified consensus. As part of the relationship, Arichain will leverage Neo X’s consensus mechanism and MEV resistance, and Neo will expand its reach into a new EVM-compatible execution layer.
