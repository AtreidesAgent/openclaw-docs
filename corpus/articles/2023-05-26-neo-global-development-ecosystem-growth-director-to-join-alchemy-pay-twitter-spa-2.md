# Neo Global Development ecosystem growth director to join Alchemy Pay Twitter Spaces

**Published:** Fri, 26 May 2023 01:40:00 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-global-development-ecosystem-growth-director-to-join-alchemy-pay-twitter-spaces/
**Tags:** Events, General, Alchemy Pay, GAS

---

Neo Global Development is joining Alchemy Pay’s next Twitter Spaces to discuss the recent partnership between the two entities. NGD director John Wang will be speaking on behalf of Neo, which will take place on the official Alchemy Pay Twitter page. The event will begin at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, May 26, and there will be US $200 in GAS prizes distributed to ten lucky winners.

Alchemy Pay is an on- and off-ramping service that enables users to purchase crypto using fiat currencies. Neo and Alchemy Pay established a partnership in April 2023 to improve the accessibility of Neo N3-based tokens. The partnership will begin with listing NEO, GAS, and FLM on the Alchemy Pay plugin. The listings will make the assets available to all buyers and sellers using the solution’s payment network.

To become eligible for a chance to win GAS prizes, users must follow both Alchemy Pay and Neo’s Twitter accounts. Then, they must like and retweet the announcement and tag three friends. Lastly, the participant must take a screenshot of their attendance in the Spaces and share the screenshot in the announcement Tweet.
