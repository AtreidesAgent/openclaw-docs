# Fourth NudesArmy meme campaign to focus on Quantum Resistant Ledger, equivalent of US $5,250 in rewards

**Published:** Thu, 06 Oct 2022 22:37:00 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/meme/fourth-nudesarmy-meme-campaign-to-focus-on-quantum-resistant-ledger-equivalent-of-us-5250-in-rewards/
**Tags:** General, Meme, NUDES, QRL

---

NudesToken is distributing roughly US $5,250 in rewards as part of its fourth meme campaign, which focuses on the Quantum Resistant Ledger blockchain. The contest runs from 10:00 a.m. (UTC) Tuesday, Oct. 11 through 10:00 a.m. on Oct. 25. Additionally, 1 trillion NUDES will be airdropped to anyone who completes at least one daily task and claims NUDES tokens at least once between Oct. 4 and Oct. 18.

QRL is a blockchain platform powered by XMSS, a post-quantum secure digital signature scheme. The network is aimed at providing multiple options for safe custody of digital assets, and at interacting with blockchains built on the QRL protocol. The project has more than 12,000 GitHub contributions from 57 contributors and is currently preparing to migrate from a proof-of-work consensus mechanism to a proof-of-stake model.

To participate in the contest, users must first connect their Twitter account with the Nudes.Army website. Then they can upload meme-based images or videos up to 10 MB in size. When entering a meme for the competition, users must provide a title and select the “QRL [CONTEST]” category. Once the meme has been uploaded, the entrant must then Tweet a link to their meme using the following text template:

Look at this #meme I created for the @SendnudesToken x @QRLedger contest where there’s 100,000 QRL in prizes! Check out my meme here:

[LINK]

#contest #giveaway #NudesArmy $NUDES $QRL #QRL #quantum #crypto #memes

The top five qualifying submissions with the most upvotes will share from a reward pool of $3,000 in QRL, and excellence award winners will share from a pool of $2,000 in QRL and $250 in NUDES tokens. The top-placed memes will win the following prizes:

- 1st place meme: $1,200 in QRL tokens

- 2nd place meme: $750 in QRL tokens

- 3rd place meme: $500 in QRL tokens

- 4th place meme: $300 in QRL tokens

- 5th place meme: $250 in QRL tokens

- Excellence Awards: $2,000 in QRL tokens and 250 billion NUDES

The Excellence Award category is reserved for content that the NudesToken team deems high-quality memes. If there are multiple Excellence Awards, each winner will split the $2,250 pot evenly.

Participants who hold a Hired Gun NFT or OG NFT will receive multipliers on meme upvotes the NudesToken team tallies. Hired Gun NFT holders will receive 1.15x upvotes, and OG NFT holders will receive 1.3x upvotes. However, only one NFT per wallet is eligible; there can’t be multiples stacked in a wallet to increase the multipliers for votes.

As with the previous meme competitions, there will be an airdrop of 1 trillion NUDES for users who complete at least one daily task and claim NUDES tokens at least once between Tuesday, Oct. 4 and Oct. 18. Daily task examples include making, sharing, voting, and commenting on memes, or inviting friends. The airdrop will be split evenly amongst all qualifying wallets.
