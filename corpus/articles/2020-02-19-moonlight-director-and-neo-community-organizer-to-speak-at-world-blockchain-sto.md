# Moonlight director and Neo community organizer to speak at World Blockchain STO Summit

**Published:** Wed, 19 Feb 2020 21:40:14 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/moonlight-director-and-neo-community-organizer-to-speak-at-world-blockchain-sto-summit/
**Tags:** Events, LX, NEO

---

Moonlight, a Neo-based identity and workforce platform, has announced its director, Elias Tan, will be participating at the World Blockchain STO Summit on June 17th and 18th in Singapore. In addition to serving as a Moonlight director, Tan is also the organizer of the Neo Singapore local community.

The third World Blockchain STO Summit seeks to highlight innovative technologies with various verticals in the blockchain industry, particularly as it pertains to security token offerings (STO). The first summit was held in Mauritius in July 2019, and the second held in the United Arab Emirates in October 2019.

Per a conversation with Neo News Today, Tan intends to discuss various use cases of the Moonlight platform and the Neo ecosystem. Additional subject matter is likely to include security tokenization, with Tan noting he is currently collaborating with Neo Global Development on discussion points relating to the Neo ecosystem.

The event will take place at the Resort World Sentosa. Ticket prices range from US $250 to US $499, depending on options for access to particular services, activities, and events.
