# Tesla Model S 75D to be won in KuCoin QLC promotion

**Published:** Thu, 25 Jan 2018 04:54:04 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/kucoin-qlc-tesla/
**Tags:** Exchanges, QLC

---

KuCoin and Qlink have announced a (QLC) trading competition that will take place between 23:00, January 25, 2018 and 23:00 February 3, 2018 with a Tesla Model S 75D up for grabs. Times and dates will be based on Hong Kong Time (GMT +8). The competition has been listed in celebration of the 2018 New Year and the Chinese Spring Festival.

Qlink will reward QLC traders on KuCoin based on their QLC trading volume rankings, which is determined on a basis of the total volume of buys and sells. The competition will provide the rewards specific places in the top 200 ranked QLC traders. In addition to the Tesla, traders will also have the opportunity to win other prizes such as a Macbook Pro, iPhone X, or Xiaomi Mi Mix 2.

The 1st place trader will receive the Tesla Model S 75D, however, a cash reward is also an option equivalent to 80% of the Tesla face value.

Traders who place 2nd through 10th, will receive a Macbook Pro (13 inch, 2.3 GHz Processor, and 128 GB storage).

Traders who place 11th, 22nd, 33rd, 44th, 55th, 66th, 77th, 88th, and 99th, will receive an iPhone X (65 GB).

And, traders who place 111th, 122nd, 133rd, 144th, 155th, 166th, 177th, 188th, and 199th, will receive a Mi Mix 2 (Full Screen Display, 6GB RAM, and 128GB Storage).

Unfortunately, Chinese passport holders, and those who are unable to pass Know Your Customer (KYC) standard, will not be eligible to claim a prize.
