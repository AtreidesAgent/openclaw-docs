# NEO Colorado meetup to discuss NEO’s path to network decentralization

**Published:** Mon, 21 Jan 2019 23:59:32 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-colorado-meetup-to-discuss-neos-path-to-network-decentralization/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO Colorado will be hosting its second meetup on Friday, January 25th. The presentation at the event will discuss NEO’s path to decentralization, followed by a question and answer session.

In November of 2018, NEO Colorado hosted its first meetup, which attracted representatives from City of Zion, Aphelion, NeoTracker/NEO-ONE, and members of local blockchain startup companies and communities. The following month, NEO Colorado gave a condensed presentation at the Decentralize Colorado Community Lightning Talks, among five other 10-minute presentations.

The theme of the upcoming NEO Colorado meetup will be oriented around NEO’s path to network decentralization. Points of discussion will include NEO’s decentralization objectives in whitepaper 2.0, the status of current node network, the node candidate application process, and an example of a NEO node candidate.

The presentation will end with information about the upcoming NEO DevCon, “ContiNEO – We keep on going.”

For those who will be unable to attend, a video and audio stream will be made available following the event.

Time and Venue

Time: 6:30 pm – 8:00 pm (MST)

Date: Friday, January 25th, 2019

Location: Enterprise Coworking

3000 Lawrence Street

Denver, CO 80205

Registration for the NEO Colorado meetup is free.
