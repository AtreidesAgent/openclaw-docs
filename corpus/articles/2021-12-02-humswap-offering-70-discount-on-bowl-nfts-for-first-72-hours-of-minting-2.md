# Humswap offering 70% discount on BOWL NFTs for first 72 hours of minting

**Published:** Thu, 02 Dec 2021 22:35:01 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/humswap-offering-70-discount-on-bowl-nfts-for-first-72-hours-of-minting/
**Tags:** Airdrop, DeFi, Exchanges, General, NFT, BOWL, GAS

---

Humswap is offering a 70% GAS price reduction of BOWL NFTs for the first 72 hours of minting following launch. Beginning at 8:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, Dec. 3, 2021, BOWLs can be purchased for 9 GAS each. The discounted price window closes at 8:00 p.m. on Monday, Dec. 6. Afterward, BOWL NFTs will carry a cost of 30 GAS to mint.

Additionally, Humswap will conduct its first two airdrops in Q1 of 2022. The first airdrops will be for users who hold both a Neoverse N, E, or O series NFT and a BOWL NFT in the same address. The snapshot for this airdrop will occur at 3:00 a.m. (UTC) on Dec. 10, 2021. The other will be an airdrop event just for BOWL NFT holders.

Humswap is a DeFi platform built on Neo N3 that will offer liquidity pools for various tokens, a marketplace for NFTs, and token swapping. It will also provide an opportunity for users to play games and win prizes.

BOWL NFTs will act as a staple of the Humswap platform, offering owners exclusive benefits such as access to a members-only portal, games, and airdrops. In addition to special access rights, BOWL NFTs have a chance to mint with unique attributes that provide functional benefits on the platform. Examples include reduced swapping fees, bonus stacking yields, and LP boosters.

Looking forward, the Humswap team will focus on developing the liquidity pool, swapping, and asset pairing protocols. They said, “We are focusing on building a dynamic DEX that will allow for dynamic swap pairs created by anyone. Our model will follow Uniswap’s.”
