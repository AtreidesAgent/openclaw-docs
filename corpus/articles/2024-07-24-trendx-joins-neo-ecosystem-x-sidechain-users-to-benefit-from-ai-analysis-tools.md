# TrendX joins Neo ecosystem, X sidechain users to benefit from AI analysis tools

**Published:** Wed, 24 Jul 2024 01:47:17 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/trendx-joins-neo-ecosystem-x-sidechain-users-to-benefit-from-ai-analysis-tools/
**Tags:** General, NeoX, TrendX

---

TrendX is joining the Neo X sidechain ecosystem to provide AI tools for observing cryptocurrency markets. TrendX is an AI analysis and trend-tracking platform that captures investment opportunities in real-time.

TrendX offers a variety of signals and metrics users can view to discover new and existing tokens. The Projects section offers insights on social activity for individual projects using metrics such as real-time sentiment, discussion, followers, KOL & Whale activity, and influence.  tab breaks down tokens into i, and many more Web3 verticals.

The Trend section tracks various Web3 verticals such as DePin, Liquid Staking Derivatives, Real-World Assets, AI, Meme, Layer 2, DAO, and GameFi, and ranks their popularity by social media references, sentiment, and the amount that projects from each category have fundraised.

Alpha Square offers sentiment indicators, signals from traders, on-chain data and token chart indicators, and allows users to pre-set parameters for trading strategies.

Additionally, the platform provides opportunities for users to access research papers, participate in a TrendX token airdrop, discover trending topics, and more.

In March, TrendX was one of eight teams accepted into the Web3.0 Global Acceleration Program. The initiative is a collaboration between Neo and Web3Labs, a gateway for Web3 startups in Hong Kong, seeking to support industry growth by funding innovative projects. TrendX has received support in mentorship, investment incubation, technical and marketing support, resource integration, and legal consultation.

Galxe Quest

To celebrate the collaboration, Neo has launched a Galxe Quest in coordination with TrendX as part of the “Neo X Summer Fiesta.” The campaign offers an equivalent of US $200 in rewards—$100 in GAS provided by Neo and $100 in USDT (on the BNB Chain) supplied by TrendX. GAS will be distributed to winners after Neo X MainNet launches.

Users can earn points for the TrendX Galxe Quest by following Neo and the partners’ X accounts, creating and sharing posts on X, joining the Neo and partner Telegram channels, and obtaining the “Neo Knight” role in the Neo Discord server.

Galxe Quest is a platform for building and engaging with Web3 communities through a turn-key, no-code solution for connecting projects with individuals and running activities through social media platforms. Per Galxe’s Dune Dashboard, the campaign platform has attracted approximately 25.29 million unique addresses across 34 blockchains through more than 58,000 quests.
