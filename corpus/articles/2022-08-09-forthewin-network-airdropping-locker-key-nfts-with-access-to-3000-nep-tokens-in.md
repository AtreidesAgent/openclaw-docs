# Forthewin Network airdropping Locker Key NFTs with access to 3,000 NEP tokens in one year

**Published:** Tue, 09 Aug 2022 19:30:27 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/airdrop/forthewin-network-airdropping-locker-key-nfts-with-access-to-3000-nep-tokens-in-one-year/
**Tags:** Airdrop, DeFi, General, ForthewinNetwork, NEP, TTM

---

Forthewin Network is conducting an airdrop to Neo wallets that contain Fused Cryptonaut NFTs. Eligible wallets will receive a Locker Key NFT that will provide access to 3,000 NEP tokens after one year. At the time of the snapshot, Fused Cryptonaut NFTs must be in the user’s wallet and not in the Arenas or listed on an NFT marketplace. The snapshot will take place at 5:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Aug. 11.

The Forthewin development team is conducting the airdrop to celebrate the launch of Locker, a time locking utility for NEP-17 tokens. The service is designed to give Neo-based crypto startups and users the tools to handle vesting, delayed payments, and other time-restricted transfers.

Fused Cryptonaut NFTs are comprised of a Cryptonaut and a Rune NFT combined to battle in Tournaments and Arenas. The fifth Battle Arena tournament in Arena 128 opens at 5:30 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Aug. 11.
