# Binance exchange now supporting the deposit and trading of N3 NEO and GAS

**Published:** Mon, 22 Nov 2021 22:24:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/binance-exchange-now-supporting-the-deposit-and-trading-of-n3-neo-and-gas/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, GAS, NEO

---

Binance has added support for N3 NEO and GAS to its custodial exchange. Users of the exchange can now deposit NEO and GAS from the Neo N3 network, though withdrawals are not yet available. NEO/BUSD, NEO/USDT, NEO/BNB, NEO/BTC, NEO/ETH, NEO/TRY, NEO/USDC, and GAS/BTC markets are currently open for trading.

Alongside N3, Binance intends to continue supporting the deposit and withdrawals of NEO on the Legacy network.

Binance was founded in 2017 and is registered in the Cayman Islands. At the time of press, Binance ranks first in 24-hour volume on CoinMarketCap.com with approximately US $31.5 billion.

Binance joins BigONE, CEX.IO, Gate.io, Huobi, and OKEx as the exchanges currently supporting N3 NEO.

The full announcement can be found at the link below: https://www.binance.com/en/support/announcement/5764e08b99324ef8aab0c620e53bd1e1
