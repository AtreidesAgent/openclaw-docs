# John deVadoss to host roundtable discussion on enterprise adoption at BlockDown virtual conference on June 18th

**Published:** Wed, 17 Jun 2020 19:19:11 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/john-devadoss-to-host-roundtable-discussion-on-enterprise-adoption-at-blockdown-virtual-conference-on-june-18th/
**Tags:** Events, NEO, NGD Seattle

---

On Thursday, June 18th, 2020 Neo Global Development (NGD) Seattle lead, John deVadoss, will host a roundtable discussion during the second BlockDown virtual event. The discussion will take place at 3:00 pm (UTC -5) and is intended to focus on what the industry needs to accelerate adoption at the enterprise level.

Other topics of interest include incentives, economic infrastructure, game theory, and governance. The conversation will take place in English, with live translations in Spanish and Turkish.

Viewers can tentatively expect a recording of deVadoss’ roundtable to be uploaded to BlockDown’s YouTube channel for those unable to attend the session at the scheduled date and time.

The first BlockDown virtual conference was held in April 2020, where Neo founder, Da Hongfei, delivered a keynote speech that focused on blockchain solutions in a post-pandemic world, new features in Neo3, and an update on the progress of Neo’s cross-chain interoperability protocol.

Event Details

The two-day Block Down virtual event proposes to capture an aggregate of 24-hours of content and offer conference-like networking opportunities. Notable speakers include Changpeng Zhao, Akon, Jed McCaleb, Brock Pierce, and David Schwartz, among others.

Each registrant will be able to create a unique 3D avatar to navigate the virtual conference, visit exhibition booths, and attend events. 2D options are also available to attendees who prefer to simply livestream footage.

There will be no special equipment (i.e., virtual reality headset) required for individuals to participate.

There is no cost to attend the virtual event with a General Admission ticket. Additional packages that offer access to exclusive content are available at a cost of at £20.00 for the Business level and £50.00 for the VIP level.

Registration for the event can be found at the link below:

https://blockdownconf.com/tickets/
