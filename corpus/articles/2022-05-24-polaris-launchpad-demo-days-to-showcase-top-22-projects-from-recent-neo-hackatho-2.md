# Polaris Launchpad demo days to showcase top 22 projects from recent Neo hackathon

**Published:** Tue, 24 May 2022 03:25:56 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/polaris-launchpad-demo-days-to-showcase-top-22-projects-from-recent-neo-hackathon/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting two weeks of Polaris Launchpad demos starting May 23, 2022, and running through June 2. 22 of the top projects built during the recent hackathon phase will be showcased.

Each demo day session will run from 2:00 p.m. (UTC) to 3:00 p.m, featuring three to four projects. NGD will record the sessions and upload the videos to the official Neo YouTube channel.

The Polaris Launchpad demo day on May 23 included presentations from the Superson Solution, SOMNIUMWAVE, and Neo-Effisend projects. The presentations can be found below:

Demo Week One Agenda

Tuesday, May 24

- Feedio

- Findtruman

- Planet X

Wednesday, May 25

- DogeRift

- Pumpkin Swap

- NeoDashboard

Thursday, May 26

- DAO Cake

- NeoDao

- Bowerbird

- LatchBox

Looking forward, NGD will release the schedule and project overview for the second week of Polaris Launchpad demonstrations. The winners of the hackathon will be announced on Monday, May 30.
