## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 68 is a post-Blockchain Rio debrief, highlighting the Block Spirits activation that drove 25,000+ on-chain invocations in two days on Neo N3 — a 629% increase from one app alone. Dylan shares takeaways about Latin America as an untapped market, Guil discusses SpoonOS YouTube educational content reaching 200-300 live viewers, and the episode covers CenterPoint 2 in Singapore, a new GrantShares proposal from Vaultin, and Flamingo Finance listing USDC.

### Topics Covered
- Blockchain Rio activation results: 25,000+ on-chain invocations, 31,562 total over 5 days, ~$4,700 in gas spent
- Latin America as a region that needs blockchain technology (remittances, currency debasement)
- The institutional trend at conferences vs. developer/community energy
- SpoonOS Dev Call Season 1 conclusion and YouTube educational content performance
- CenterPoint 2 announcement: September 27-29 at Carlton Hotel, Singapore
- Travel grants available through GrantShares for CenterPoint attendees
- New GrantShares proposal from Vaultin (escape room puzzle platform)
- Flamingo Finance USDC listing
- Plans to attend ETH DevConnect in Argentina (November)
- Upcoming community call with NGD and developer communities
