# Neo News: Week in Review – March 10 – March 16

**Published:** Mon, 17 Mar 2025 16:22:05 +0000

General Updates

Neo Global Development Head of Ecosystem Growth John Wang joined a panel entitled Let’s BUILD – Connecting the future and creating a new Web3 ecosystem in Hong Kong. Other panelists included representatives from Web3Labs, TON Foundation, and OxU Hong Kong Blockchain Club.

Neo teamed up with KiloEx on a Galxe Quest campaign to increase awareness for the launch of the KiloEx token, KILO.

GrantShares announced it is calling for new DAO members after adding a new Request for Proposals feature. Candidates will be expected to participate in GrantShares governance decisions, evaluate proposals, and support ecosystem growth. Interested applicants can submit proposals via the GrantShares dApp before March 23, 2025.

DecentraMind announced a collaboration with Neo on a community rewards campaign through the social distribution platform. To participate in the campaign, users must quote the announcement link with a comment, include their quest invitation link in the comment, and submit the URL of their quoted post. The reward pool for participation includes 200 HIBIT tokens. The campaign is currently live and runs through March 21.

Flamingo Finance announced that it will sunset rewards for reverse liquidity pools beginning March 14. Reverse pools allow users to stake FLM and “x” tokens in a liquidity pool and, in return, receive “x” tokens as a reward instead of FLM.

Enclave Wallet launched the beta version of its account abstraction web wallet on Neo N3 TestNet.

Neo Smart Economy Japan hosted an AMA to discuss NeoPod community activities and ways in which users can participate.

NeoDashboard launched a lottery-based game, Jackpot Junction, on its platform that allows users to join pools of various sizes (i.e., 5,000 or 10,000 NDMEME ticket entries) and potentially win NDMEME token prizes. Additionally, the NDMEME NEP-17 token has been listed on Flamingo Finance OrderBook+ with a pair against the FUSD over-collateralized stablecoin.

Frank Coin’s FRANK NEP-17 token was listed on Flamingo Finance OrderBook+ with a pair against the FUSD stablecoin. Additionally, Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the NeoF1 Discord server and distributed 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

NeoRedPill NRP NEP-17 token was added to nDapp’s app and dApp discovery platform.

NNT Catch Up

NNT released Episode 85 of The Smart Economy Podcast featuring Patrick Young, head of go-to-market at Galxe, a platform for Web3 community growth and engagement. Topics of discussion include how Galxe builds and maintains resilient Web3 communities, Galxe’s identity solutions, the role of Galxe’s EarnDrop program in assisting with token distribution and community building, the development of Galxe’s upcoming L1 blockchain called Gravity Chain, the convergence of blockchain and AI technologies, Galxe’s suite of products creating an end-to-end lifecycle management system for Web3 projects, and much more.

NNT editors co-hosted Crypto Coffee and Blockchain Beer Spaces #53 on the official The Smart Economy Podcast X account. Topics of discussion included Neo community initiatives from the ground up, the pros and cons of the US outlook on crypto and blockchain, lessons learned from the first week of OrderBook+ 2.0 on Flamingo Finance, the role FLOCKS can play in onboarding new DeFi users, tips for surviving moments of negative sentiment, and Enclave going live on Neo N3 TestNet.

In Case You Missed It

Hibit integrated support for the Neo X EVM sidechain into its non-custodial exchange, enabling trading and permissionless token listings for Neo X assets. Additionally, users can compete for rewards by participating in a DecentraMind community campaign.
