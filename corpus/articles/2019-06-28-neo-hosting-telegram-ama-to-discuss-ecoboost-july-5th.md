# NEO hosting Telegram AMA to discuss EcoBoost – July 5th

**Published:** Fri, 28 Jun 2019 21:45:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-hosting-telegram-ama-to-discuss-ecoboost-july-5th/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

NEO has announced an upcoming live Ask Me Anything (AMA) event, scheduled to take place from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm (UTC +8) on Friday, July 5th on the NEO Telegram. The topic of discussion is the official launch of the EcoBoost fund.

The US $100m NEO EcoBoost fund aims to provide NEO-based projects with financial assistance alongside a support network to encourage growth. NEO Global Development (NGD) recently released an announcement highlighting the three phases of the fund.

The newly launched first phase of the EcoBoost fund is focused on establishing long-term partnerships with entities in the blockchain industry such as media outlets, wallet providers, dApp discovery platforms, and larger-scale organizations such as universities or exchanges.

Source: NEO

NEO representatives attending the AMA include:

- Yuan Gao, NEO Global Development (NGD) marketing director

- Longfei Wang, NEO blockchain engineer

- John Wang, NEO director of Ecosystem Growth
