# TOTHEMOON announces “Mint Week,” feat. marketplace launch and release of 7,800 NFTs

**Published:** Sun, 05 Dec 2021 18:01:16 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/tothemoon-announces-mint-week-feat-marketplace-launch-and-release-of-7800-nfts/
**Tags:** Airdrop, Events, Gaming, General, Metaverse, NFT, GAS, NEO, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON has announced an upcoming “Mint Week,” which will see the launch of The Market, the project’s first airdrop, and the minting of the first four Character types. Mint Week will run from Monday, Dec. 13 through Sunday, Dec. 19.

TOTHEMOON is a strategic community-building NFT game built on Neo N3 where collectors engage with the moon-themed metaverse. The TOTHEMOON team aims to establish this metaverse by layering and integrating NFTs in a way that establishes a thriving supply and demand economy. When users purchase a character NFT, they become eligible for Item NFT and NEP-17 TTM token airdrops.

The team will release different types of NFTs in various cycles as the gameplay expands, beginning with Cycle 1, expected to take place over a year approximately. Later cycles will offer new types of NFTs that serve different purposes as the game evolves. Various characters and items will be introduced through a series of releases and airdrops dubbed “Moon Phases.” The first moon phase, entitled “New Moon,” will kick off during Mint Week.

The Market, The Airdrop, and The AMA

The Market will launch on Monday, Dec. 13. This in-platform marketplace will eventually allow users to buy, sell, trade, and auction NFTs. Initially, it will only support the buying and selling of NFTs. However, in Q1 2022, the development team intends to implement trading functionality.

On Tuesday, Dec. 14, those who minted a Neoverse N3 Element piece during Neo’s NFT celebration event will receive an Item airdrop. Items in TOTHEMOON interact with and respond to characteristics of other in-game NFTs. For example, a fishing rod NFT used for mini-games can also be used to stake and earn rewards.

On Wednesday, Dec. 15, the team is hosting an AMA on the TOTHEMOON official Discord server, beginning at 5:00 p.m. (UTC). Participants who join the AMA will be eligible to win rewards from the team.

Minting NFTs

Character type NFTs will begin minting on Thursday, Dec. 16, 2021. Participants must use the Neo Line or O3 Wallet to mint an NFT. The Character NFTs will be available for purchase on a first-come, first-serve basis via The Market. Once an NFT has been minted, users can sell and buy their NFTs immediately on The Market.

Character NFTs will each be minted with a fixed supply and different costs. The only way to purchase a Character NFT is with GAS. Listing date, characters, supply, and cost are as follows:

- Dec. 16 – Moon Baby: 100 NFTs at 50 GAS each

- Dec. 17 – Moon Trainer: 200 NFTs at 25 GAS each

- Dec. 18 – Cryptonaut: 2,500 NFTs at 10 GAS each

- Dec. 19 – Moon Creature: 5,000 NFTs at 10 GAS each

The exact time on the day each character mint begins will be determined by community members in an upcoming poll. The poll will be held on the TOTHEMOON Twitter account, running from Monday, Dec. 6 to Friday, Dec. 10.
