# Da Hongfei to speak at APAC Conference in Melbourne, Australia

**Published:** Sun, 14 Jan 2018 06:32:32 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/apac-blockchain-concerence-melbourne/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

The APAC Blockchain Conference will be held at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Center in Australia, on March 13 – 15, 2018. The event was developed in partnership with the Australian Digital Commerce Association (ADCA). The ADCA is the industry network for Australian businesses implementing blockchain solutions, whose members work together to build understanding of the potential of blockchain technology, share best practices and engage with government and regulatory stakeholders. The theme of the APAC Blockchain Conference is “Seizing the Opportunities and Realizing the Commercial Potential of this Revolutionary Technology.”

The second annual event will bring together blockchain innovators, business leaders, and regulators to help build blockchain strategies and assess the viability of the technology. The first event, held in March of 2017, hosted over 150 leaders at the forefront of blockchain technology to discuss leading projects, innovative start-ups, and regulations. Attendance is expected to be comprised of individuals in the banking, finance, government, insurance, supply chain, informations and communications technology, and legal sectors.

Of the 42 speakers listed, NEO’s founder, Da Hongfei has been invited to lead a session on Tuesday, March 13, entitled “From Digital Asset to Smart Economy – Public Blockchain Can Be Legal/Compliance-Compatible.” The full agenda can be found at the APAC Blockchain Conference website

Ticket “event packages” are available on-line, and range in various costs depending on the amount of time an attendee plans to spend at the conference. More information on pricing can be found at: http://www.apacblockchain.com.au/registration.
