# Pink Paper writing competition now live, US $1,000 in rewards for winning entrants

**Published:** Thu, 20 Apr 2023 15:12:26 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/pink-paper-writing-competition-now-live-us-1000-in-rewards-for-winning-entrants/
**Tags:** General, fUSDT, GAS, NEO

---

Pink Paper is hosting a writing competition with a total prize pool equivalent to US $1,000. Participants can submit written content about the Neo ecosystem or Pink Paper. The competition is currently live and is running through Tuesday, April 25. 

Pink Paper allows individuals and communities to publish written content while retaining complete ownership. The decentralized content publishing platform lets users tip creators, establish a reputation system, and participate in crowdfunding opportunities.

Contest entrants must publish an article about Neo or Pink Paper on the decentralized content-sharing platform to enter. Then, participants must retweet their article on Twitter, including a link to this Pink Paper tweet and the #PinkPaper hashtag in the retweet. The Pink Paper team has created a list of eligible topics for the competition, which include but are not limited to:

- An Introduction to Neo

- Neo’s Smart Contract System

- Neo’s Consensus Algorithm

- An Introduction to Pink Paper

- Features of Pink Paper

The submissions will be judged by various factors, which are clear and concise writing, originality, focus, structure, creativity, clarity, relevance, grammar and spelling, and authenticity. The Pink Paper team noted that the participant is automatically disqualified if ChatGPT submissions are detected.

The Pink Paper team will judge the submissions and deliberate the winners. Prize winners will share from a pool of $1,000 equivalent of NEO, GAS, or USDT, which will be distributed accordingly: 

- First place: $300

- Second place: $200

- Third place: $100

- Runner-up (8 prizes): $25 each

- Participation (20 prizes): $10 each
