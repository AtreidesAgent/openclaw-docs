# Nash announces Q1 report and AMA livestream to take place April 24th

**Published:** Fri, 17 Apr 2020 19:08:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/nash-announces-q1-report-and-ama-livestream-to-take-place-april-24th/
**Tags:** Events, NEX

---

Nash has announced that its next Quarterly Report event is scheduled to take place online at 6:00 pm (UTC+0) on Friday, April 24th, 2020. A link to the livestream will be shared via the Nash Twitter shortly before the event.

In a shift from the format of previous Quarterly Report events, there will no longer be predefined “topics” that the team will present on. Moving forward, Nash plans on providing updates on the growth and execution of its products and services. This includes products that are not yet publicly available but have continued to progress internally, such as Nash Pay and the Nash fiat ramp.

Nash community members can also post questions on the Nash Community forum Q1 2020 thread, which may be included in the ask me anything (AMA) portion of the Quarterly Report event.

The announcement can be found at the link below:
