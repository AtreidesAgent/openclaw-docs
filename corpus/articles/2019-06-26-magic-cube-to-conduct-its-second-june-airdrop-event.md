# Magic Cube to conduct its second June airdrop event

**Published:** Wed, 26 Jun 2019 17:12:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/magic-cube-to-conduct-its-second-june-airdrop-event/
**Tags:** General, MCC

---

Magic Cube, a NEO-based blockchain gaming platform, has announced its second token airdrop event for the month of June. Participants who complete a four-step registration process will receive 1,000 MCC tokens. The campaign runs from Tuesday, June 25th through Sunday, June 30th.

(Update: As of June 26th, Magic Cube has ended registration for the airdrop early, citing “overwhelming demand.”)

For the campaign, Magic Cube has partnered with BitMart, a centralized exchange. As part of the cooperative efforts, BitMart will allow Magic Cube to use its Telegram channel to market the MCC airdrop event.

The back-to-back airdrop events are part of a Western marketing strategy. In a conversation with NEO News Today, Cole Petersen, Magic Cube’s chief U.S. operations partner, said:

After building a robust community in China, Magic Cube is now trying to expand into the U.S. markets and is kicking off this western expansion with several community-based giveaways. In the coming months, we hope to be able to have a large and supportive English community that can allow us to spread our vision of building a decentralized entertainment ecosystem where everyone – from gamers to developers – can play a role.

To become eligible for the airdrop, individuals have five days to register through the four-step process.

First, participants must follow the Magic Cube Twitter profile, then like and retweet the announcement. Afterward, participants will need to subscribe to the Magic Cube Telegram channel.

Once these tasks are complete, participants must submit their Twitter username, Telegram handle, and NEO wallet address through the following Google document:

https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe9JtcRgXQS_ZDqEKPSJ3fZVzxDh-fzi-CFM1x6VbLv24ifNg/viewform

According to Petersen, the reward distribution will take place within 24 hours following the conclusion of the registration period.
