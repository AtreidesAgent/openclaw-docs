# Da Hongfei, Lili Zhao, and Odd Jørgen Røland to speak at Epicenter conference hosted by Coin Telegraph

**Published:** Fri, 05 Nov 2021 22:47:28 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/da-hongfei-lili-zhao-and-odd-jorgen-to-speak-at-epicenter-conference-hosted-by-coin-telegraph/
**Tags:** Events, General, Neo Core, NEO

---

Neo ecosystem representatives are speaking at Coin Telegraph’s upcoming conference, Epicenter, focusing on Web 3.0 and cryptocurrency. Neo co-founder Da Hongfei released a video ahead of the event inviting Neo community members to attend in person or watch the free live stream.

Epicenter will be held in Lisbon, Portugal, and begins at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Saturday, Nov. 6. Neo is a Titled Sponsor of the event.

Da is scheduled to deliver an opening talk, while Neo Global Development Zürich lead, Lili Zhao, will deliver a keynote speech on Neo N3. Additionally, Flamingo Finance lead Odd Jørgen Røland will participate on a panel alongside Zhao. The speakers will take the stage according to the agenda:

- 1:10 p.m. – Da Hongfei opening talk: Neo N3: Building Blocks to The Web 3.0.

- 2:40 p.m. – Lili Zhao keynote speech: N3 Empowering The Smart Economy – A Journey of Faith and Vision.

- 3:00 p.m. – Lili Zhao and Odd Jørgen Røland panel: The crypto landscape and adoption in Asia and Europe.
