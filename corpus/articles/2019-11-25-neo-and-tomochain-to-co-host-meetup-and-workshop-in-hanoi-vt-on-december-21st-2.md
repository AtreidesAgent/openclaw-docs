# Neo and TomoChain to co-host meetup and workshop in Hanoi, VT on December 21st

**Published:** Mon, 25 Nov 2019 23:30:26 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-and-tomochain-to-co-host-meetup-and-workshop-in-hanoi-vt-on-december-21st/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

Neo and TomoChain have announced a joint meetup and workshop that will take place on Saturday, December 21st, 2019, in Hanoi, Vietnam. The meetup and workshop announcement was made in the days following the announcement of a strategic partnership between the two public blockchain providers. The purpose of the co-hosted event is to “embrace the different technologies” and connect local communities with ecosystem projects.

The day-long event will have two tracts. In the first half of the day, attendees will learn about the Neo EcoBoost program, as well as various gaming projects in the Neo ecosystem. The second half of the day intends to focus on a “deep dive into the smart contract development” from Neo and TomoChain developers.

In addition to TomoChain, Neo ecosystem partners to present at the event include MixMarvel and NeoWorld.

Neo and TomoChain will provide finger food and beverages for event attendees during the coffee breaks.

Additionally, there will be “surprise gifts,” and the first 50 guests of the workshop will receive NEO & TOMO tokens at the equivalent value of US $10.
