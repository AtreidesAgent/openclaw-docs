# NEO participating in Cointime Summit and community meetup in Ho Chi Minh City – July 25th

**Published:** Fri, 05 Jul 2019 23:35:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-participating-in-cointime-summit-and-community-meetup-in-ho-chi-minh-city-july-25th/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO Global Development (NGD) is participating at the Cointime Summit taking place in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam, on Thursday, July 25th. Following the event, NGD will hold a community meetup to discuss NEO3 and the NEO EcoBoost program.

Cointime Summit

The Cointime Summit will take place on July 25th and 26th at the Hotel Equatorial Ho Chi Minh City. It is co-hosted by Jinse Finance, a finance media platform, and The Blockchainer, a blockchain startup service provider. The event aims to bring together major exchanges in Southeast Asia, key opinion leaders, and developers to offer in-depth discussion on the future financial landscapes, technological advancements, and more.

John Wang, NGD head of ecosystem growth, will deliver a speech on NEO’s US $100 million EcoBoost Initiative. Other companies participating in the conference include Facebook, Coinbase, COSMOS, Ripple, Binance, Dash, Ledger, IBM, and more. NEO will host one of the 40+ booths at the event.

General admission is free. VIP tickets are $800 and will provide attendees with access to an investor VIP dinner.

Community Meetup

Following NEO’s presentation at the Cointime Summit, NGD is co-hosting a community meetup with LagomX, a blockchain marketing company, and Coin68, an independent decentralized ledger technology publication.

The meetup will take place at the same location as the Cointime Summit, and Wang will speak more about the NEO EcoBoost Initiative. Additionally, NGD head of marketing Yuan Gao will deliver a presentation on NEO and the tentative Q2 2020 launch of NEO3.

Agenda and Registration Details

Agenda

2:30 pm – Welcome note by LagomX and Coin68

2:40 pm – About NEO & NEO3 by Yuan Gao

2:55 pm – NEO EcoBoost by John Wang

3:10 pm – Q&A and Networking
