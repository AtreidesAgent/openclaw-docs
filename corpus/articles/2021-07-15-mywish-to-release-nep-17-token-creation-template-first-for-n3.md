# MyWish to release NEP-17 token creation template, first for N3

**Published:** Thu, 15 Jul 2021 22:39:52 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/mywish-to-release-nep-17-token-creation-template-first-for-n3/
**Tags:** Development, General, GAS, NEO

---

MyWish is integrating Neo N3 TestNet support into its smart contract generation platform. The first template release is for creating NEP-17 tokens, which will allow users to launch a token on N3.

MyWish is a service that offers its users tools to easily create, deploy, and manage smart contracts on multiple blockchains. Its website states the platform has approximately 39,000 users and launched more than 27,000 smart contracts.

At the time of press, MyWish supports Neo, Ethereum, Polygon, Tron, Binance Smart Chain, and other blockchain networks. It offers smart contract templates for public token sales, airdrops, investment pools, and many other uses.

MyWish has been participating in the Neo ecosystem since 2018, first implementing neo-python support on its smart contract platform. In addition to smart contract templates for the Neo blockchain, MyWish also manages a GAS distribution faucet for Neo Legacy and Neo N3 TestNets.

MyWish intends to integrate N3 TestNet support on July 16, 2021.
