# Da Hongfei to join Rachel Wolfson for X Live AMA on March 3

**Published:** Tue, 03 Mar 2026 20:43:30 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/da-hongfei-to-join-rachel-wolfson-for-x-live-ama-on-march-3/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo co-founder Da Hongfei is scheduled to participate in an AMA hosted by journalist Rachel Wolfson, creator of the Web3 Deep Dive. The session will take place via X Live at 2:00 a.m. (UTC) on Wed., March 4.

Wolfson framed the AMA as an opportunity to “dig in” to developments around Neo, stating she has been following the project and wishes to discuss Neo’s first financial report since 2020, covering 12 years of operations, as well as strategic decisions believed to be underway.

The timing aligns with recent community attention on Neo Foundation transparency and treasury governance, following a public dispute between Neo’s founding figures that played out on X in late 2025.

Da has also publicly acknowledged the planned discussion, replying that he is “ready and looking forward to it.”

According to Wolfson’s comments, the conversation is expected to include both favorable and difficult topics, with a focus on financial reporting and broader strategic direction.
