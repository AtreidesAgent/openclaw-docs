# Red Pulse participating in three events on February 28th

**Published:** Sat, 24 Feb 2018 16:10:54 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/red-pulse-events-on-february-28/
**Tags:** Events, PHX

---

February 24, 2018 – Red Pulse will be participating in three events that will take place on February 28, 2018. Jonathan Ha, CEO, will be at a fireside chat in Tokyo, and Stanley Chao, co-founder of Red Pulse, will be attending the ITAS conference, and hosting a community meetup in Luxembourg. 

Red Pulse is Hong Kong-based company that aims to solve the issue of information overload, and connects outside analysts, industry experts, and academics to provide content to those who seek to learn about Chinese economic and capital markets.

Beyond Blocks Meetup in Tokyo

Jonathan Ha, CEO of Red Pulse, will be attending a meetup hosted by Beyond Blocks in Tokyo, Japan at 6:30pm on Wednesday, February 28, 2018. The meetup’s agenda has Jonathan participating in a Fireside Chat with moderator Gabriel Yang. 

The event is being held in the approach to the Beyond Blocks Summit, which will take place in Tokyo this April. Jonathan will be taking the stage for a second consecutive visit to the annual Beyond Blocks Summit. The meetup on February 28th will connect cryptocurrency and blockchain enthusiasts with one another, and provide an opportunity to interact with Jonathan.

Entry to the event is free, and tickets can be acquired at the Beyond Blocks Tokyo Meetup on Eventbrite.

Red Pulse at International Transfer Agency Summit (ITAS)

The ITAS conference is the second event Red Pulse will take part in on February 28th. Stanley Chao, co-founder of Red Pulse, will be moderating a panel entitled “Intelligent automation: Practical Implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI).” 

Stanley will be moderating a panel comprised of Christian Gillot, CEO of Tetrao, Hugo Larguinho Brás, Director of Pricewaterhouse Coopers, and Helmut Mach, CEO and Founder of ArpaFX GmbH, discussing the topic of AI and how businesses are implementing the technology.

The session is scheduled to take place from 2:45pm to 3:30pm. 

ITAS expects to host 150+ attendees, 50+ expert and industry leader speakers, comprised of transfer agents, asset managers, fund distributors, regulators, and financial technology innovators over the conference’s three day timeframe.

Tickets for the conference can be purchased on the ITAS booking page, and the agenda can be found here.

Community Meetup in Luxembourg

Following the ITAS session, Red Pulse will host their first ever community meetup in Luxembourg. 

Stanley Chao will meet with blockchain enthusiasts to discuss Red Pulse’s innovative tokenized research platform, company updates, progress on their roadmap, and provide the community an opportunity for questions and answers. There will also be a free RPX t-shirt giveaway.

The event will be taking place from 6:00pm to 10:00pm at the Luxembourg City Hotel. Those who are interested in attending the meetup can register for free at the Red Pulse Eventbrite page.

For more information about Red Pulse visit their website, token website, Twitter, Reddit, Discord, YouTube, Medium, or Telegram.
