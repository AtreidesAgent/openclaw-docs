# FindTruman holding creative writing competition for Neo Hostel arc of its whodunit-style game

**Published:** Thu, 12 May 2022 18:31:34 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/gaming/findtruman-holding-creative-writing-competition-for-neo-hostel-arc-of-its-whodunit-style-game/
**Tags:** Gaming, Metaverse, NFT, NUDES

---

The FindTruman game developers are hosting a creative writing campaign for a Neo-themed storyline. Interested individuals are encouraged to submit a creative storyline based on their experiences exploring the Neo Hostel. The deadline for submissions is 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on May 14, 2022.

FindTruman is a crowd-sourced, whodunit-style game, where players search for clues in a 3D hotel environment to find Truman, the mysterious killer. Players can vote on the plot’s direction by using FindTruman Genesis NFTs. Additional utility for the FTG NFTs includes staking, priority access to particular areas of the platform, and potential airdrops.

According to the FindTruman announcement on Twitter, each “reasonable” submission for the campaign is eligible for 300 points applicable to an FTG NFT auction and an equivalent of US $10 in NEP-17 NUDES tokens. The top two stories will receive a free FTG NFT and an equivalent $100 in NUDES. Additionally, the FindTruman team will decorate the Neo Hostel using cues from the two winning scripts.
