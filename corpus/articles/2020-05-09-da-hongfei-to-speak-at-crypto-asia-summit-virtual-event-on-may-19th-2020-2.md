# Da Hongfei to speak at Crypto Asia Summit virtual event on May 19th, 2020

**Published:** Sat, 09 May 2020 20:25:48 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/da-hongfei-to-speak-at-crypto-asia-summit-virtual-event-on-may-19th-2020/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

Neo co-founder, Da Hongfei, is scheduled to deliver a keynote speech at the Crypto Asia Summit (CAS) virtual conference on Wednesday, May 19th, 2020. Da is expected to speak from 9:00 am to 10:00 am (UTC +9).

General subject matter for CAS 2020 is anticipated to include a swath of metrics and tools that analysts use to research blockchain-based projects, decentralized finance on Bitcoin and Ethereum, and blockchain adoption across Asian markets.

According to ChainTalk, the organizers of the five-day virtual event, there will be more than 50 confirmed speakers, 100 media and platinum sponsors, and 5,000 attendees.

At the time of press, there is no cost to attend the event. Though, free tickets are limited, which conference hosts claim to have a value of US $497. The attendee limit is cut off at 1,500 participants.

Registration for the event can be found at the link below:

https://chaintalk.media/summit/
