# John deVadoss to deliver hour-long presentation at BlockConf Digital virtual conference on May 25th

**Published:** Thu, 21 May 2020 21:30:06 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/john-devadoss-to-deliver-hour-long-presentation-at-blockconf-digital-virtual-conference-on-may-25th/
**Tags:** Events, NEO, NGD Seattle

---

Neo Global Development (NGD) Seattle lead, John deVadoss, is scheduled to deliver a presentation at the BlockConf Digital virtual conference taking place on Tuesday, May 26th, 2020. deVadoss’ presentation entitled, Realizing the Smart Economy: A Standards-based Approach, will be held from 5:00 am to 6:00 am (UTC +8).

The primary sponsor for the virtual conference is Kaspersky, a multinational cybersecurity and anti-virus provider, alongside numerous other blockchain-based and financial technology companies. Speakers for the event will include representatives from custodial exchanges, blockchain platforms, venture capital firms, educational entities, startups, and more.

There are three packages for attending the event.

A free tier will allow access to the e-booths and e-stages that will host the presentations. The business package costs US $40, which offers access to e-booth chats, the ability to request business cards, workshops, and networking opportunities. The VIP package costs US $70 and allows a direct line of communication to ask questions of guest speakers, schedule private meetings, and access offline materials.

Registration for the event can be found at the link below:

https://www.blockconf.digital/#register
