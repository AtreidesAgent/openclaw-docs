# DogeRift founder joining Neo Live to discuss upcoming 2D and 3D game and token integration

**Published:** Wed, 06 Jul 2022 16:04:22 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/dogerift-founder-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-upcoming-2d-and-3d-game-and-token-integration/
**Tags:** Events, Gaming, NFT, DOGEF, DOGER

---

Neo Global Development will host Marko Sinko, the founder of DogeRift, on the next installment of Neo Live. Participants will be eligible to earn from a pool of an equivalent in US $300 of DOGER tokens and 10 Bored Doge Club NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 12:00 p.m. (UTC) on Friday, July 8, 2022.

DogeRift will be a P2E game incorporating non-fungible, governance, and utility tokens into its 2D and 3D gaming universe. Two of the GameFi platform’s tokens, DOGER and DOGEF, have already launched on N3 and are available via Flamingo’s reverse pools. The DogeRift game will offer two types of gameplay, player-versus-environment and player-versus-player, each with its own P2E mechanics. Users must first purchase a Doge character from the DogeRift marketplace in order to play.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
