# NGD announces winners of the Polaris Launchpad hackathon

**Published:** Mon, 30 May 2022 20:22:24 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/dao/ngd-announces-winners-of-the-polaris-launchpad-hackathon/
**Tags:** DAO, DeFi, Development, Gaming, Metaverse, NEO

---

Neo has announced the winners of the Polaris Launchpad Hackathon event, awarding prizes from a US $500,000 pool. Five grand prizes were available across five Polaris Launchpad categories, in addition to 14 excellence awards and one special mention award.

Each grand prize-winning project will receive $35,000 in NEO tokens. Further, each winning team will receive a custom-tailored set of post-hackathon incubation opportunities through participation in the Neo Global Development EcoBoost program. EcoBoost is designed to enhance the Neo ecosystem by offering end-to-end lifecycle support for small and medium-sized teams who develop on or are looking to integrate with or migrate to the Neo blockchain.

Excellence and special award winners will receive $8,000 in NEO tokens and customized consultation time with the NGD EcoGrowth team regarding their projects.

All winning projects that received a multiplier award during the Planathon phase will also have their prizes multiplied by a factor ranging from 1.1 to 1.5x.

The third and final phase of the Polaris Launchpad, Polaris Plus, begins June 8 and runs through June 21. This portion of the event will take place on the DoraHacks platform. The Neo community will contribute GAS to their favorite projects which Neo will then match through quadratic funding from a minimum $57,500 pool.

Quadratic funding encourages users to fund proposals by amplifying smaller donations made by the general community. The amount of GAS each user donates is then matched from a grant pool, equal to the square of the sum of the square roots of contributions received minus the actual contribution.

Best DeFi Project

Pumpkin Swap

Demo Video

Pumpkin Swap is a Uniswap-like DEX that allows users to create liquidity pools, provide liquidity, and launch an LP with minimal fees. The platform will also enable users to stake LP tokens on Flamingo and potentially non-custodial exchanges on other blockchains. The platform offers swapping, pooling, and other utility (i.e., create NEP-17 token, create LP, lock/unlock liquidity).

Best Metaverse and NFT Project

Demo Video

Dogerift is a GameFi app built on Neo. Players use their NFTs as playable characters to earn tokens that can be used in-game to improve a player’s gameplay or be traded for profit. Players use their NEO wallets as game accounts to log in to the game and can use NFTs as playable characters and items.

Best DAO Project

NeoDao

Demo Video

NeoDao is a toolkit and client-side Javascript library. The toolkit offers a package consisting of the typical operations a blockchain requires, reducing the necessity for a developer’s expertise in blockchain programming.
