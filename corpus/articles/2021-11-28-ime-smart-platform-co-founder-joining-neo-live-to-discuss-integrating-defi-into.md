# iMe Smart Platform co-founder joining Neo Live to discuss integrating DeFi into messaging application

**Published:** Sun, 28 Nov 2021 13:00:15 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/ime-smart-platform-co-founder-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-integrating-defi-into-messaging-application/
**Tags:** DeFi, Events, General, IME, LIME, NEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host wallet and translation platform, iMe, on the next installment of Neo Live. Alex Borutskiy, iMe co-founder, is joining the AMA to discuss the messaging application and integrating DeFi features. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn LIME tokens from a pool of US $300 in equivalent value.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 10:00 a.m. (UTC) on Monday, Nov. 29, 2021.

iMe is a messaging platform powered by the Telegram API, which incorporates blockchain-enabled features. The iMe messenger has a built-in language translator and offers support for “neurobots” – personal chat assistants trained to provide users with quick and relevant replies to questions. The application also includes a wallet that supports multiple cryptocurrencies on Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain. The iMe app is available for iOS and Android. Additionally, iMe was recently accepted into the Early Adoption Program. 

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
