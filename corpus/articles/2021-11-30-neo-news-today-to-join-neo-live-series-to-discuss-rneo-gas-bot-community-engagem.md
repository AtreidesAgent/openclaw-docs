# Neo News Today to join Neo Live series to discuss, r/NEO gas bot, community engagement

**Published:** Tue, 30 Nov 2021 23:24:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-news-today-to-join-neo-live-series-to-discuss-r-neo-gas-bot-community-engagement/
**Tags:** Events, General, NNT, GAS, NEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host Neo News Today on the next installment of Neo Live. NNT founder, Dean Jeffs, and editors, Dylan Grabowski and Brett Rhodes, are joining the AMA to discuss disseminating ecosystem information, the r/NEO GasBot, and how others can get involved. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn GAS from a pool of US $200 in equivalent value.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 3:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Dec. 2, 2021.

Neo News Today was founded in 2017 to serve as an objective source of information for dApps, developers, and events in the ecosystem. Additionally, NNT is among the initial 21 members of the Neo Council, which is tasked with maintaining the network’s health and liveness. Since its founding, NNT has covered the Neo ecosystem by way of articles, videos, and podcasts, while also creating the r/NEO tipping bot, redeveloping nDapp.org, and distributing grants from its council node income.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
