# Neo to host AMA alongside Blockchain-based Service Network and Poly Network on November 13th

**Published:** Wed, 11 Nov 2020 23:40:28 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-to-host-ama-alongside-blockchain-based-service-network-and-poly-enterprise-on-november-13th/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development Ecosystem Growth director, John Wang, will participate in an Ask Me Anything alongside Poly Network and Blockchain-based Service Network representatives. The hour-long AMA will occur on Neo’s Official Telegram channel at 2:00 pm (UTC+8) on Friday, November 13th, 2020.

BSN Chief Architect, Henry Ma, and Poly Enterprise tech lead, Tan Yuan, will take part in the AMA alongside Wang. Participants intend to discuss Poly Enterprise’s recent integration into BSN China and how interoperability can accelerate dApp development and mass adoption. Poly Enterprise is an enterprise version of Poly Network.

The BSN is a cross-cloud, cross-portal, cross-framework global public infrastructure network used to deploy and operate blockchain applications. BSN China is a subsidiary of the BSN with features that allow it to remain compliant with Chinese blockchain regulations.

During the AMA, there will be a sharing session from the participants followed by a live question and answer discussion.
