## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 43 is the most Neo-focused CC&BB of the year, covering Ndapp and GasBot's 2024 progress (wallet connectivity, Glida swaps, gas claiming), the Enclave account abstraction wallet's Q1-Q2 2025 roadmap, reactivating legacy NEO holders through tools like Glida, BattleHard's NFT upcycling and vendor contract as unique-to-Neo innovations, and multiple community giveaway campaigns happening in the ecosystem.

### Topics Covered
- Dylan joining the Ndapp brainstorm meeting for the first time — vision for Ndapp as "the front page for Neo"
- Meeting Lopez, the full-time developer behind Ndapp and GasBot
- Ndapp 2024 highlights: wallet connectivity, gas claiming, vote changing, Glida-powered swaps
- GasBot: new tokens added, exploring NeoX integration
- Glida as a tool for autonomy — enabling legacy migrators to get gas without begging
- Enclave wallet roadmap: Q1-Q2 2025 launch with biometric login, DAPI, sponsored gas operations
- Enclave's killer feature: sponsoring gas claims from unclaimed gas (no need to break NEO)
- BattleHard: NFT upcycling unique to Neo; vendor contract for token sales without DEX/AMM
- Three contracts from BattleHard live on mainnet
- Community giveaways: NNT's 100 gas giveaway (requires NeoNS name), Neo's Naughty or Nice game theory campaign (400 gas pool), Neo Dashboard's advent campaign (ND meme tokens), BattleHard's B token giveaway
- Neo approaching 10th anniversary (Antshares mainnet live in 2015)
- The importance of not LARPing NeoNS names — "Dylan's checking"
