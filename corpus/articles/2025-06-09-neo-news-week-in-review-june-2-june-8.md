General Updates

Neo Core developers unanimously approved adding the stateRoot into the block header, a debate that has been going on amongst Neo Core dating back to 2019. In blockchain networks, the stateRoot in a block header is a cryptographic hash representing the entire state of the blockchain at a particular point in time (specifically, after all transactions in the block have been executed). It is a compact, tamper-evident fingerprint of the blockchain’s current state. It plays a crucial role in ensuring consistency, security, and trustless verification across the network. The Neo Core developers will draft a formal proposal and share it with the Technical Committee and Neo Council.

SpoonOS announced it will attend the SuperAI conference and that the project is a Gold Sponsor of the event. The conference will take place on June 18 and 19 at the Marina Bay Sands Hotel in Singapore.

ITEM Systems kicked off an AMA on Reddit to open a dialogue with the community about its 2025 roadmap and the emerging physical asset trends in the Web3 space.

COZ announced it will be a gold sponsor at Blockchain.RIO, a premiere crypto conference in Latin America that anticipates more than 18,000 attendees.

nDapp re-integrated swapping support on the app and dApp discovery platform. The swapping feature was temporarily removed after Flamingo Finance migrated from the AMM model to the on-chain order book.

Flamingo Finance noted Epoch 9 concluded, that the total payout was US $9,091.78, and 304,585 FLM were burned.

NeoUltimateShop integrated a new payments solution that allows shoppers to pay for goods with PayPal and every token listed on Coinbase, in addition to the GAS that users could already use for payments. Additionally, the team is running a campaign until June 28, during which five shoppers who have placed an order will win a prize for a mystery mug and a metal card to securely store their seedphrase.

Neo Smart Economy Vietnam announced a mobile legends tournament exclusively for Neo brand ambassadors, with up to 50 GAS in rewards. The campaign is currently live and concludes on June 14.

NeoRedPill hosted a Telegram quiz event that offered participants GAS rewards.

NeoDashboard burned 283,884 NDMEME tokens, which was the third burning event in as many weeks.

Frank Coin hosted a GasBot trivia contest on the FrankCoin channel of the NeoF1 Discord server and distributed 3 GAS in rewards to participants.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released its monthly updates for April and May, which touched upon NeoGo v0.109.1, NeoFS v0.46.1, NeoFS MainNet, S3 Gateway v0.38, and REST gateway v0.12.

AxLabs released Neow3j v3.24.0, which is compatible with Neo v3.8 and includes a range of significant updates. The release includes multiple breaking changes, feature additions, and internal improvements.

NNT Catch Up

NNT released Episode 91 of The Smart Economy Podcast featuring Ryan Galvankar, the founder of Plaza Finance, a platform built on Base specializing in programmable derivatives. Topics of discussion included how traditional finance expertise shaped Plaza’s approach to building decentralized derivatives, why Base was chosen as the L2 solution for Plaza’s products, the mechanics behind bondETH’s stable 10% annual yield through quarterly coupon payments, how levETH achieves “leverage without liquidation” through self-balancing protocols, why institutional adoption of Ethereum continues despite market sentiment fluctuations, and more.

NNT hosted Crypto Coffee and Blockchain Beer Spaces #63 on the official The Smart Economy Podcast X account. Topics of discussion included compounding progress of small-scale activities in the Neo ecosystem, COZ and SpoonOS as Gold Sponsors for major events, NeoUltimateShop adding PayPal support and launching a reward campaign, NDMEME and FLM token burns this week, and Neo Core unanimously voted in stateRoot in Header.
