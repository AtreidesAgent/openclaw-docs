# Spotcoin token distribution to begin December 12th

**Published:** Mon, 10 Dec 2018 22:53:08 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/spotcoin-token-distribution-to-begin-december-12th/
**Tags:** General, SPOT

---

Spotcoin has announced that its SPOT token distribution will begin on Wednesday, December 12th. The distribution has been approved to move forward following the sign off of a third-party financial report and conclusion of the token swap. SPOT tokens will be delivered to the following groups accordingly:

- Pre token sale participants,

- Private placement participants,

- Public token sale participants,

- Airdrop/referral participants, then

- Spotcoin team member.

The Spotcoin team advises members to pay attention to its Medium channel for future updates.
