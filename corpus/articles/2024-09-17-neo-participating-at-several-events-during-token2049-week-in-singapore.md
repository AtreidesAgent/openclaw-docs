# Neo participating at several events during TOKEN2049 week in Singapore

**Published:** Tue, 17 Sep 2024 04:30:30 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-participating-at-several-events-during-token2049-week-in-singapore/
**Tags:** Events, General, BlockSpirits, COZ, IntersectFinance, NeoX, TOKEN2049

---

Neo will participate in multiple events leading up to and at the TOKEN2049 conference in Singapore. Alongside co-hosting side events, Neo will continue its tradition of hosting non-fungible item-enabled activations, continuing the BlockSpirits game which was a key feature of the WebX 2024 booth in Tokyo, Japan.

On Sep. 17, Neo’s Head of Eco Growth John Wang is participating in a panel entitled Advancing Public Blockchain Infrastructure at the Innoblock 2024 event. Innoblock 2024 is a two-day Web3 innovation festival focused on the evolution of dApps and blockchain-enabled applications. The event is hosting over 100 speakers, 50 exhibitors, and 5,000 registered guests. Neo and representatives from BNB Chain, Soneium, and Kaia will participate on the panel.

That same night, Neo is co-hosting a side event in collaboration with SevenX Ventures. The event will kick off with a VIP dinner, followed by a networking session of Web3 venture capitalists, communities, media, and emerging projects from the Neo X ecosystem such as Intersect Finance. The dinner is invite-only, but the networking event is open to all and will run from 9:00 p.m. to 11:30 p.m.

On Sep. 18 and 19, Neo will be a platinum sponsor for TOKEN2049 and run a booth at the conference, where the second BlockSpirits activation will occur. BlockSpirits is an NFI-enabled game built by COZ that allows visitors to obtain a physical card, tap-scan it, and unlock an elemental spirit character. Once the character is unlocked, the cardholder can battle their spirits against other players. There will be a series of prizes for users who accumulate the most battle points, including a PlayStation 5, Nintendo Switch, Marshall mini-speaker, and a drone camera.

Lastly, on Sep. 18, Neo is co-hosting a coffee chat alongside PumpBTC, Smrti Lab, Istari Venture, Free, and Meson. The conversations will focus on the DeFi ecosystem, with guests from each of the represented entities speaking at different time slots between 2:00 p.m. and 5:00 p.m. The event will take place at the PS.Cafe, located in the Marina Bay Sands hotel, where TOKEN2049 is located.
