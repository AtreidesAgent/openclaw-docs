# Neo Christmas campaign releases limited edition Blockchain Cutie and 0xWarriors game items

**Published:** Fri, 20 Dec 2019 20:44:17 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-christmas-campaign-releases-limited-edition-blockchain-cutie-and-0xwarriors-game-items/
**Tags:** Gaming, General, 0xWarriors, BlockchainCuties, NEO

---

Neo has announced a Christmas campaign giving the community an opportunity to win limited edition game items in collaboration with ecosystem partners. 0xWarriors and Blockchain Cuties have been revealed as the first two participating games, with more to be announced in the coming days.

To be eligible to win, participants must follow @NEO_Blockchain on Twitter and retweet the appropriate tweets. Winners will be announced on Christmas Eve.

Blockchain Cuties

Blockchain Cuties, a multi-blockchain collectible adventure game, announced plans to integrate with the Neo ecosystem in June 2019. In August, the team released the first four Neo-based cuties that were designed after four members of the Neo ecosystem.

Blockchain Cuties is contributing limited edition Christmas themed cutie characters featuring a Neo green Santa outfit as seen in the tweet below.

 Join the celebration: follow us and RT to win a limited edition cutie from @BlockchainCutie. 

Stay tuned for more #NeoXmas… pic.twitter.com/KtyXCuMo14

— Neo Smart Economy (@Neo_Blockchain) December 19, 2019

 

0xWarriors

0xGames, a blockchain game development studio, announced plans to integrate its first game, 0xWarriors, with the Neo blockchain in September 2019. 0xWarriors is a multiplayer game where players can manage a team of up to five warriors, which they can equip with various types of weaponry and armor.

0xWarriors is contributing Nephrite chests to the Christmas giveaway, each guaranteed to contain one Epic level item.

Follow us and RT to get a Nephrite chest in @0xWarriors! 

All winners to be picked on X’mas Eve and stay tuned for more… #NeoXmas pic.twitter.com/RSULW469Yj

More giveaways will be revealed on the Neo Smart Economy Twitter in the coming days.
