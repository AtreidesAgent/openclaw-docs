# Upcoming NEO Colorado meetup to provide NEO DevCon highlights

**Published:** Wed, 20 Feb 2019 21:07:58 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/upcoming-neo-colorado-meetup-to-provide-neo-devcon-highlights/
**Tags:** Events, NEO

---

NEO Colorado will be hosting its third meetup on Monday, February 25th. The meetup will include a presentation offering highlights from the recent NEO DevCon that took place in Seattle, US on February 16th and 17th.

The local event will cover announcements and updates on Da Hongfei’s and NEO leadership presentations, further information about NEO 3.0, the launch of a NEO Global Development (NGD) office in Seattle, community initiatives, project updates, plus more.

NEO Colorado’s previous meetup offered a presentation on NEO’s path to network decentralization, previously covered by NEO News Today. The local community hosted its first meetup in November of 2018 and conducted a Community Lightning Talk the following month.

For those who will be unable to attend, a video and audio stream will be made available following the event.

Time and Venue

Time: 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm (MST)

Date: Monday, February 25th, 2019

Location: Enterprise Coworking

3000 Lawrence Street

Denver, CO 80205

Registration

Registration for the NEO Colorado meetup is free.

Individuals can register for the meetup via the following link:

https://www.meetup.com/NEO-Colorado/events/258614075/
