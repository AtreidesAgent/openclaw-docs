# Travala announce airdrop of 250,000 AVA to SWH holders

**Published:** Sat, 12 May 2018 20:29:19 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/travala-airdrop-switcheo/
**Tags:** General, AVA

---

On May 10, 2018, Travala announced plans to airdrop AVA tokens through a partnership with Switcheo. Travala is an online travel booking platform built on top of the NEO platform, that aims to “become the first decentralized global travel marketplace that will be a one-stop shop for all travel related services.”

Travala will distribute 250,000 AVA tokens to NEO wallets that hold a minimum of 2,000 Switcheo (SWH) tokens. The 250,000 AVA tokens will be divided up equally amongst qualifying wallets. For example, if 5,000 wallets hold the required amount of SWH, then each user will receive 50 AVA tokens in the airdrop (250,000 / 5,000 = 50).

The snapshot for the airdrop and distribution of tokens will take place on Friday, May 18, 2018. The specific time/block of the snapshot is still to be announced. 

Distribution of the airdrop will begin shortly after the snapshot occurs, and could take a few days to fully dole out AVA tokens. 

AVA tokens will be listed on the Switcheo DEX on Tuesday, May 22, 2018.
