# #NeoIsHere community contest deadline extended, scope expanded, and prize pool doubled

**Published:** Wed, 30 Jun 2021 11:31:53 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neoishere-community-contest-deadline-extended-scope-expanded-and-prize-pool-doubled/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is extending the #NeoIsHere competition by two weeks and expanding the scope beyond just videos. Additionally, the Neo community will vote on the submissions and be eligible to receive rewards for participating.

The #NeoIsHere campaign aims to raise awareness of the forthcoming Neo N3 MainNet launch. Initially, participants could only submit a 30-second or less video, but can now put forth GIFs, images, digital art, or other visual content.

NGD requires submissions to include elements reflecting Neo’s branding (i.e., logo, slogans, features) and emphasize the #NeoIsHere theme. The content must have English subtitles if produced in another language.

The prize pool will also expand to offer approximately US $7,000 in rewards. The rewards will be shared among the Top 11 submissions and 10 voters chosen randomly. The prize distribution can be found below:

- 1st place: $1,500 in NEO

- 2nd to 4th place: $800 in NEO each

- 5th to 11th place: $400 in NEO each

- 10 Community Voters: $70 in NEO each

Contest participants must register their submission via Google Form and Tweet the content with the hashtag #NeoIsHere. Individuals who have already submitted a video for the competition are automatically registered for the extended deadline.

Final submissions are due by 8:00 a.m. (UTC) on July 15, 2021.

The Neo community will vote on submitted pieces of content from July 16 through July 19. NGD will announce the competition winners and randomly chosen voters by the end of the month.
