# Custodial exchange aggregator SwapSpace supports NEO and GAS

**Published:** Sun, 11 Jul 2021 17:14:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/custodial-exchange-aggregator-swapspace-supports-neo-and-gas/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, GAS, NEO

---

SwapSpace has added NEO and GAS, allowing users to trade these tokens with more than 450 other cryptocurrencies already on the platform. Traders can now exchange NEO and GAS without volume limits or additional fees, and aren’t required to register or provide KYC/AML information.

SwapSpace is a non-custodial cryptocurrency exchange aggregator that collects rates across various custodial trading platforms (i.e., ChangeNOW, Changelly, Binance) and offers them in one location. To conduct a token swap, users must provide a private wallet address for both cryptocurrencies they wish to trade between.

There is no limit to the amount of tokens users can swap on the platform, but users must pay the network transaction fees associated with the various blockchains. SwapSpace doesn’t charge a trading fee beyond network fees, but instead shares commissions with the exchange providers.

At the time of press, there is no on- or off-ramp available to convert to or from fiat currencies.

Looking forward, SwapSpace will integrate support for Neo N3 at the same rate as its partner custodial exchanges. The team said, “SwapSpace works on an aggregator model, new assets must go through our exchange partners first to appear on our website.” Once N3 NEO lists on exchanges, SwapSpace support will be automatic.
