# Hibit integrates support for Neo X into its cross-chain non-custodial exchange

**Published:** Wed, 12 Mar 2025 04:11:46 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/hibit-integrates-support-for-neo-x-into-its-cross-chain-non-custodial-exchange/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, HIBIT, NeoX

---

Hibit has integrated support for the Neo X EVM sidechain into its non-custodial exchange, enabling trading and permissionless token listings for Neo X assets. Additionally, users can participate in a DecentraMind community campaign to compete for rewards.

Hibit is an L1 public blockchain designed for omni-chain interoperability, initially launched within the TON blockchain ecosystem. Its architecture supports networks such as Ethereum, Sui, Bitcoin, NEAR, Avalanche, Cosmos, and Polkadot. Hibit aims to redefine DeFi by offering effortless, secure, and cost-efficient trading, integrating AI-driven automation with DeFi.

The Hibit non-custodial exchange allows any entity to list tokens on the platform and offers an advanced suite of trading and payment features across various ecosystems. Its hybrid consensus mechanism seeks to provide frictionless cross-chain transactions and provides zero gas fees for all asset transactions.

DecentraMind Community Rewards Campaign

Hibit is conducting a community rewards campaign through its social distribution platform, DecentraMind. DecentraMind is a Web3 contribution market aimed at assisting projects in building better communities through a progressive ownership model, which proposes a fairer and more efficient community token distribution method compared to traditional airdrops. This model allocates community tokens to genuine contributors rather than to spam bots, seeking to foster stronger and more vibrant communities.

To participate in the campaign, users must quote the announcement link with a comment, include their quest invitation link in the comment, and submit the URL of their quoted post. The reward pool for participation includes 200 HIBIT tokens.

The campaign is currently live and runs through March 21.
