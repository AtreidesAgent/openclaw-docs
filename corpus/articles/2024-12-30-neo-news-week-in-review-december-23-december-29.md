# Neo News: Week in Review – December 23 – December 29

**Published:** Mon, 30 Dec 2024 11:18:32 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/week-in-review/neo-news-week-in-review-december-23-december-29/
**Tags:** Week In Review, B, COZ, FRANK, KERMIT, Meme2Earn, Nash, NDMEME, NeoSPCC, NEP, NUDES

General Updates

Neo co-hosted a Web3 Christmas party in Japan in collaboration with OKCoin Japan, Tangem, and Crypto Times, and shared a video of the event.

COZ unveiled native cross-chain swap functionality in the Neon Wallet, enhancing the user experience by enabling in-wallet token swaps across blockchain protocols. The new feature is powered by SimpleSwap, a platform that has been operating since 2018 and provides access to over 1,000 cryptocurrency and fiat currencies. In addition to the swap functionality, COZ introduced a new skin feature in Neon Wallet. Currently, users who hold a Naughty or Nice GIFT from Neo or a PUPPET from COZ can access exclusive skins. Additionally, COZ hosted an AMA on the Neo subReddit to provide answers about the recently integrated cross-chain swapping feature for Neon Wallet.

Battle Hard concluded the 1,000 B token giveaway and notified the Top 10 ranked users of their pending rewards.

Meme2Earn hosted a Community Fireplace during which the team announced plans to burn 69.42% of the total NUDES token supply, and the Meme of the Day contest will be postponed throughout January 2025 to resolve bugs and issues on the meme-based platform.

Nash launched the Nash Debit Card, a new feature that enables users to spend crypto or EUR directly from their Nash account. To access the Nash Debit Card, users need to have an IBAN account set up within the Nash app. The introduction of the Nash Debit Card adds a new layer to this offering, allowing users to make everyday payments with their Nash accounts. The card also features a crypto round-up function, which automatically invests spare change from purchases into cryptocurrency, helping users build their portfolios.

NeoDashboard concluded its Advent Calendar Giveaway with day #24. COZ joined the Neo Dashboard NDMEME Advent Calendar Event to increase the Christmas Day prize pool for participants to win up to 100,000 NDMEME tokens.

Frank Coin concluded the holiday token giveaway event and distributed GAS, NDMEME, NEP, and KERMIT tokens to six campaign participants.

In Case You Missed It

Neo SPCC released several updates across its core products and services, including upgrades for key NeoFS components and a NeoGo patch release to fix errors with NeoFS block fetching.
