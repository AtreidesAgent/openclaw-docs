Dylan speaks with William Song, founder of Lyrebird. Discussion topics include Terra’s collapse and how it changed William’s perception of “defending the peg,” how Lyrebird will adapt and potentially move forward with a fractionally backed stablecoin, the importance of using yield-generating assets for collateral in the Bowerbird lending protocol, using Neo’s native oracle network, and more!
