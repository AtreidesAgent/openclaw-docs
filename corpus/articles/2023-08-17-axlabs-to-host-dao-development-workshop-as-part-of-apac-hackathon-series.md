# AxLabs to host DAO development workshop as part of APAC Hackathon series

**Published:** Thu, 17 Aug 2023 01:46:36 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/axlabs-to-host-dao-development-workshop-as-part-of-apac-hackathon-series/
**Tags:** DAO, Development, Events, APAC Hackathon, AxLabs

---

Neo Global Development has scheduled a development workshop focusing on building with DAOs. The DAO building workshop will begin at 9:00 a.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Aug. 17, and will be hosted on Neo’s Official YouTube Channel. The event, titled “BUIDL DAO with AxLabs,” will be hosted by AxLabs software engineer Claude Muller.  AxLabs maintains Neo’s Neow3j tooling suite, which offers tools to build dApps and smart contracts.

The session will guide attendees through concepts, tooling, and smart contracts for building DAOs. As with the previous workshops, this event is not exclusive to hackathon competitors, and anyone interested is welcome to join in the learning experience.

The Neo APAC Hackathon

The Neo APAC Hackathon is an initiative designed to foster Web3 growth in the Asia-Pacific region. Running from July to October 2023, this event offers over $1,100,000 in prizes and grant funding. With virtual sessions, on-the-ground “GAS Stations” across five major cities, and a grand finale in Hong Kong, the Hackathon seeks to attract innovative Web3 startups to the Neo ecosystem.
