# TOTHEMOON to distribute Moon Device NFTs to qualified wallets after the Waxing Crescent mint

**Published:** Fri, 29 Apr 2022 20:48:14 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/airdrop/tothemoon-to-distribute-moon-device-nfts-to-qualified-wallets-after-the-waxing-crescent-mint/
**Tags:** Airdrop, Gaming, NFT, GAS, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON is releasing Moon Devices, a collection of NFTs which will be airdropped after the upcoming Waxing Crescent Character type NFT mint. The Moon Devices will unlock unique free mints of forthcoming special TTM NFT collections.

There are eight TTM Moon Phases in Cycle 1, which are minting events for Character type NFTs. TTM minted the first round of Character type NFTs in Dec. 2021. All Moon Baby and Moon Trainer NFTs were sold within the first minute after release, and all Cryptonaut NFTs sold within the first six hours. Moon Creature NFTs also sold out.

In the Waxing Crescent phase, the TTM team will release 50 new Cryptonauts, four new Moon Trainers, four new Moon Babies, and the second-generation Moon Creatures. At the beginning of the Waxing Crescent phase, Moon Creature NFT holders will also receive a randomized second-gen Moon Creature for each first-gen creature held.

The Waxing Crescent mint is the next step in TTM’s roadmap. Afterward, the team will announce more information about the snapshot for the Moon Device airdrop.

First Moon Device Airdrop

There are two ways to qualify for the Moon Device airdrop. First, a Neo wallet must hold a Moon Duo NFT. One hundred twenty Moon Duo NFTs were purchased through auction in Feb. 2022 and comprise two character types offering both Moon and Cryptonaut Points.

The second method is by pairing multi-generation Cryptonauts and their Origin traits. There are seven (i.e., all the continents), but only five pairs necessary to become eligible. To qualify for one Moon Device NFT, a wallet must hold both a New Moon and Waxing Crescent Cryptonaut NFT, and each pair must share one of the seven Origins. Five Origins need to be accounted for, therefore, the qualified wallet will have at least 10 Cryptonaut NFTs.

There is a total supply of 5,000 Moon Device NFTs, which will be distributed over seven events throughout Cycle 1. Any that are not distributed via airdrop will be put up for auction on the TTM Marketplace. The proceeds from the potential sale will be split equally amongst wallets holding a Moon Device NFT.
