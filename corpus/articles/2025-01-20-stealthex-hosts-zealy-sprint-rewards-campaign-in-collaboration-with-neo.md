# StealthEX hosts Zealy sprint rewards campaign in collaboration with Neo

**Published:** Mon, 20 Jan 2025 01:29:38 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/stealthex-hosts-zealy-sprint-rewards-campaign-in-collaboration-with-neo/
**Tags:** Events, General, StealthEX

---

StealthEX has launched a Zealy rewards campaign in collaboration with Neo. The initiative, which began on Jan. 16 and will run through Feb. 18, invites participants to complete various tasks for a chance to win a share of a 600 USDT prize pool.

StealthEX is a non-custodial exchange that offers competitive cryptocurrency swap rates, supports more than 1,500 digital assets, and operates without requiring registration.

The campaign features 17 tasks spread across different categories, such as interacting with Neo and StealthEX social channels, using the exchange’s platform, and more. Points earned from completing tasks will determine participants’ leaderboard rankings.

The top 20 leaderboard positions will receive cash prizes distributed as follows:

- 1st place: $200

- 2nd place: $100

- 3rd – 5th places: $50 each

- 6th – 20th places: $10 each

At the time of publication, 111 participants have entered the campaign.
