# Alchemy Pay CEO joining Neo Live to discuss fiat gateway and payments services, ACH rewards for participants

**Published:** Tue, 16 Nov 2021 18:22:29 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/alchemy-pay-ceo-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-fiat-gateway-and-payments-services-ach-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, ACH, NEO, Neo Live

---

Neo Global Development will host hybrid crypto-fiat gateway and payments services provider, Alchemy Pay, on the next installment of Neo Live. John Tan, Alchemy Pay CEO, is joining the AMA to discuss integrating NEO and using the cryptocurrency for global payments. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn ACH tokens from a pool of US $200 in equivalent value.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, Nov. 17, 2021.

Alchemy Pay was founded in 2018 and is a cryptocurrency and fiat payment system used by more than 3,000 physical businesses across 65 countries. The Alchemy Pay platform offers payment services and products for both merchants and shoppers. For merchants, the platform integrates with e-commerce websites and also offers a point-of-sale system. For shoppers, Alchemy Pay offers a mobile application and a physical debit card to pay for goods and services in cryptocurrency. Looking forward, NGD and Alchemy Pay will explore the possibility of establishing a joint ecosystem fund to support DeFi and payment-related projects building on the Neo N3 blockchain.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event held on the official Neo Telegram channel. It seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
