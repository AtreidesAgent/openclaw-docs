# SOMNIUMWAVE founder joining Neo Live to discuss 3D metaverse world and NFT integration

**Published:** Tue, 28 Jun 2022 21:18:15 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/somniumwave-founder-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-3d-metaverse-world-and-nft-integration/
**Tags:** General, SOM

---

Neo Global Development will host the founder of SOMNIUMWAVE on the next installment of Neo Live. Sergio Avila is joining the AMA to discuss the forthcoming 3D world and NFT integration. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn from a pool of 10 SOMNIUMWAVE NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 4:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, June 30, 2022.

SOMNIUMWAVE is an open-world 3D blockchain game designed to reward users for holding NFTs and competing in matchmaking. Players may buy and sell lands, vehicles, and weapons using the SOM NEP-17 token. The platform will tentatively offer buildings that host art galleries to display NFT collections.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
