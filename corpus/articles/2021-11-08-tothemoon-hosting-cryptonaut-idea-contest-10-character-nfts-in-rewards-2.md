# TOTHEMOON hosting Cryptonaut idea contest, 10 Character NFTs in rewards

**Published:** Mon, 08 Nov 2021 17:58:11 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/tothemoon-hosting-cryptonaut-idea-contest-10-character-nfts-in-rewards/
**Tags:** Events, Gaming, General, Metaverse, NFT, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON has announced its third community rewards campaign, the Cryptonaut Ideas Contest, in as many weeks. Unique, community-generated Characters submitted as part of the campaign may be included in the upcoming NFT-based metaverse game. The top ten submissions will each win a Cryptonaut Character NFT. The rules for the event include:

- Tweet the three character ideas and tag TOTHEMOON’s Twitter account

- Include two Twitter friends in the tweet

- Include the following hashtags: $NEO $GAS #NeoN3 #Metaverse

- Post a link to the tweet in the TOTHEMOON Discord server’s #cryptonaut-ideas channel

The contest is running through 4:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Nov. 11, 2021.
