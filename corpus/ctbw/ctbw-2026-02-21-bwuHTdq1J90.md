## Analysis

### Summary
The inaugural episode (Episode 0) of Crypto Talk and Blockchain Walk, recorded at ETH Boulder. Dylan interviews Willy Wonka, a serial Web3 builder who just started as Head of Product at Venice and launched Blank.space, a customizable Farcaster client platform. They discuss surviving crypto cycles, whether AI agents will comprise the first billion on-chain users, and the intimate builder-focused vibes of ETH Boulder versus the scale of ETH Denver.

### Topics Covered
- Willy Wonka's background as a serial Web2/Web3 builder and new role at Venice
- Blank.space — a WordPress-like community platform builder on Farcaster
- Surviving multiple crypto winters (both on their third cycle since 2017)
- Community sentiment as a function of price vs. mission-driven building
- Whether the first billion on-chain users will be humans or AI agents
- ETH Boulder vs. ETH Denver — depth of conversation vs. breadth of attendance
- The ETH Denver hackathon experience

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- **We're in a third crypto winter**: States directly "Right now we're in the midst of a third crypto winter since I've been in this space since 2017."
- **Bear markets reveal true believers**: Implies through his questions that bear market conferences are more authentic because attendees are there for values, not upside.
- **First billion users question**: Poses the provocative question of whether the first billion on-chain users will be more humans or AI agents — a question he returns to across multiple episodes.

### Guest Perspectives
- **Willy Wonka** (Head of Product, Venice; Co-founder, Blank.space): Believes the original reasons for entering crypto haven't changed and are actually being delivered on. Thinks AI agents will likely be the majority of the first billion active on-chain addresses. Expresses concern about negative public perception of crypto hindering human adoption. Values bear market building as a time with fewer distractions. Prefers smaller conferences like ETH Boulder for deeper conversations over larger events like ETH Denver.

### People & Projects Mentioned

- **ETH Denver** — Large annual Ethereum conference, noted for its free attendance and hackathon
