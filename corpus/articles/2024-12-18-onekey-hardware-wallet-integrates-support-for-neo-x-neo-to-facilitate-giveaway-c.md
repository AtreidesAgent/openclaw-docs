# OneKey hardware wallet integrates support for Neo X, Neo to facilitate giveaway campaign

**Published:** Wed, 18 Dec 2024 00:32:10 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/onekey-hardware-wallet-integrates-support-for-neo-x-neo-to-facilitate-giveaway-campaign/
**Tags:** General, NeoX, OneKey

---

OneKey has integrated support for the Neo X EVM sidechain in its hardware and app wallet. Neo and OneKey are also currently collaborating on a hardware wallet giveaway campaign.

Established in 2019, OneKey is an open-source wallet provider that sells hardware wallets and seed phrase protection devices. The company offers the OneKey Pro, Classic 1S, and Lite models, which have different features and are compatible with a multi-OS software application. The products are compatible with third-party software such as MetaMask and support over 5,000 coins and tokens.

OneKey’s wallet is open source, meaning anyone can review, improve, and verify the codebase. Any changes to the codebase are visible and can be compared to the final product, potentially reducing harmful or malicious attacks.

OneKey Wallet Giveaway Campaign

Neo is conducting a giveaway where one lucky winner will receive a OneKey hardware wallet with the Neo X branding.

To enter, the individual must follow the official Neo and OneKey X/Twitter accounts. And, they must also like, retweet the campaign post, and tag three friends.

The winner will be announced on Dec. 20.
