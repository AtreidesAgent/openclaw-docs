## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 8 is a live recording from ETH Denver 2024 Day 2 featuring four hosts — Dylan, Edge, Tyler, and Guil. The conversation covers Neo's growing presence at the event through ITEM Systems NFI plaque technology (with ~30 beer redemptions at Ratio Beer Works), conversations with EVM teams like Circle and ENS about NeoX collaboration, and strategic planning for a larger Neo footprint at ETH Denver 2025 including hackathon bounties and systemic NFI-based experiences rather than traditional booth presence.

### Topics Covered
- Neo N3 network statistics (95,704 addresses, block height 4,983,814)
- ETH Denver 2024 logistics: moved to National Western Stock Show, better internet/layout than 2023
- Multi-chain presence: Polkadot, Cosmos (Atom Day), NEAR, Bitcoin summit, L3 discussions
- Agoric: chain abstraction via JavaScript tooling (Cosmos side event)
- DePIN (decentralized physical infrastructure networks) as emerging narrative
- ZK-enabled badge onboarding at ETH Denver (first time ZK used for simple user security)
- POAP scanning stations throughout the event
- NFI plaque beer event at Ratio Beer Works: ~30 quality redemptions
- Conversations with Circle about USDC on NeoVM or NeoEVM
- Tyler/Gil conversations with ENS team about NFC integrations
- Ryu Coin team building protocol-agnostic Web3 mobile game marketplace
- ETH Denver 2025 strategy: systemic presence over booth (NFI shirts, hackathon bounties, gamified experiences)
- ETH Denver founders wanted to collaborate but timing was too late (December conversations for February event)
- Buy one get one free beer campaign continuing through March
