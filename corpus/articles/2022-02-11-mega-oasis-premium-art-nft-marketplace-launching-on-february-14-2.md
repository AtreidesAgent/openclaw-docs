# Mega Oasis premium art NFT marketplace launching on February 14

**Published:** Fri, 11 Feb 2022 21:16:31 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/mega-oasis-premium-art-nft-marketplace-launching-on-february-14th/
**Tags:** General, NFT, bNEO, MegaOasis

---

A new NFT marketplace aiming to bring traditional artists into Neo’s NFT ecosystem is launching on Monday, Feb. 14. The platform will kick off with the sale of a collection by Zhenchen Liu, a renowned artist whose works have been shown in prestigious venues all over the world.

The Mega Oasis marketplace is designed to offer collectors access to unique NFTs made specifically for the platform. It is targeted at both existing NFT collectors as well as art lovers who haven’t yet ventured into the blockchain world.

The first NFT series for sale on Mega Oasis, Meta Panacea, is based on an existing collection of physical art pieces, Panacea, created by Liu.

Zhenchen Liu

Liu is a Chinese artist who works in Paris and Shanghai. He has studied at the school of Fine Art in Shanghai, the National Superior school of Arts in Nice, and the Fresnoy National Studio of Contemporary Arts in Tourcoing. Liu’s pieces can be found in various museums throughout the world, including Grand Palais, Petit Palais, and Centre Pompidou in Paris, Reina Sofía National Art Museum in Madrid, and the Museum of Natural History in New York.

Source: ZhenchenLiu.com

Additionally, his videos have been screened at festivals such as the Locarno International Film Festival, Oberhausen International Film Festival, Festival de Clermont-Ferrand, among others. Liu’s work predominantly focuses on the postmodern urban experience through moving images and video installations.

Meta Panacea NFTs

Meta Panacea is Liu’s first NFT collection and will only be distributed through the Mega Oasis marketplace.

Source: MegaOasis.io

The Meta Panacea collection is inspired by the medicinal power of plants, herbs, and minerals. A total of 403 NFTs were designed for the collection, which will be distributed across multiple series. Each category has a floor price denoted in USD and a varying number of NFTs. They include:

- Herb: 297 NFTs at a $180 floor price

- Ingenuity: 90 NFTs at a $400 floor price

- Elixir: 15 NFTs at a $1,200 floor price

Meta Panacea NFTs can be purchased using bNEO. There will also be a fourth “surprise category” announced when the collection is released.
