- Ethereum far ahead of Bitcoin on post-quantum security — positive note amid concerns.
- EF leadership turnover (Tomasz after 1 year) and cypherpunk manifesto hint at internal disagreements on priorities (scaling/security/AI) — not coal mine canary yet but concerning.
- EF's AI trust layer push risks scope creep, draining resources amid scaling/quantum needs — questions if too big for limited capacity.
