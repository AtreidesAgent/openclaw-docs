# Alchemint invited to JBaaS Tokyo Blockchain Summit

**Published:** Wed, 04 Jul 2018 03:40:25 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/alchemint-invited-to-jbaas-tokyo-blockchain-summit/
**Tags:** General, SDS

---

Alchemint has been invited to the JBass Tokyo Blockchain Summit, planned to take place on Saturday, July 7th, 2018. As an organization, JBaaS has hosted nearly 100 roadshows and 50 meet ups throughout Japan, with its members mostly concentrated in Tokyo, Osaka, Kobe, Nagoya, and Kumamoto. 

JBaaS has become one of Japan’s first offline community groups that cater to cryptocurrency enthusiasts, and has garnered a number of media resources in the blockchain field and cryptocurrency market. 

The Tokyo Blockchain Summit will be geared towards “ordinary [people] who want to understand blockchain technology and follow the trend, investor[s] who want to identify the quality of [a] project, [and] entrepreneur[s] who seek high-quality blockchain projects in overseas markets.”

Chris Qi, Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Alchemint, will speak at the Tokyo Blockchain Summit. His topics of discussion are likely to include Alchemint as a stable coin on the NEO blockchain, its asset validation facilitation, asset trading, leveraged investing, and various commercial applications.
