# Zeepin detail Q3 roadmap and call for ZeeRights testers

**Published:** Sun, 27 May 2018 04:55:59 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/zeepin-roadmap-and-call-for-closed-beta-testers/
**Tags:** General, ZPT

---

Zeepin recently released its roadmap for Quarter 3 of 2018. The team has planned a four-month global tour with visits to Singapore, Silicon Valley, Amsterdam, and South Korea. In June, Zeepin will be visiting four cities across China.

Global Tour

Zeepin’s development team will visit Singapore to host a meetup on June 16. Zeepin will release the Galaxy whitepaper and present the consensus mechanism of the Zeepin and Galaxy chains. This meetup will coincide with the release of CryptoGalaxy beta v1.5.

The next stop on Zeepin’s global tour is Silicon Valley in July. Here Zeepin will give presentations on the development planning for Zeepin and Galaxy chains and share decentralized applications (dApp) specifications. CryptoGalaxy beta v1.6 is also planned for release.

The following month, the Zeepin team will visit Amsterdam. This event will be the official launch of the Zeepin and Galaxy MainNets. Zeepin will discuss the structure and management of the Zeepin and Galaxy ecosystems and release CryptoGalaxy beta v1.7.

The final stop will take place in September in South Korea in Quarter 4 of 2018. This event will be the inaugural Zeepin DevCon, aiming to provide a platform for launching more dApps and introducing community-based teams. CryptoGalaxy beta v1.8 will also be released.

Events in China

In conjunction with the beginning of Zeepin’s global tour, the development team will visit four Chinese cities in June. Zeepin will be connecting with members of the creative industry who may benefit from blockchain based applications.

The team will travel to the four cities in China on the dates listed below:

- June 2: Shanghai

- June 9: Beijing

- June 23: Hangzhou

- June 30: Chengdu

Zeepin recruiting ZeeRights beta-closed testers

Zeepin is currently accepting applications for the ZeeRights dApp closed beta-closed testing. ZeeRights is an asset digitization and copyright protection protocol on the Zeepin blockchain.

Zeepin is seeking professionals with an engineering or design background who have prior experience in software testing. Ten spots are available. Those chosen to participate will receive a reward of 2,000 ZPT and 4,000 GALA tokens.

Full details on how to apply can be found at the link below:

https://medium.com/@zeepin/we-are-recruiting-beta-testers-for-zeerights-eb9480c75e27

Future rounds of beta tests will follow, which will provide opportunities for community members to participate.
