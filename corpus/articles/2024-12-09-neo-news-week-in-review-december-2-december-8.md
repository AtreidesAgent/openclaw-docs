# Neo News: Week in Review – December 2 – December 8

**Published:** Mon, 09 Dec 2024 16:46:04 +0000

General Updates

Meson, a cross-chain swap platform specializing in stablecoin transfers, announced its upcoming integration with Neo X. The collaboration will provide Neo users with access to Meson’s infrastructure, which supports transfers across more than 50 blockchain networks. Integration with Neo X will allow users to provide liquidity in swap pools as well as bridge in and out of the ecosystem.

Battle Hard is hosting a US $1,000 giveaway in B tokens to celebrate the launch of its Hardened, Legends, and Vendor contracts on Neo N3 MainNet. Battle Hard is a platform designed to inject new utility into NFTs, enabling them to possess battle-ready attributes while reactivating dormant wallets by reviving abandoned projects. The campaign, which began on Dec. 4, will reward 10 users who generate the most exposure for Neo-based projects with 100 B tokens each. Winners will be announced on Dec. 24.

Flamingo Finance shared a recording of the November 2024 Community Lagoon AMA, which noted the Orderbook launch on TestNet for beta testing, updates on the Flamingo data dashboard, upcoming plans for the DeFi trading platform, forthcoming cross-chain functionality, upcoming Neo X support via AMM, and more.

Meme2Earn released a recording of its November 2024 Community Fireplace, which noted updates on the latest meme contest to celebrate 200,000 posts, reaching out to new projects to conduct Meme2Earn campaigns, and more.

Neo Turkey social media community launched, dedicated to providing information in Turkish to the international Neo community.

Frank Coin hosted a Discord trivia event where winners were rewarded with GAS.

Developer Updates

Neo SPCC released NeoGo v0.107.1, featuring a new NeoFS-based solution for synchronizing blocks. The release remains compatible with Neo C# v3.7.5, the current version on the Neo MainNet, and does not require database resynchronization, offering a straightforward upgrade process for node operators. These updates were originally released in NeoGo v0.107.0. However, a patched release, v0.107.1, has been distributed to fix an issue with the NeoFS BlockFetcher shutdown procedure. Neo SPCC also released dBFT v0.3.1, which includes a set of library API extensions made to fit the needs of developing the Neo X MEV-resistant blockchain node.

COZ released v0.5.0 of the Neo N3 Ledger app, delivering new features and improvements to enhance the user experience. Version 0.5.0 introduces several updates aimed at improving transaction verification and compatibility. The Neo N3 Ledger app can be downloaded from the Ledger Live store.

NNT Catch Up

NNT released Episode 78 of The Smart Economy Podcast with Joe Vezzani and Jon Farjo, co-founders of LunarCrush, a platform that provides social media intelligence and analysis on digital assets. Topics of discussion include how social media metrics can help determine the appetite for specific digital assets, the metrics and categories that LunarCrush follows, how AI has improved the team’s ability and processes, the forthcoming rise of AI agents, and much more.

NNT editors co-hosted Crypto Coffee and Blockchain Beer Spaces #42 on the official The Smart Economy Podcast X account. Topics of discussion included Bitcoin surpassing the $100k milestone and Ethereum’s outlook, whether the memecoin meta is finally over, the launch of the new Neo Turkish community, the Neo Enclave account abstraction platform, and DogeRift sunsetting and potentially returning some user funds.

In Case You Missed It

Meme2Earn rebranded the TIPS meme token ticker back to NUDES, the original symbol launched in January 2022. The decision follows community feedback and reflects the project’s intent to embrace its origins as the first memecoin on the Neo N3 blockchain. The announcement was made on the What we talk about when we talk about memes X Space hosted by Neo.

Nichome announced a new partnership with Supernova Shards, a multiplayer space simulator. This collaboration is planned to integrate Supernova Shards’ gaming experience into Nichome’s gamified rewards platform, expanding the opportunities for users to earn X Credits.

NeoDashboard launched an NDMEME Advent Calendar Event, offering participants a chance to win up to 10,000 NDMEME tokens daily. The event, which runs for 24 days starting Dec. 1, utilizes COZ’s Dice contract for transparent on-chain number draws.

COZ released Neon Wallet Mobile v1.3.0, its first version for iOS and Android with support for Neo X, accompanied by various other improvements. Users now have complete access to Neo X from within the mobile wallet, alongside several other EVM-based networks, including Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, and Polygon.
