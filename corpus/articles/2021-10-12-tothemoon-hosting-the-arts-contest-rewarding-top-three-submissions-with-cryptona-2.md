# TOTHEMOON hosting The Arts Contest, rewarding top three submissions with Cryptonaut NFT

**Published:** Tue, 12 Oct 2021 17:50:57 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/tothemoon-hosting-the-arts-contest-rewarding-top-three-submissions-with-cryptonaut-nft/
**Tags:** Events, Gaming, General, Metaverse, NFT, TTM

---

TOTHEMOON has announced a two-week community campaign entitled The Arts Contest. Contestants can submit any form of artwork inspired by the upcoming strategic community-building NFT game. The contest is live and running through 12:00 p.m. (UTC) on Monday, Oct. 25, 2021.

While any form of art can be submitted for the contest, TOTHEMOON highlighted the following:

- Hand-Drawn Art

- Digital Art

- Painting

- Creative Writing

- Music

- Sound Engineering

- 2D Animation

- 3D Animation

The leadership team will handpick their three favorite pieces of TOTHEMOON-inspired artwork, whose creators will win one of the following characters:

- Cryptonaut .034

- Cryptonaut .013

- Cryptonaut .026

Participants can submit an unlimited number art entries, but must do so in the TOTHEMOON ArtStation channel on Discord.

TOTHEMOON is a metaverse that seeks to layer and integrate NFTs in a way that establishes a thriving supply and demand economy. Characters, buildings, and items in the game are represented by NFTs, which interact and respond to characteristics of other NFTs. While users must individually accumulate items and characters, there is also a communal element necessary for success in the game.
