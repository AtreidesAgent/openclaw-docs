# APEX to be listed on HotBit exchange, third wave of KRATOS One rewards available

**Published:** Tue, 06 Nov 2018 21:24:00 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/apex-to-be-listed-on-hotbit-exchange-third-wave-of-kratos-one-rewards-available/
**Tags:** General, CPX

---

APEX Network announced a listing on HotBit, and that the third wave of KRATOS One rewards are ready to be claimed. The KRATOS One program was designed for future APEX token (CPX) holding voternodes, to act as participants in the voting process and provide a check-and-balance to supernodes that process and validate transactions. An overview of the APEX node system was previously covered by NEO News Today.

APEX Network token (CPX) deposits on HotBit will begin at 2:00 pm (UTC +8) on Thursday, November 8th, and trading is scheduled to begin at 2:00 pm (UTC +8) on Friday, November 9th.

The CPX token will be paired with Ethereum (ETH) and Bitcoin (BTC).

At the time of this writing, HotBit is the #51 ranked cryptocurrency exchange by 24-hour volume trading, with $26.1 million. The exchange offers over 320+ trading pairs with ETH, BTC, and USDT. 

KRATOS One third wave rewards

APEX Network has also announced KRATOS One third wave rewards are now available to be claimed. The announcement of the third wave was recently covered by NEO News Today. 

The snapshot for the third wave of KRATOS One rewards took place on October 30th, and can now be claimed on an updated version of the APEX Wallet found at the link below:

https://www.apexnetwork.io/wallet/downloads.html 

After participants have installed the APEX Wallet, they will need to submit the node holding their NEO key through the wallets “Claim Function.”
