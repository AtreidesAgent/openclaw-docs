# Zeux lets users pay with NEO for products and services through Apple and Samsung Pay

**Published:** Fri, 03 May 2019 15:34:43 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/zeux-lets-users-pay-with-neo-for-products-and-services-through-apple-and-samsung-pay/
**Tags:** General, NEO

---

Zeux, a digital payment wallet, announced upcoming support for NEO, available on its app beginning Tuesday, May 7th. Zeux offers a mobile application that “allows users to convert their cryptocurrency and pay in just one click.” 

Further, through the integration with Zeux, NEO can be used to purchase product and services through Apple and Samsung Pay. 

The Zeux app “provides a digital bank account on top of a digital wallet address for each user.” There, users can make payments and have access to a “range of investment products.”

By listing NEO, Zeux founder and CEO, Frank Zhou believes the app may expand its current infrastructure and reach a broader market. With regards to NEO, Zhou believes the public blockchain “is paving the way for a smart economy by giving developers the tools to deploy and scale their own smart contract applications without learning another unique language.”
