# Neo and SpoonOS announce Spoon-Fed Ideas, connecting general audience with Scoop AI Hackathon builders

**Published:** Wed, 29 Oct 2025 23:26:45 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/ai/neo-and-spoonos-announce-spoon-fed-ideas-connecting-general-audience-with-scoop-ai-hackathon-builders/
**Tags:** AI, Events, General, GAS, SpoonOS

---

Neo and SpoonOS have launched Spoon-Fed Ideas, a unique way that ecosystem members can contribute to the multi-stop, multi-month Scoop AI Hackathon. Spoon-Fed Ideas is an initiative designed to connect non-developers with builders in the Web3 and AI space. If a Spoon-Fed Idea wins one of the various hackathon “Bowls,” then both the individual who makes the suggestion and developer who builds it will win GAS rewards.

The campaign invites anyone to submit creative ideas for SpoonOS-powered projects by posting on X using the hashtag #SpoonFedIdeas. For non-developers, participation requires sharing a Tweet about an idea you’d like to see built, and including the corresponding hashtag in the body of the message. A representative from the Neo team will be monitoring the hashtag’s activity.

Once approved by moderators, submissions will be listed on the official campaign page, where participants from all eight Scoop AI Hackathon events can explore ideas for potential builds. Developers are encouraged to choose ideas that are innovative, feasible, and have strong community backing. Engagement metrics such as likes, replies, and reposts can help ideas stand out to potential teams.

While developers are not required to build an idea submitted through the campaign, Spoon-Fed Ideas is aimed at being a source of inspiration and an avenue to connect builders with potential early users.

SpoonOS will award non-developer contributors US $150 in GAS if their idea is selected by a team that wins first place at any Scoop AI Hackathon event. The winning developer team will receive a $100 bonus in addition to the standard prize.

Idea submissions must originate from individuals who are not participating in the hackathon as builders. Attempts to submit and develop one’s own idea may result in disqualification. Neo and SpoonOS reserve the right to make final decisions regarding submission validity and award eligibility.
