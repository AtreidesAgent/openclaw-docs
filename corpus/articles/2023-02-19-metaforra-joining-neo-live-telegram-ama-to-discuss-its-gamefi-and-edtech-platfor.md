# METAFORRA joining Neo Live Telegram AMA to discuss its GameFi and EdTech platform

**Published:** Sun, 19 Feb 2023 22:32:25 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/metaforra-joining-neo-live-telegram-ama-to-discuss-its-gamefi-and-edtech-platform/
**Tags:** Events, General, METAFORRA, METTA

---

Neo Global Development will host Arthur, COO of METAFORRA, on the next installment of Neo Live. Five participants will be eligible to win an equivalent of US $150 in METTA tokens. The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Monday, Feb. 20.

METAFORRA is a GameFi ecosystem that focuses on mobile and cross-platform games. Additionally, the platform offers an NFT marketplace and education technology components. The team intends to support five games on the platform, including Futuructa, Quantum War, Steampunk Tower, Steampunk Tower 2, and Idle Raider of the Dice Heroes. METAFORRA and NGD seek to collaborate on creating a gaming ecosystem that competes against traditional games by offering players more control of their assets and data.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
