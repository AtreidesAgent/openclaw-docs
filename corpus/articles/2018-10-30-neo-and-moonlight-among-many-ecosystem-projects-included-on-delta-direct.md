# NEO and Moonlight among many ecosystem projects included on Delta Direct

**Published:** Tue, 30 Oct 2018 17:48:50 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-and-moonlight-among-many-ecosystem-projects-included-on-delta-direct/
**Tags:** General, CPX, EFX, LRC, LX, ONT, SWTH, TOLL

---

NEO and Moonlight were recently added to Delta Direct, a function of Delta portfolio tracking application version 1.4. Delta aims to provide its users the most recent coin prices and market charts via alerts. NEO and Moonlight are among many projects in the NEO ecosystem that have been added to the platform, including Bridge Protocol, Effect.AI, Ontology, Loopring, Switcheo, and APEX Network. 

The Delta Direct function strives to “[empower] users to get better informed about their current and potential future investments.” The application seeks to act as a direct link between coin and token projects, with existing and future investors. Through the application “users will get instantly notified about new blog posts or direct messages from the [project] team.”
