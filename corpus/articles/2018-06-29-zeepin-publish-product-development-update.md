# Zeepin publish product development update

**Published:** Fri, 29 Jun 2018 05:18:39 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/zeepin-publish-product-development-update/
**Tags:** General, ZPT

---

On June 28, 2018, Zeepin released its bi-weekly progress report, which highlighted product developments and details about an upcoming meetup in Hangzhou, China.

Product Development 

Functional development for ZeeRights has been completed. ZeeRights is a creative assets digitization, copyright registration, and copyright transaction platform. The application is currently in closed testing, with the beta testing stage to begin soon. Additionally, Zeepin is making technical improvements to the public blockchain connection, consortium blockchain, and the ZeeWallet. 

The development team has also finished the ZeeCreate process and prototype designs, and is currently preparing the technical development of its request files. Additionally, the product user experience (UX) design and user interface (UI) design development has begun. ZeeCreate aims to connect creative content with people in need of creative ideas, utilizing ZPT tokens as the medium of exchange on embedded smart contracts. 

Lastly, the Zeepin team highlighted CryptoGalaxy version 1.5 updates, which NEO News Today recently covered. 

CryptoGalaxy open testing will start in early July of 2018. To apply for testing, email anze.noc@zeepin.com, and include full name, email address associated with in-game account, and nationality.

Upcoming Meetup in Hangzhou

Zeepin is holding an event in Hangzhou as part of Zeepin’s China Meetup Tour. The theme of the meetup is “How Blockchain Can Bring Scenario Application to Life in the Creative Industry,” and will take place from 14:00 to 16:00 on Saturday, June 30th. 

The agenda for the event is planned as follows:

- 13:30 to 14:00 – Event check-in.

- 14:00 to 14:15 – dApp scene application of Wenchuang industry.

- 14:15 to 14:35 – Blockchain development and entrepreneurial opportunities.

- 14:35 to 14:55 – “Why choose wisdom?” Intellectual Ecology Introduction.

- 14:55 to 15:15 – Tea break.

- 15:05 to 15:20 – Blockchain investment and community construction.

- 15:20 to 15:30 – Application of blockchain in supply chain finance

- 15:30 to 16:00 – Q&A

The event will be free to the public.
