# Alphacat offer access to NEO price swing forecasting bot

**Published:** Fri, 01 Jun 2018 14:15:05 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/alphacat-neo-price-swing-forecasting-bot/
**Tags:** General, ACAT, NEO

---

Alphacat has concluded its community vote for the next cryptocurrency to receive a dedicated price swing forecasting bot. The Alphacat team has an operational Bitcoin (BTC) forecasting bot, which predicted BTC price rises with a 63.3% accuracy in April of 2018. 

International and Chinese communities were polled by Alphacat, with overwhelming support for NEO to be the next forecasting bot released. A total of 60.4% of the international, and 31.7% of the Chinese communities voted in favor of NEO.

Currently, the Alphacat Team is beta testing the NEO price swing forecast bot on the Alphacat engine. Like the BTC forecasting bot, NEO price swing predictions will be derived from 1,000 deep learning bots. The bots individually compute the likelihood of a gain or decrease in the price of a coin, and aggregates the 1,000 predictions into a percentage probability.

NEO forecasting bot services will be provided to ACAT token holders for one month, at no cost, if they’re holding at least 1 million ACAT tokens. Applications for a month of free NEO forecasting bot services will be accepted until June 3rd, 2018.
