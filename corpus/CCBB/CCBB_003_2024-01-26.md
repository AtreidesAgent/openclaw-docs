Dylan and Edge also discuss Neo's marketing challenges relative to competitors like Tron and Solana, Tron's dominance in stablecoin transfers, and tease an upcoming ITEM Systems collaboration with ETH Denver 2024 involving non-fungible item (NFI) plaques from the Denver Walls mural festival.
