# Neo Ultimate Shop and Neo Red Pill Launch ‘Nikolaus Event’ Holiday Giveaway

**Published:** Thu, 11 Dec 2025 19:17:35 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-ultimate-shop-and-neo-red-pill-launch-nikolaus-event-holiday-giveaway/
**Tags:** Events, General, NeoUltimateShop, NRP

---

Neo Ultimate Shop and Neo Red Pill have unveiled the “Nikolaus Event,” a Christmas‑themed giveaway campaign offering community members a chance to win physical merchandise as well as NRP and NEO tokens. The campaign is aimed at rewarding engagement across social channels and promoting visibility for both the merchandise store and the memecoin ecosystem.

To participate in the Nikolaus Event, users must:

- Retweet the official announcement

- Follow both the Neo Ultimate Shop and Neo Red Pill X accounts

- Tag a friend in the comment section

The Nikolaus Event is broken into three drawings:

- First drawing (Dec. 7): Already completed; two winners each received 500,000 NRP tokens

- Second drawing (Dec. 24): Two participants will be selected, each winning 1 million NRP tokens

- Mega drawing (Dec. 24): One “mega winner” will receive a metal seed phrase card, an NRP T‑shirt, NRP coffee mug, and 20 NEO

About Neo Ultimate Shop and Neo Red Pill

Neo Ultimate Shop is a webstore offering physical merchandise tied to the Neo ecosystem and broader blockchain culture. The store was bootstrapped via a successful proposal on the GrantShares funding platform, which allocated 1,581 GAS for development and marketing purposes.

Neo Red Pill is a community‑originated memecoin that launched within the Neo ecosystem in early 2025. The project conducted initial token distributions across 66 wallets and has executed multiple token‑distribution campaigns in collaboration with members of the Neo community. The team behind NRP also operates the Neo Ultimate Shop, linking the digital memecoin with tangible merchandise offerings.
