Day two of ETH Denver 2026 with Kent, an ETH Denver steward and crypto veteran since 2012, who co-founded CoinCap (a crypto data API at the intersection of LLMs and crypto). They have a wide-ranging conversation about bear market survival strategies, the importance of IRL events for countering online negativity, Ethereum's decade of near-100% uptime as proof of success, and why price should not be used as a proxy for the health of the ecosystem.

### Guest Perspectives
- **Kent** (ETH Denver Steward, CoinCap co-founder): In crypto since learning about Bitcoin in 2012; started ETH Denver involvement in 2014. Prefers the smaller, more intimate ETH Denver because vibe and energy matter more than headcount. Building CoinCap with a crypto data API that used Morpheus token staking for access, now pivoting strategies as token prices declined. Passionate about music and looking for music-related crypto projects. Believes Ethereum is objectively a success based on its 10+ year uptime and the original white paper vision being realized.
