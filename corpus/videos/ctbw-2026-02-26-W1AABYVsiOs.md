## Analysis

### Summary
Day one of ETH Denver 2026 with Grimajo, a self-funded content creator and podcast host attending his first ETH Denver. They discuss the Little Quest on-chain scavenger hunt spanning ETH Boulder through ETH Denver, the content creator grind during bear markets, market cycle dynamics, and both Dylan and Grimajo's reflections on how ETH Denver has evolved from an intimate Colorado event to a multi-chain international conference.

### Topics Covered
- **Little Quest** — On-chain scavenger hunt (ETH Denver edition, third iteration) with local Denver landmarks and content creator track
- Grimajo's first ETH Denver experience — self-funded California-based creator
- ETH Denver's evolution into a multi-chain conference and new venue impressions
- The long coffee lines and laser latte art slowing everything down
- Bear market content creation as a form of expression and coping
- Market cycle analysis — shorter/weirder cycles compared to previous bull/bear patterns
- The Farcaster community and Tempo hiring Merkle team members
- Pokemon card opening as a bear market hobby and on-chain digitization via Courtyard
- Grimajo's search for a full-time Web3 job while working in Web2 tech
- Dylan's 8-year ETH Denver veteran perspective and nostalgia for the early days

### Dylan's Takes & Opinions
- **ETH Denver has lost some innocence as it grew**: "I feel like there was like a certain magic that happened here and that innocence was like lost as it grew." Reflects on the industry maturing with banks, the SEC chair visiting, and Farcaster discussions about forking.
- **Bear market feels different this time**: "We didn't have our blow-off top... we're not having a terrifying tumultuous bottom either... this was always what we thought was gonna happen when the institutions came."
- **This is his 8th ETH Denver**: Positions himself as a long-time local who has rose-colored glasses about the event.
- **Multi-chain is a feature, not a bug**: "We win when there's more chains. That's the thesis of decentralization."
- **Content creators are the backbone**: "The content creators stick through thick and thin."

### Guest Perspectives
- **Grimajo** (Podcast host, Behind the Screen with Grimajo): Self-funded creator from California at his first ETH Denver. Runs the Little Quest on-chain scavenger hunt designed to get conference attendees to explore the local area as tourists. Approaching podcast episode 50. Still seeking full-time Web3 employment while working in Web2 tech. Finds bear market content creation valuable as self-expression. Notes that market cycles feel shorter and weirder than before — no clear blow-off top, just micro-pops. Opens Pokemon packs as a bear market coping mechanism and digitizes them on-chain via Courtyard. Passionate about privacy, P2P tech, and AI agents.
