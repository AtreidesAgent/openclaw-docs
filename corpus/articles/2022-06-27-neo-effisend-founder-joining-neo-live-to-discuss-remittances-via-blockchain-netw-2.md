# Neo-Effisend founder joining Neo Live to discuss remittances via blockchain networks

**Published:** Mon, 27 Jun 2022 21:57:47 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/neo-effisend-founder-joining-neo-live-to-discuss-remittances-via-blockchain-networks/
**Tags:** General, Neo-Effisend

---

Neo Global Development will host the founder of Neo-Effisend on the next installment of Neo Live. Luis Oliver is joining the AMA to discuss remittances and remittance apps on blockchain. Neo Live participants will be eligible to earn from a pool of 10 Neo-Effisend NFTs.

The hour-long session will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 12:00 p.m. (UTC) on Wednesday, June 29, 2022.

Neo-Effisend is a Neo-based mobile dApp that integrates with Rapyd and aims to improve financial inclusion in Mexico and Latin America.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event that seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
