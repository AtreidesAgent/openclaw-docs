# NGD to host demo week of winning projects from Neo Frontier Launchpad event

**Published:** Fri, 23 Jul 2021 20:45:01 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/ngd-to-host-demo-week-of-winning-projects-from-neo-frontier-launchpad-event/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo Global Development is hosting a Neo Frontier Launchpad demo week starting July 26 and has published the schedule for the event. The week will showcase the top projects built during the recent hackathon. Winners were announced earlier this week and can be found here.

The week-long series of project presentations runs 3:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. (UTC) each day Monday, July 26, through Friday, July 30. NGD will record each of the sessions and upload the video to the official Neo YouTube channel. The demo week agenda is as follows:

Monday, July 26

- Digital Verse

- Tantalis NFT and data marketplace

- De-centralize Data, Research, and Management Consulting

Tuesday, July 27

- Chain.Game

- Argo

Wednesday, July 28

- Rentfuse

- IntelliJ Plugin for Neo Blockchain

- NOTAI

Thursday, July 29

- HumSwap

- Lyrebird

Friday, July 30

- dClubhouse

- Impel

- TOTHEMOON UNIVERSE

Sessions will be conducted via Zoom (Meeting ID 7865101197, passcode 123456).
