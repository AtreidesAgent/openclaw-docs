# Moonlight updates brand and website ahead of RC1 launch

**Published:** Wed, 04 Mar 2020 19:00:00 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/moonlight-updates-website-ahead-of-rc1-launch/
**Tags:** General, LX

---

Moonlight has updated its brand in line with user feedback as part of preparations for the launch of release candidate one. The Moonlight website has also been updated to highlight various platform features, such as how it intends to offer verified digital credentials and professional experience, data control and management, and detailed analytics.

In addition, Moonlight has migrated its blog and all existing content from Medium to its own website. Moving forward, Moonlight intends to use its new blog platform for all news and announcements.

In a conversation with Neo News Today, Moonlight design director, Ben Hallowes, said:

Before revising the design language, we asked a number of our users and potential users what they wanted and needed from our site. Overwhelmingly, the responses focused on providing content that answers new users’ questions in clear terms, using an engaging and accessible aesthetic.

The new site not only does a better job of informing visitors who we are as a company, what matters to us and what our USP is, it also provides an enhanced user experience.

As a team, we believe the redesign will reinforce Moonlight’s aspirations as a thought leader in the identity and recruitment spaces.

We look forward to any community feedback and are excited to reveal the many exciting new developments we have in the pipeline as they become available over the coming months!
