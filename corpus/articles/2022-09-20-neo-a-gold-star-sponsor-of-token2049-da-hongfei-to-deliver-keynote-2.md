# Neo a gold star sponsor of Token2049, Da Hongfei to deliver keynote

**Published:** Tue, 20 Sep 2022 18:17:12 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-a-gold-star-sponsor-of-token2049-da-hongfei-to-deliver-keynote/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo is a gold sponsor at the upcoming Token2049 conference, being held at the Marina Bay Sands hotel in Singapore on Sep. 28 and 29. Neo founder Da Hongfei will deliver a keynote speech entitled The Future of Web3: Governance, Security and Compliance.

Additionally, Neo will bring its interactive chest game first demoed at Consensus 2022. Conference attendees will be able to collect a paper wallet at the booth and use a preloaded NFT to unlock a physical box. Opening the box rewards the participants with token prizes from ecosystem projects.

Token2049 is an annual cryptocurrency and blockchain conference with an event each year in Singapore and London, UK. The events aim to connect founders and executives of leading Web3 companies to share their perspectives on the blockchain industry and cryptocurrency markets. The Singapore conference serves as the flagship event for Asia Crypto Week, which encapsulates a week of independently organized events.

Token2049 will feature more than 200 speakers, host more than 7,000 attendees, and offer access to more than 250 exhibitors.

Currently, tickets cost US $1,299 for registration, but the price will increase to $1,499 on Sep. 26.
