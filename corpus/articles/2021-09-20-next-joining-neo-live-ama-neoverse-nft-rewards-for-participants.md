# NEXT joining Neo Live AMA, Neoverse NFT rewards for participants

**Published:** Mon, 20 Sep 2021 22:16:56 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/next-joining-neo-live-ama-neoverse-nft-rewards-for-participants/
**Tags:** Events, General, Neo Live, Neoverse, NEXT

---

Neo Global Development will host China-based Neo developer community, NEXT, on the third Neo Live session of 2021. NeoLine tech lead, Kyle, will join the AMA to talk about the mobile and browser-based wallet. NGD will also reward five participants with a Neoverse blind box. The hour-long Neo Live event will take place on the official Neo Telegram channel, beginning at 1:00 p.m. (UTC) on Thursday, Sep. 23, 2021.

Neo Live is an AMA-style community event on the official Neo Telegram channel. The event seeks to connect the Neo community with NGD core teams, ecosystem projects, the Neo developer community, and blockchain leaders to learn about the latest technological developments and industry insights.
