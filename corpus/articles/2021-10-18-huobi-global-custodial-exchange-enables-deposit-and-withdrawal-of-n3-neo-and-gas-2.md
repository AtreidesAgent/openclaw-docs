# Huobi Global custodial exchange enables deposit and withdrawal of N3 NEO and GAS

**Published:** Mon, 18 Oct 2021 22:46:49 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/huobi-global-custodial-exchange-enables-deposit-and-withdrawal-of-n3-neo-and-gas/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, GAS, Huobi, NEO

---

Huobi Global has announced that N3 NEO and GAS deposit and withdrawal services have resumed on the custodial exchange. Huobi Global is currently ranked #7 in 24-hour volume on spot exchanges at approximately US $4.27 billion, according to CoinMarketCap.

On Sep. 14, 2021, Huobi migrated user NEO and GAS to N3 and suspended deposits and withdrawals. However, the team stated it would distribute migration rewards proportionately to the amount of NEO held in the user’s account. A snapshot of user accounts was taken at 4:00 p.m. (UTC) on the same day.

At the time of press, Gate.io and CEX.IO also offer support for N3 NEO and GAS. Looking forward, other large custodial exchanges have signaled their intent to support N3 assets in 2021. These include Binance, Bittrex, and Bitfinex.

The full announcement can be found at the link below:

https://www.huobi.ps/support/en-us/detail/84888616239876
