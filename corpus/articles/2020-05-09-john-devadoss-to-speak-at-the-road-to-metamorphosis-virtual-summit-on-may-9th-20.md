# John deVadoss to speak at The Road to Metamorphosis virtual summit on May 9th, 2020

**Published:** Sat, 09 May 2020 00:23:42 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/john-devadoss-to-speak-at-the-road-to-metamorphosis-virtual-summit-on-may-9th-2020/
**Tags:** Events, NGD Seattle

---

Neo Global Development (NGD) Seattle lead, John deVadoss, is scheduled to deliver a presentation at The Road to Metamorphosis virtual summit taking place on Saturday, May 9th, 2020. deVadoss’ presentation – Future of Money – Stablecoins as the New Moat – aims to offer an overview of new stablecoin markets in India and will be held from 6:00 pm to 6:30 pm (UTC +5:30).

The summit was initially supposed to take place in Bengaluru, India, on May 23rd, 2020, but has shifted to a virtual conference in response to the COVID-19 global pandemic. The one-day event claims to host more than 20 speakers and anticipates more than 5,000 attendees.

The Road to Metamorphosis virtual summit is hosted by Octaloop, an India-based blockchain and cryptocurrency community that was founded in 2013, when “Bitcoin was trading under [US] $300.”

There is no cost to attend the event.

Registration for the event can be found at the link below:
