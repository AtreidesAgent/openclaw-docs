# Wowoo WWB token to begin trading on Bit-Z, May 15h

**Published:** Thu, 10 May 2018 17:56:21 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/wowoo-to-be-listed-on-bit-z/
**Tags:** Exchanges, WWB

---

On May 8th, 2018, Wowoo announced via their Telegram channel that its WWB token will be listed for trading on Bit-Z on May 15, 2018. The Hong Kong-based exchange will be the first to list WWB, and was one of six exchanges Wowoo had considered for its initial listing.

Bit-Z has a large market share in Hong Kong, Beijing, and Singapore, and currently offers 84 coin/token pairings for trading. According to CoinMarketCap, as of 10:00 pm (MST) on May 9, 2018, Bit-Z had the 10th largest 24-hour trading volume among all exchanges at $255,483,276 USD.

Wowoo is currently developing a bonus token campaign, and anticipates releasing as much as 400,000 WWB for distribution. Details will be made available soon. In the meantime, the team suggests registering for an account at Bit-Z.com. WWB token deposits have not yet opened.

For more information about the Bit-Z exchange visit their website, Twitter, Telegram, Medium, or Slack.
