# Neo touring top colleges across the US throughout October including Harvard, Yale, and more.

**Published:** Wed, 21 Sep 2022 18:22:20 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/events/neo-touring-top-colleges-across-the-us-throughout-october-including-harvard-yale-and-more/
**Tags:** Events, General, NEO

---

Neo is embarking on a tour of more than a dozen universities across the US to discuss Web3 opportunities and the Neo blockchain with college students. The cross-campus journey launches in October, with the mission to build, bridge, and broaden relationships with the next generation of change-makers across the country.

Through the tour, Neo Global Development intends to build connections with students and share innovative ideas. The aim is to bridge connections between blockchain, fintech, and VC student clubs to strengthen the foundation of Web3. NGD also seeks to raise awareness of Neo through access to incentive programs like EcoBoost, GrantShares, and more.

The tour will include visits to the following universities (broken down by region of the country):

East Coast

- Oct. 2: Yale

- Oct. 3: Boston College

- Oct. 4: MIT

- Oct. 5: Harvard

- Oct. 6: New York City (Featuring university clubs from NYU, Columbia, Fordham, Cornell, and Parsons.)

Midwest

- Oct. 12: University of Michigan

- Oct. 13: Northwestern University

- Oct. 14: University of Illinois – Champaign

Greater Los Angeles Area

- Oct. 17: University of California – Los Angeles

- Oct. 18: University of Southern California

- Oct. 20: University of California – San Diego

Greater San Francisco Bay Area

- Oct. 24: University of California – Berkeley

- Oct. 25: Stanford

Source: NEO Global Development

The tour will feature presentations from various members of the Neo ecosystem, who will share responsibility for speaking to students in different regions. The list of speakers includes:

- Da Hongfei, Neo Founder

- John deVadoss, President of NGD Enterprise

- Harry Pierson, Chief Architect of NGD Enterprise

- John Wang, Head of NGD EcoGrowth

- Steven Liu, Head of NGD Development

- Guil. Sperb Machado, Founder of AxLabs

- Tyler Adams, Founder and Executive Officer of COZ

- Dylan Grabowski, Editor of Neo News Today

- And other VCs, professors, media reporters, and project leaders to be announced later.

Attendees of each university event will receive an exclusive NFT. NGD will release more information about claiming the NFTs in the coming weeks.
