# Travala partnership enhances travel booking platform, 400,000 new properties to list in December

**Published:** Tue, 27 Nov 2018 19:28:39 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/travala-partnership-enhances-travel-booking-platform-400000-new-properties-to-list-in-december/
**Tags:** General, AVA

---

Travala has recently partnered with Gimmonix and announced an upcoming listing of 400,000 new properties in December. Gimmonix is a technology company that helps businesses boost their efficiency through automated mapping technology, API connectivity, and profit optimization tools.

Through the partnership, Travala will gain access to a network of over 80 hotel suppliers, which will also include mapping inventory options such as enhanced room images and descriptions. By automatically mapping inventory, customers will have personalized search results when using the Travala platform. 

Gimmonix’s enhanced processing speeds and enterprise-level scaling will allow the Travala platform to search 100k properties in 2 seconds, which will purportedly be the “highest speed searches in the industry.”  The aggregation layer will also offer on-demand integration with hotel, flight, and car suppliers. Additionally, this layer will be able to support a variety of payment gateways. 

Lastly, once Gimmonix’s software is fully integrated with Travala in Q1 of 2019, Travala states it will offer access to 1.5 million properties.

New Properties in December

In December, Travala will list more than 400,000 new properties as a result of strategic partnerships made during the previous months. The addition of the new properties increases Travala accommodations to 550,000 offerings in 210 countries.

The new properties listed on the platform will include the first accommodations in North America, South America, and Africa.
