# Switcheo Exchange lists another batch of ERC-20 tokens on its DEX

**Published:** Tue, 27 Nov 2018 19:16:15 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/general/switcheo-exchange-lists-another-batch-of-erc-20-tokens-on-its-dex/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, SWTH

---

Switcheo has recently launched its Callisto update, which includes Ethereum ERC-20 token trading on the decentralized exchange (DEX). The launch of Callisto was recently covered by NEO News Today. Though the Ethereum DEX is now active on Switcheo, the platform has not yet incorporated cross-chain trading, but interoperability functionality is in development. 

Upon the launch of Callisto, Switcheo listed over a dozen ERC-20 tokens, and as of November 26th has listed 19 more. The addition of the new ERC-20 tokens brings Switcheo’s Ethereum DEX offering to a total of 34 pairings with ETH. 

Deposits for tokens opened at 11:30 am (UTC +8) on Tuesday, November 27th, and trading began at 12:00 pm (UTC +8) the same day.
