## Analysis

### Summary
Episode 77 is a post-DevConnect debrief with Gramajo, a content creator Dylan met at the Nouns DAO booth in Buenos Aires. Dylan shares his experience seeking reinvigoration at the developer-oriented event after months of negative Neo ecosystem sentiment, his search for real stablecoin adoption in Argentina (finding only one person in 2.5 weeks), and the contrast between builder energy at DevConnect vs. the malaise felt by alt-L1 ecosystems. Gramajo shares insights on content creation sustainability and his plans to explore new ecosystems including Neo.

- Ethereum DevConnect Buenos Aires experience and vibes — immaculate builder energy despite market dumps
- Dylan's 2.5-week search for real stablecoin adoption in Argentina — found one Venezuelan emigrant on his last day
- The collective crunch alt-L1 ecosystems are feeling right now
- Nouns DAO booth as the standout community activation — kid-friendly, art, recycled plastics
- Content creation sustainability during bear markets — how creators survive
- Privacy as a major theme at DevConnect (privacy pools, Aztec, ZK, SimpleX, Session)
- COZ activation in Sao Paulo with Iron Studios — Block Spirits up 7,814%
