# Neo N3 exchange support update – Q1 2022

**Published:** Fri, 25 Mar 2022 21:30:05 +0000
**URL:** https://neonewstoday.com/exchanges/neo-n3-exchange-support-update-q1-2022/
**Tags:** Exchanges, General, FLM, GAS, NEO

---

In Sep. 2021, exchanges began to integrate support for Neo N3 assets, with several trading platforms removing Legacy NEP-5 tokens and replacing them with NEP-17. As of March 2022, nearly a dozen exchanges offer trading for Neo N3 tokens, with more still on the way. Neo News Today has compiled a list of international platforms and the pairs that users can trade against.

N3 NEO is available on:

- Flamingo (FLM, GAS, bNEO, fUSDT, fWETH, fWBTC, pONT, GM, fCAKE, SWTH)

- Binance (USDT, BUSD, BNB, ETH, BTC, TRY, RUB)

- Huobi (BTC, USDT)

- OKX (USDT, BTC, ETH)

- Gate.io (USDT, BTC)

- MEXC (USDT)

- BigONE (USD, USDT)

- IndoDAX (IDR)

- CEX.io (EUR, GBP, USD, USDT)

- BKEx (USDT, BTC, ETH)

- WazirX (USDT, INR)

N3 GAS is available on:

- Flamingo (FLM, NEO, bNEO, fUSDT, fWETH, fWBTC, pONT, GM, fCAKE, SWTH)

- CEX.io (EUR, GBP, USD, USDT)

- Binance (BTC)

- Huobi (BTC, ETH)

- OKX (USDT, BTC, ETH)

- Gate.io (USDT, BTC)

- MEXC (USDT)

- BigONE (USDT)

- BKEx (USDT)

N3 FLM is available on:

- Flamingo (NEO, GAS, bNEO, fUSDT, fWETH, fWBTC, pONT, GM, fCAKE, SWTH)

- OKX (USDT)

- MEXC (USDT)

- Gate.io (USDT, ETH)

Looking forward, the BitPanda exchange is adding support for N3 and paused Legacy deposits and withdrawals on March 21, 2022. After the migration is complete, users will only be able to deposit and withdraw NEP-17 NEO and GAS.

NNT also reached out to Neo Global Development to query the migration status of other large exchanges that are currently trading Legacy NEO. The EcoGrowth team indicated that they are in communication with exchanges such as Bittrex, Bitfinex, and Kucoin, and are providing support where needed. Ultimately however, the listing of N3 NEO is in the hands of each exchange and NGD are unable to provide timelines.
